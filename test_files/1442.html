Radiant Child Interview: Victoria Magrath

Filed in: Blog, Celebrate The Difference, Civil Regime, Fashion, Feature, INTERVIEWS, People, Radiant, Radiant Child, Spring, Trends | Tags: Celebrate The Difference, Civil Clothing, Civil Clothing Spring 2014 Collection, Civil Radiant Collection, Civil Regime, In The Frow, In The Frow for Civil Clothing, Radiant Child, Radiant Children, Victoria Magrath

UK Fashion and Beauty Blogger Victoria Magrath effortlessly rocks our Spring “Radiant” threads. Pink locks and all, the In The Frow blogger celebrates her difference with her cutting-edge fashion and groundbreaking, worldly style. Check out our interview with the UK beauty below and be sure to check out her awesome blog, In The Frow!

1.) Please tell us who you are and where you’re from.

My name is Victoria and I am a Fashion Lecturer and fashion blogger from Manchester, UK who writes daily for a fashion, beauty and personal style blog, Inthefrow.com.

2.) How do you celebrate the difference? (what is your passion and why do you do it?)

I have a huge passion for fashion blogging and fashion photography and spend hours of my day taking photographs and writing about them on my blog, Inthefrow. It gives me the freedom to be myself, build my confidence and share some amazing products with likeminded individuals. It gives me a place to write about what I love. The feedback I get only spurs me on to continue and I love every day.

3.) Why should others join the civil regime? (we support peoples passions and differences of all different types…but you can put it into your own words)

Civil Clothing allows individuals to express themselves the way they want to and to show their own passions and loves without boundaries. People should join the Civil regime if they want to make their own statement.
