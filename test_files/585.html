Hello October

September 28, 2015September 25, 2015 Socratic Salon

Grab your boots and pumpkin spice—we’re jumping into Fall with two books that have caused a big splash in the reading world. We hope you’ll join us in discussing them!

October 12: The Heart Goes Last by Margaret Atwood with AnnaSaurus Rex

October 26: Did You Ever Have a Family by Bill Clegg with Lauren from Malcolm Avenue Review

You can also expect fiery discussions about the merit of classic books and what really makes something scary. We recently updated our Goodreads group to include books we’re considering under the category “Currently Reading”, and have added a discussion space for you to let us know how you feel about those possibilities. As always, we would love to hear your suggestions, especially if you have ideas for Nonfiction November!
