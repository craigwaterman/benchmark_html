Barely There Sandals!

I have been patiently waiting for the sun to arrive or even slightly warmer weather, perhaps wishful thinking on my part! In any case I have decided not to wait any longer to step into some Spring outfits.

 Click Here To See More!


I know strappy heels are in but I have to say I find the barely there sandal so flattering and versatile. They can be worn with shorts and a T for a more casual Summer night out but can equally be paired with a dress for a Summer wedding. 

I searched high and low for the perfect pair and I found them in Schuh which is a shop I would not really frequent. I shared the link below.


White jeans are something I could not live without and you will see them featuring on the blog a lot, so I am apologising in advance.

 


  White Jeans // Blue Shirt // Heels


 
This pair from H&M are the perfect pair of ripped whites and an even better price tag. They are also high waisted which I am a an avid fan of.



Have a lovely week.
