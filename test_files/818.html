sweater weather

Spoonful of Fashion | Spoonful of Shopping | Uncategorized

Fashion, Shopping

J&N

The air is crisp and we are happy! Sweater weather is our favorite time of year and we are so excited to finally be able to slip into some cozy knits! Recently, we took a trip to SHE in Bloomfield Hills and had a fun afternoon trying on new fall fashions! The store is FULL of cozy knits, chunky sweaters, warm wraps and great fall/winter accessories!

This Yigal Azrouel sweater is AMAZING!!! The material is so soft {not itchy} and the colors are so neutral. Not to mention the fringe detail! It can be dressed up with joggers and a blouse {as shown} or it can be worn casually with denim and a basic tee. However, with a heavy price tag…this sweater may not be so practical. Thus, here are some less expensive  and similar looking options that may fit the bill…literally!

Happy Shopping!
