The Best Bathing Suits for Spring Break

Posted in Fashion

Spring Break is quickly approaching, which means it’s time to get out the trendy bikinis and get on the plane! Here’s a look at my top pics for your next getaway!

1) Color Blocking

Color blocking is a fun, makes you look tan and ensures your bikini will match whatever outfit you throw on over it! I love this style from Body Glove, because it’s similar to Triangl’s styles, but are more affordable and will stand out (because let’s be honest: everyone wears Triangl and it’s become basic).

Shop: Body Glove Borderline Suit

 

2) High Neck

Tops that are high-rise create a really beautiful silhouette. I love this style by Red Carter because it’s such a fun print. The keyhole detail keeps it sexy…just watch out for awkward tan lines!

Shop: Red Carter Shangiri La Bikini Top

 

3) One Pieces

It seems that this look got a major upgrade this season! One piece swimsuits got glam with creative cutouts, sexy sheer paneling and fun patterns! Look sophisticated on the beach rocking one of these!

Shop: Lili Fama Heart of a Hippie Swimsuit

 

4) Bustier Tops

This style is perfect for those of us who aren’t blessed with ample busts. A bustier style is comfortable, supportive and perfect for dancing the day away at the beach!

Shop: Urban Outfitters Out From Under Bikini Top

 

Have a fab Spring Break!
