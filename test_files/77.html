Appreciation Circles

Our class has been holding "Appreciation Circles" for all of our students. To start our day, we put a student in the middle of a circle, and then each classmate says an appreciation for the person in the middle. Students start out each statement with "I appreciate you because/for...." and add in things that they appreciate. So far, we have held them for our fifth grade students. We will continue with the fourth graders next week. In honor of our circles, I wanted to share with you things that I appreciate about each of our students in our classroom community:

Thursday, May 9, 2013

Terrific Thursday Thinking!

As usual, we did our morning routine, and then I-time! After I-Time, we went to art. In art we finished our symmetrical art and some of us got back our art from Youth Art Month. Then we wrapped up, and went into math. In math we did our regular stuff and continued to work with protractors. Then we had lunch and recess. After lunch and recess we read our books and then watched a BrainPOP video about invertebrates. Then we had Daily 5 and people got books about vertebrates to write their Notes and Thinking. We are working on paraphrasing and expanding our thoughts as we summarize what we read.  Then we wrapped up the day and went home!

By Killian.

Wednesday, May 8, 2013

Wheelin' Wednesday

WE came into school, got settled and did our business! AS usual, the ol' community circle, math problem of the day, and journal. Soon after that, we did i-time and then went to gym. In gym we got to ride our bikes, scooters, roller skates, etc. because today is Wheelin' Wednesday! But incase we didn't have a bike or something else, others got to walk and then play games! After gym we wrote our writing goals and got to read for a bit. In math we went over how to use protractors again and we actually got to use them to measure angles. Then, we had recess. After recess, we read a little bit and then read some of the book Hatchet.  Then, we did Daily 5 and some strategy groups.

Tuesday, May 7, 2013

MAPS Tuesday

Right after we got settled in the morning, we reviewed multiplying and dividing for our MAPS test. Then, we had I-Time and went to music. In music we drew pictures for our poems about animals.  Soon we went to Gym and did 2 laps. After 2 laps, we practiced for long jump on field day. When we came back to our class, we got ready for Math MAPS testing and went down to the computer lab. Then, we went to lunch and recess. About 20 minutes later, we went back in, and did MORE maps for those who didn't finish! Then we did Daily 5 and Mrs. Rivest took some videos of us doing research on animals. Then we packed up and went home!

Monday, May 6, 2013

Math Monday

THis morning, we did a lot of math because we have our math MAPS testing tomorrow. THen, we reviewed fractions. Soon after that, we had I-Time and reviewed MORE math. Finally we had snack and Words Their Way.

During Math Workshop we reviewed how to use protractors and practiced using them. After that we had recess.

After recess, we started a National Geographic article called "Beat The Heat." It was about animals trying to get cooled down on hot days. After that, we had SEL and we went over one of the strategies of refusing peer pressure, which was Better Idea. WE wrote down ideas to do in certain activities like watching TV and listening to songs. When we came back into the class, we played a part of MATH JEOPARDY. Then we packed up and went home.
