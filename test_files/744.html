Happy New Year! Plus, A 2015 Goals Printable

January 1, 2015 by Michelle Leave a Comment

Hope everyone had a fun and safe New Year’s Eve! I wish every one of you out there a happy, healthy, and prosperous New Year!

I don’t really believe in resolutions  because I pretty much never keep them. But, I did make a printable goal sheet if you are interested. It is done by the month, but also has a spot for yearly goals. I much prefer the monthly goals. Breaking my goals down into chunks this way feels more doable, and there is a good chance my goals might change along the way. Just because it was a resolution in January doesn’t mean that I even still want to achieve it in July.

Hope you have a great day, and once again, Happy New Year!
