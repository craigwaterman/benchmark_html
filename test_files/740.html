DIY: Fall wreath

This one was so much fun. (like I say about all of them).
So I didn't know what else to put on it and I was thinkin I needed a cute sign.
I scrambled some stuff and thought of something amazing.
For the lettering I just Googled 'cute fall fonts' and found a cute one and typed it in for a preview and drew on my little sign with a pencil, than a black marker.
Amazing right!!

Cute Fall sign:

  • Cardboard
  • acrylic paint (fall colors). I chose dark green
  • black marker
  •  lettering layout
Cut what size you want. I just measured the wreath and decided on how big I wanted it. 
Paint both sides; let dry.
Once dry put lettering on. (what I did above)



Now for the wreath.

What you'll need:
  • Wire wreath
  • wired burlap ribbon
  • fall flowers
  • fall decor (pumpkins, scar crow etc..)
  • hot glue gun
  • fall sign
What to do:
Wrap the burlap ribbon around the wreath like I did in the picture on the right. 
Glue the end and glue some parts together so they won't unravel like the one on the left.
Place flowers and decor the way you want it. Now hot glue them really good. 
Last the sign. 
Make sure you hot glue everything like crazy or it will all come off during storage.






Thats it.... seriously how cute is this wreath. 
This is a little different than my other ones. Wal-mart has been out of the branch wreaths for weeks and I wanted to make it so bad. I love the burlap.
Good luck and have fun.

See you in December!! for winter wreath.
