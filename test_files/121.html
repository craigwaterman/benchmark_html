Boca Health and Fitness Family Festival

Posted on March 5, 2015 by hredlich

ihelpmoms.com is celebrating healthy living by hosting the first Boca Health and Fitness Family Festival at Evolution Fitness Boca Raton on March 28 from 10 am- 2 pm in partnership with Operation Fit Moms and North Broward Preparatory School. The unique event will include healthy eating for families, teaching families how to make an active lifestyle fun and fighting childhood obesity.

The free event will benefit Junior League of Boca Raton’s Diaper Bank by encouraging families to donate a package of diapers in lieu of an admission cost. “This event is part of our mission to connect moms and families to high quality, family focused professionals that can help them to health, happiness and to make motherhood easier,” Michelle Brown, ihelpmoms.com founder, said.

The whole family can participate in activities including a fittest female and fittest male challenge, plank-off challenge, toddler activity station, kids fitness contest, family fitness competitions, bounce houses and more. Various health and fitness professionals including pediatric dentists, massage therapists, family chiropractors and mommy fitness trainers will also be at the event.

Pre-registration is required and can be completed at ihelpmoms.com/events. For more information on ihelpmoms, like their Facebook and follow them on Twitter. Ihelpmoms.com is an online community launched in April 2014 to support and empower women with kids who are looking for family-focused professionals and services to make motherhood easier. Founder Michelle Brown was having a difficult recovery with her first daughter and designed the community to help moms that are struggling, stressed or far away from their families. The nationwide website connects moms to valuable resources to make motherhood easier.
