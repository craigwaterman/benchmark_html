About Us

Building on your vision, exceeding every expectation

Since its founding in 1993, Prime 1 Builders, Inc., has established itself as a sought-after builder of custom homes, distinctive renovations and commercial workspace construction.Prime 1 takes a team-oriented approach. We surround every project with best-in-class professionals and work in concert with top interior designers and architects from around the country.

LEADERSHIP

With over 30 years’ experience in the construction industry, Daniel J. Meade, President, has built solid credentials with projects ranging in value to $7.5 million. Dan’s resume includes building upscale hotels, an ocean estate in the Chesapeake Bay area, town homes in the Mt. Washington section of Pittsburgh, converting a Southside warehouse into a beautiful modern residence, doctor and dental office build-outs and much more.

Under Dan’s guidance, Prime 1 Builders, Inc. has grown steadily–focusing on residential and commercial construction and development projects in Western Pennsylvania. His team-oriented approach and attention to detail ensures clients receive the highest level of service and workmanship.

OUR TEAM

Successful construction is a team sport. It takes experience in all disciplines and the ability to work together for a common goal. At Prime 1 Builders, Inc., our team of estimators, supervisors, office support, carpenters and construction workers each have at least 20 years of experience.

OUR PROCESS

The process starts with initial consultation with owner Dan Meade to determine your needs and project scope. From there, our professional estimator works to make your dream project at a realistic cost.

Once the proposal is accepted and signed, a Prime 1 coordinator schedules all trades, accessing the finest plumbers, HVAC, electricians and specialty contractors. Be assured that a supervisor from Prime 1 will always be present on your job site from start to completion.

RECOGNITION

South Side Courtyard House as featured on the cover of Pittsburgh Magazine, 2010. Grand Design Award/Home of the Year (Best New Custom Home Under $1 million) Contractor for the 2009 Honor Award, American Institute of Architects Pittsburgh.
