Best Day Ever!

Hi there scrappy friends!
Today I am sharing a layout I created for the 5th Christmas Caroling challenge over at My Scraps & More- use something shiny. I used a new to me scrappy company- Paper House and love their products! This is my layout using their Emerald collection line:

 
Here are some close ups:
 
The arrow is not an orange brown like it appears, it's actually gold. Love it!

 
The embellies here are all Paper House with the exception of the sequins and cut file. The sequins are from Walmart and both cut files- the circle and Dream Frame are both Nicole Nowasad, I believe.

 
More yummy green and gold embellies:
