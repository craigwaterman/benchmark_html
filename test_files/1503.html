Good Day, Sunshine!

-->
"Good Day Sunshine is a collection of essays, stories, and midnight ramblings inspired by life, The Beatles, and the beautiful sunshine." -Kiersten

Hello, loves!  Today I have the pleasure of introducing you to Kiersten from Good Day Sunshine!
Here are some snippets about this lovely lady, and don't forget to check out her blog to see more! 



 If you could describe your blog in one word, what would it be and why?


Random!

            It really is, especially because it’s a lifestyle blog.  One day I’ll be posting about my baking disasters and the next I’m writing a series of letters to the boyfriend/husband I haven’t met yet So hopefully you don’t mind inconsistency...






 Show/tell us the quote that defines your life


            I know this seems like a copout, but I don’t think any one song or quote will define anybody’s life.  People are way to broad for that!  But I love quotes, so here are a couple of my current favorites:



            “And though she be but little, she is fierce.” - Shakespeare

            “I love her, and that’s the beginning and end of everything” - F. Scott Fitgerald

            “What a treacherous thing to think that a person is more than a person” - John Green

            “No matter what anybody tells you, words and ideas can change the world” - Dead Poet’s Society 


   What can people expect when they click over to your blog?


            Everything! (well, except for fashion....you’re not going to find any of that)  Good Day Sunshine is incredibly random, so you can find just about anything over there.  Mostly though, you’ll find my day-to-day life, my thoughts that I like to tell myself are philosophical and need to be shared, and my photographs.
