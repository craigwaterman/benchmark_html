A Sneek Peek @ the Vision of Kidcentric Portland

Posted on February 11, 2014 by Kidcentric Portland
Image

Kidcentric has been not only a love song to my children Christopher Bryan and Ava Brooklyn but an homage to the life my husband, Neil and I have created together.  As a family we work mindfully at creating a loving, compassionate, creative space to learn grow and live.  We are passionate about natural allergy-free living, independent thinking and a bustling kitchen where we teach others to create food that inspires, heals, and brings people together.

Bird Sanctuary
Homeschool Adventure

Our home has always been haven to an eclectic mix of supremely creative, talented individuals from whom we’ve learned, grown and nurtured.  Kidcentric was originally founded in Orlando, Florida where we honed themed, child-led playdates, classes and events fun for the whole family.  Devised with the collaboration and guidance of play therapists, parenting mentors, other artists, chefs and experienced homeschool parents Kidcentric became known for offering the finest in child-led resources and a compassionate, emergent environment where children could thrive under the reciprocal respect due every living being .  By bringing Kidcentric events and festivals to Portland we have a unique opportunity to share our blessings with other like-minded families through:

Homeschool Playdates
 Stay & Play style with parents, no drop off, for ages 2-9 but babies in arms and nurslings are always welcome.  For more info please see FAQ
Bi-Monthly Homeschoool Adventure Field Trip Collective
Reggio Style Art & Culinary Classes
   With early childhood educator, artist and professional Chef Renee Rose.
Musical Instruction
     With local and visiting musicians through the art of storytelling.
Magical Book Fair Events for Homeschool Families
  During “traditional” school hours with read-a-thon, story tellers, performances and interactive storybook characters!

What Happens If?
Mud Playgroup
Ava Brooklyn & Cruz Margiotta-Wheeler

Parenting & New Baby Workshops for Gentle and Attachment Lifestyles
 With some of the countries finest parenting coaches, play therapists, child physicians, doulas and foremost avant-garde, progressive homeschool education professionals.
Natural Healing Workshops
     To assure you have the best natural resources and information to help heal your children and family naturally.  
Homeschool Curriculum and Methodologies Workshops

Family Friendly, Allergy-Free Music – Art – MultiCultural Festivals
     Camping, glamping, music and folk festivals featuring allergy-free cuisine, child-led activities for all ages and extraordinary entertainment for the whole family.

Kidcentric Fall Festival
Mark DeMaio of 3rd Wheel Dot Org

As we continue gather knowledge, strength and inspiration through dear friends near and far we aspire to share their talents with the Portland community via regular traveling artist events.  Through art, film, cuisine, music and natural healing practices our long-term goal is to create a holistic family retreat and wellness center where we can explore, create, learn and heal in a magical space.  In Kidcentric’s Portland infancy we will offer events in both a family style home environment and a variety of locations throughout Portland.  As we continue to grow, a physical location will be collectively created with some of our dearest friends and colleagues.

For more information on taking part in our Kidcentric weekly playgroup or homeschool adventure field trip group and continue to support our mission please visit our FAQ page.

Blessings,
Renee Rose

All Kidcentric events will begin in April 2014 in NE Portland.  Please feel free to engage with us via social media through Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest.
