Julie Barkan

Julie Barkan has always been involved in creative endeavors.

She spent more than twenty-five years in the creative departments of leading advertising agencies in Chicago, New York and Los Angeles.

Julie Barkan first started designing her handcrafted jewelry in New York where she sold to friends, and displayed pieces in juried crafts shows and a Soho store featuring unique handcrafted fashions.

Life as an advertising Creative Director kept her too busy to continue designing jewelry for several years. But the design bug could not be kept dormant forever.

Eight years ago she started designing again, first, for herself, then for friends but when store buyers started commenting on pieces she was wearing and asked to see more of her work, she decided it was time to go public again.  She now designs handcrafted jewelry full time in Los Angeles, selling at private shows, boutiques and online.

Julie Barkan's namesake, Julie Barkan Jewelry is an exciting new collection of handcrafted jewelry. Each piece embodies our belief in simplicity, individuality and enduring Los Angeles style. We’re all about creating jewelry for real people. Pieces that fit everyday life. To do that they make their jewelry affordable while always keeping a commitment to quality. Julie Barkan jewelry is either 24K gold overlay, gold filled, or 14k gold over sterling silver. The gemstones are hand picked, many of which are used to make one-of-a-kind pieces that become favorite go-to accessories. "We believe in color and sparkle and fun" -Julie Barkan.
