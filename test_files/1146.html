Naylor’s Notes: Grace Justified

The energy they have on stage is so hot I’m surprised they don’t set the stage on fire. The music they create is inspiration at it’s best.  Grace Justified’s passion is to deliver the music that sets the listener free from the fire.  To follow Jesus, and always put God first.  Give God the glory in all that you do, and watch your world immediately start changing for the best.  God is always listening, and it’s up to you to ask for his help.  Check out this brief bio from Grace Justified, and scroll through their featured pictures, sound tracks, and video’s.  All The Best, & God Bless. – Johnny

Grace in Christianity is the “free” and unmerited favor of God as manifested in the salvation of sinners and the bestowing of blessings. In 2013, Laura Thomas along with Calvin Johnson founded GRACE, an organization comprised of noted musicians and singers who serve within their respective churches, community, and together serve the church at large to uplift the building of the kingdom.

With a desire to minister and draw people from all races, genders, and cultures together through praise and worship, Grace began blending sounds and songs to create melody that would break down barriers and defies categorization.

For the bible tells us; “This was the whole purpose of Jesus coming to earth. God purchased our salvation with his own blood”, Acts 20:28. The price paid at Calvary is the ONLY price able to purchase our salvation. There are not enough good deeds available for any one of us to do, and to ever deserve God’s grace. Christ did the work to make salvation available to us. Working together with the vocalists and musicians of Grace, Laura used her own religious background as a reference point to create their music.

Grace has been working in the ministry over 5 years.  On May 31st, 2014, “Free” the title of their debut Single will be officially launched in Conway, Arkansas. This Single contains one track, plus a bonus track, that is steadily rising to become one of Arkansas favorite singles to date. These songs were recorded to change lives around the world.  On November 11th, Grace Justified will be releasing our second EP entitled, “Over Coming Adversity.”  If you’re looking for a soul steering or upbeat contemporary/traditional song you can find it on this EP.

Grace consists of 12 members & has the heart of a worshipper. Anointed, unique and called for such a time as this. Grace Justified believes in hand clapping, foot stomping old school church, where we run, dance, shout, and allow the Lord to use us while creating an unusual service for God to show up and rein in the mist of his people.




Recently nominated for 6 music awards, and being selected to be in the top tunes for the Rhythm of Gospel Awards upcoming compilation cd. Grace Justified just recently performed at the “Family, Faith, Freedom Tour with Senator Jason Rapert, and will be traveling to St Louis to perform with some of the national artist such as “Donnie McClurkin, Dorinda Clark-Cole, and many other at the National Church of God in Christ Holy Convocation on November 5th.

 

Grace Justified is currently holding #1,#2, & #4 on the Reverb National Gospel Charts. Grace Justifies has represented Arkansas well.

About Johnny Naylor:
Johnny Naylor is the founder and owner of 1st Shot Music and a feature writer for Jammerzine. His work can also be found on 1st Shot Music and Naylor’s Notes. You can also get his latest updates on his facebook page.
