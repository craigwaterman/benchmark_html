About Me

First of all…Why ashley on grace?


Well, most importantly because of Jesus. Grace is defined (in the Christian sense) as “the free and unmerited favor of God, as manifested in the salvation of sinners and the bestowal of blessings.” Yep. That sounds about right. I believe I have been given total unmerited favor. And, I try to remember that when I’m in difficult situations with others, or even myself. Simply put, I try to give the grace that was given to me.

It just so happens that the other meaning of grace, “simple elegance or refinement of movement” really speaks to me. And by speaks to me, I mean not at all. Grace is most definitely not my middle name, but it is
what I aspire to be.

My life is not particularly special. Just like everyone else­­I’m figuring it out as I go. I do my best and try to put a little more “good” back into this world. My interests evolve daily but a few of the standouts in my life have been a love for food, people, animals, college basketball, being outside and good country music. Hopefully, you find a little bit of all that and more on this site.

The boring stuff

I am a (late) twenty­something graduate of Indiana University (Hoo­Hoo­Hoo­Hoosiers!). After stumbling my way down a few different paths in public relations, event planning and dental assisting. ­­I’ve found myself in the (agriculture) advertising world. It isn’t all Mad Men and drinking martinis but I do love it and we have a damn good time. I have a small, but tight­knit family, a few above average girlfriends and one stellar boyfriend. I reside in the Indianapolis area.
