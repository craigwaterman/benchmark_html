Quote:
Originally Posted by boostedisbetter
If I was GUARANTEED over a 10% return, it might be worth it to me, but for now I don't know something that will do that... Advice?
It all depends on what's important to you, and what you'll be giving up to pay off your loans early.

If my rates were really low (like 1-2%) or paying down my loans would've prevented me from contributing to my 401k/IRA, I might not have made the same decision.

But my loans were at like 6.5% and I was still able to contribute to both my 401k/IRA, so the only trade-off was the opportunity value of that cash flow.

Would I beat 6.5% in the market? Maybe. Maybe not.



Since that was a toss up, I decided to go for the mental satisfaction of being debt-free.
