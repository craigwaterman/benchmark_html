Matching Mondays: Citrus & Seersucker

Welcome to Cozy Home's inaugural Matching Mondays post!!

Miss Cozy and I put together a pair of cute outfits for the girls to wear to meet Mr. Cozy for lunch during his work day; amazingly our big sister/little sister coordinating outfits come from two different shops!


Perfect for sunny summertime, the baby's outfit (size 0-3) is from Gymboree, the "Lemon Bumblebee" collection in the newborn section, and it's on sale right now! 

Lemon and orange-dotted onesie and green seersucker bloomers -- and to stretch the outfit further, I had a onesie made with Baby Cozy's monogram in lime green to wear with the bloomer as well, from this shop, Little Graces!

For Miss Cozy, an "apron dress" from my all-time favorite children's shop, Janie and Jack.
Though we bought this dress on sale last summer, you can track down parts of the Taste of Capri collection, including this dress, on Ebay -- I found some here.
