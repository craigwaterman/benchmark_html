Franche Vita Mineral Makeup




I am always on the look out for new mineral makeup products. So when I came across Franche's website, I just had to share it with my all of you. I was intrigued to know more about it. Franche is formulated with the finest ingredients and latest technology. It treats and protects the skin without any trace of alcohols, dyes, talcs, oils, fragrances or preservatives that can be harmful or cause irritation to the skin.


The formulations are a unique combination of minerals, including Micro Titanium Dioxide, Zinc, Microsome Vitamins A & E, Bismuth and Iron Oxide pigments. The minerals in the extensive color palette reflect the light allowing a greater color spectrum than most cosmetics and leave your skin with the most velvety, sumptuous look and feel possible. The pressed foundations, eye shadows, blushes and shimmer powders contain a natural SPF 20 and are very water-resistant.

Franche is one of the companies that has joined in the effort that requires the health and beauty industry to phase out the use of dangerous chemicals and replace them with safer alternatives, by signing the Campaign For Safe Cosmetics.
