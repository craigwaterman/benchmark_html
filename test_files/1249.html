Latest News: Upcoming Shows and Activities

 

 

"Essaouira Archway" (22" x 15") was awarded "First Honorable Mention" in the Alaska Watercolor Society's 41st Annual Juried Exhibition, opening October 2 at the Aurora Fine Art Gallery, Anchorage.

 

 

"Fisherman in Essaouira" was juried into the 47th Watercolor West International Juried Exhibition, October 17 through December 13, 

La Brea, CA

 

 

"Campbell Creek Winter Sunset" (15" x 22")  has been selected for inclusion to the upcoming Art for Alaska Parks Exhibition, Stephan Fine Art Gallery, September 4 - 18, 2015

 

 

"Taking A Break" (22" x 15") will be on display at the Rocky Mountain National Watermedia Exhibition from September 19 - October 25, 2015 at the Foothills Art Center in Golden, CO.

 

 

I have been granted a "Percent For Art" commission from the Alaska State Council on the Arts for an installation at the new State of Alaska Geologic Materials Center in Anchorage.
