About

                    FOLLOW MY JOURNEY

The Vixxen Style is my fashion blog. I am a young and creative stylist from Miami, Florida.

At first glance, this is a regular blog, but once you dive in you’ll notice it’s actually a travel journal; a trip around the realm of fashion and style, that is, The Vixxen Style.

A little about me…

What’s is the VIXXEN style?

Vixxen means ‘female fox’.

The Vixxen style is essentially pop. It’s easy, colorful and never boring. Vixxen style is a personal style which makes you feel comfortable and beautiful.

I believe that dresses are made to be creative, they are a way to state your own personal style and of course to have fun with.

Who is Jessica Maldonado? 

I am a young and creative stylist. I was born and raised in Miami, Florida. I am 20 years old and I am currently attending college for business.

My life and love are surrounded around fashion. Besides this blog and my social accounts, I also take part in style shootings, fashion shows and, of course, I am a stylist.

Ever since I was a little girl, I had a clear idea about what I wanted to do in my future. All I wanted to do was spread my own idea of style and involve people in my evolution in personal style.

What’s your idea of style?

“Stop waiting for approval. Do what you want and live your life”, this is what I feel.

Style is all about being free and making it personal. It’s about reflecting your mood and what you like. Following this line of course, you’ll never be boring (or bored).

 What sort of blog?

I see style as an actual journey through the many possibilities of dressing.

This blog, my Twitter, and Instagram pages create a sort of fashion-travel journal, where I want to share my impressions, thoughts and feelings on style and fashion.

 Why a journey?

Style is constantly developing. Whether it’s slow or quick, everyone at one point in their life changes and develops her or his personal style. I love seeing my fashion experience as an actual journey, in which you get to know new things, and appreciate new tastes and looks. Traveling, both in a material and immaterial sense, means opening your horizons and accepting new influences that can, more or less, affect your way of being and your way of dressing.

I opened this blog to share with everyone my style adventure. Of course, a journey is more exciting when you can share it!

See you around!

Jessica M.
