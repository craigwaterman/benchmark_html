Background

Summary

With 30+ years in the Health, Wellness and Beauty Industry as a Healthy Hair and Body Consultant, Master Hair Stylist, Hair Thinning and Loss Specialist, Salon and Spa Owner, Educator, Platform Artist, Business Owner and Business Consultant.

I have developed team of highly educated professionals that strive to provide a friendly environment for our clients to receive health,wellness and beauty services. Our goal as Service Providers is to listen to our clients and make suggestions that provide them with the highest quality service possible.

Our Unique Specialties are Solutions for Hair Thinning and Loss, Dimensional and Corrective Hair Coloring, Precision Hair Cutting, Anti Aging Skin Care, Healthy Nail Care, Healthy Body Care and Nutritional Healing.
