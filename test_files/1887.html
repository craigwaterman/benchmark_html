Shabby Chic Country Sisters

Welcome to the home of Shabby Chic Country Sisters! Shabby Chic Country Sisters was formed by two very close sisters with a passion for furniture inherited from their Grandmother, this has led them to start a business restoring and improving furniture. Being from the country themselves there is a natural love for country furniture along with inspiration from the 18th century from Britain and France.
