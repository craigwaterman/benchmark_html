Evaluations

EVALUATION
Question one
What have I learnt while completing this project?
During this project I have learned a lot. I had to use Photoshop for the majority of my work which meant I had to learn how to use it. I had to get used to working with layers and the tool box. It was very frustrating at first. At first it was very difficult to get my head around the layers and the tool box. However after using Photoshop a few times I learnt how to use the layer and tool box correctly and was able to create my magazine cover. Throughout the task I have learnt different types of information that will help me in my media course, such as what semiotics is; also what connotation and denotation mean and how to apply it to different things such as adverts like I did with the Wayne Rooney advert. While taking photos of my models for my front cover I also learnt the different type of camera angles and what they are used for.
Also when we was given a task we had to work individually to complete the work, so I learnt how to work individually and get my work done by a dead line. Which has taught me how to manage my time to complete all my work.
Another thing that I have learnt while completing this task is how to use online blogger, as I have never used a website like this before and I've never had to use a website before to publish my work on the be graded, when I have done coursework or any work I have been used to printing it off and handed it in to the teacher.

Question three
what conventions did I use?
On my magazine front cover I used many magazine conventions. I used a mast head, main images, main feature story, other feature stories, a plug, a skyline, barcode, price, issue number and date. This is because these are the main conventions of a magazine cover, my mast head ‘Wyke Weekly’ is in a green colour and is a larger font to the rest of the writing; therefore my mast head is easy to spot. My feature stories and main feature stories differ from purple to green. This is because my colour schemes were purple and green because Wyke’s colour is purple and on the colour wheel from my research green complements purple very well. Also my header is in a purple colour too. My plug is also in the colour scheme of green and purple. I have the essential information such as the date, barcode, issue number and price on the front cover as they give vital information. I have shown my final product to several people and they have all said and commented on how the colours really  stand out and it is easier to distinguish between the main feature story and the other feature stories. This is great, as this is what I wanted. Also they commented on how the main image suits the cover very well as it is a college student holding college essentials in her hands. She is stood next to college lockers, which helps set the theme for the college very well. Overall I am very happy with the convention use on my magazine as I feel I have used most, if not all the main conventions of a magazine cover. The writing stands out and it is easy to tell which story is the main one and which ones are the other feature stories. I feel I have placed all the information very well on the front of the magazine.

Question four
What would I change if I were to do this task again?
If I was to do this assignment again I would do several things very differently. They main orientate around time keeping. I would use my study periods more efficiently and spend that time on my media work, instead of doing nothing or other work that doesn’t have a deadline. Even though I have stuck to my deadline and I have got my work finished to the best of ability I feel as though I have been rushed these past few days. In my opinion I feel as though I have spent too much time on my conventions of a magazine writing too much, and that has left me not enough time to actually complete the rest of my work, i.e. my magazine cover. When I was in class I didn’t quite finish all the work set in that lesson because I spent too not as much into my other parts, this may be clear in the much time on certain things and didn’t think ahead that I wouldn’t have enough time to finish my actual magazine cover. I feel as though I put too much effort into some parts of my assignment and Presentation in some of my work on moodle. Therefore if I was to do this again I would spread my effort out equally to each task. Another thing that I would change if I was to do this task again would be to ask more questions. I feel that because I didn’t know the teacher we had very well that I didn’t ask as many questions as I should have done, however I know feel more comfortable which means I would ask more questions in the future and if I was to do this task again because I now feel more comfortable with the teacher that I have.
