STUDIO LIFE

Double Helix Games is a game studio with a history that stretches back almost 20 years. The studio formed officially when Shiny Entertainment, creator of classic games like Earthworm Jim, Sacrifice, MDK and Enter the Matrix, merged with The Collective, Inc., makers of Indiana Jones and the Emperor's Tomb, Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith, and Buffy the Vampire Slayer. Most recently Double Helix Games launched latest generation games Killer Instinct and Strider. In 2014 Double Helix Games announced that it had been acquired by Amazon.

Fueling the creativity, design, and technical excellence that drives all our games, Double Helix Games is home to over 60 developers in sunny Orange County, California. We are underway on fantastic new games, with an awesome work environment, frequent studio events, terrific benefits, and a spirit of creativity in everything we do.
