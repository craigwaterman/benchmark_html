How to Build an Online Brand for Your Small Business

Trying to build your online brand for your small business can be very overwhelming. There are endless small business marketing options to choose from. Most business owners feel like they’re blind folding themselves and throwing darts hoping whatever their dart lands on will see the best results.

As a small business owner myself, there is no easy answer, and it varies from business to business. Don’t get caught up in what the competition is doing. Yes, it is always important to know, but, what is working for them might not work for you.

Below are a few tips your small business needs to consider when building your online brand.

  1. If you’re still not there, take advantage of social media.

Facebook and Twitter are still very popular with online users. Step outside your comfort zone and look at other popular platforms like Instagram and Pinterest. These are excellent sites to showcase your work with images and videos. Take the time to find out which platform is bringing the most quality traffic to your website.

  1. We know you’re not a writer, but set up a business blog.

Online users are smarter today and blogging can acquire new customers and influence their buying decision. Blogging is an excellent way to showcase your business, your industry knowledge and boost your rankings and traffic to your website. In addition, creating new content on a consistent basis will generate new keyword phrases for online users to find you.

  1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Are you still focused on what keywords to focus on? Keywords searches only drives so much traffic to your site. Properly optimized content generates the most organic traffic to your website. A well maintained and optimized website can produce four times more traffic than social media.

  1. Stand out and be different.

A lot of business owners talk about building their online brand but many fail to follow through. Make sure your business looks consistent across all social media platforms and your website by keeping the same look and feel of your business. Provide quality content that is valuable and useful to your followers. Use different platforms like social media and email to engage and communicate with your audience.
