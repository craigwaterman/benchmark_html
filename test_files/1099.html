Whats In My Travel Makeup Bag?

by Kristen - August 26, 2015

Hey loves,

Today I decided to post some of the things I carry in my ‘travel bag’. There were a few products that were missing that I usually have with me (like my proactive products), but for the sake of this trip I did not bring them with me. I dislike traveling heavy so I wanted to make it as light as possible. I did forget to mention my bronzer & I did not remember it until I had already filmed the video. I will name the few products I have in my bag below.

  1. Wipes
  2. Razors
  3. Lip Moisturizer
  4. Hair Moisturizer
  5. Makeup Brushes
  6. Contour & Highlight kit
  7. Foundation
  8. Pressed Powders
  9. Concealer
  10. Eyeliners
  11. Mascara
  12. Eyelash Curler
  13. Eye-shadow/Blush/Lip stain Palettes
  14. Lipsticks
  15. Mineral Face Primer
