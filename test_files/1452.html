Fashion Friday: Midi Skirt Trend

Hey everyone!
As a true fashionista you should know that one of the hottest trend this season is the Midi Skirt.
Many well known Fashion Bloggers have been seen wearing gorgeous midi skirts paired with crop tops, blouses etc. and so because I would love to own one I can't wait to go shopping for the perfect piece.
Look in high shine finishes, pick a pvc pencil skirt in pastel hues and shake it up in a full circle shape.
Now because I want to make life easier to those who are interested in the midi skirt I have selected a few items from different highstreet stores here in Ireland.
 Hope you like them!!
