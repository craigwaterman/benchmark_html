Preparing for a night out fashion wishlist 6 items I’d love to go out in

Posted on April 3, 2015 | Leave a comment

For my first fashion wishlist post I’ve gone completely risky with bold out-there prints, a range of colours and a varied price scale. The whole point of a wishlist is that although I may not be able to afford everything on it, I would seriously consider wearing them for a night out, given the opportunity.

This, I guess, is yet more evidence that I have eclectic and stand-out tastes at heart although I don’t usually dress to suit those tastes.

ASOS Vintage shell top with geo print £38

This pink geo print top from ASOS caught my eye straightaway as it would look great dressed up or down and although it flashes a little flesh (probably too much for my curves) it’s girly enough to look cute.

MM6 Cosmic Storm shirt dress £350 Urban Outfitters

This shirt dress reminded me of some kind of fantasy land, sort of like the scenes from the film ‘The last unicorn’. Although it is way out of my budget I can definitely still dream about wearing it.

This yellow floral print bandeau dress is £50 from River Island

This yellow floral print dress from River Island could be dressed up or down to wear on a nice summer beach-front stroll to cocktails at the bar afterwards.

Urban Renewal Vintage Remnants Printed Cotton Babydoll dress in pink £45 Urban Outfitters

I love the design on this dress as well as the colours, as you’ve probably already realised, I really am a fan of bright colours.

Pippa Lynn Oriental cross-back jumpsuit £68 Urban Outfitters

This dress looks so fashion forward I would have expected a much bigger price tag. Although still out of my budget I think it looks so glamorous and is something I would consider saving up for.

Textile federation orchids jumpsuit £70 Urban Outfitters

I love the bright colours and the mishmash design which is also available on other items at Urban Outfitters.

Well those are my 6 chosen items, I know they’re probably very different to what you would have picked so get in touch and let me know what you think of my choices and what you have on your current night out fashion wishlist. You can comment below or Tweet and Instagram @spursbythebeach.
