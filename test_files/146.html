Other Houses

Some of these houses we've looked at and some we haven't.  But aren't these beautiful spaces?  

We haven't decided what to do house-wise yet.  We LOVE our house but it doesn't have some things that we want for our family (playroom, place in the kitchen for MA to do her homework one day - while I pretend to cook, usable breakfast area, office for Kevin, extra garage, open den and kitchen floor plan).  

It's becoming a good time of year to be out and about looking.  And you know checking out real estate is one of our favorite things to do anyway!
