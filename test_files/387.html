  • Strength, Speed, Endurance


    Whether you seek to reclaim your athletic birth right or you identify yourself as a pro-athlete; strength, speed and endurance are non-negotiable virtues to help create your edge. Studies affirm HIIT offers superior benefits over traditional methods working out.

    High Intensity Interval Training involves alternating between very intense bouts of exercise and low intensity exercise. An example would be I sprint for 40 seconds and then jog for 60 seconds repetitively instead of just jogging at a steady pace. HIIT can be used both anaerobically (in the gym with weights or without) and aerobically with cardio. maXtreme, the app, embodies HIIT principles!

  • Strength in Flexibility


    Use the focused option to access 15 minutes targeted workouts (by muscle group); or choose your goal (get lean-strong-defined) and level (Yellow belt beginner - blue belt intermediate – red belt advanced – super advanced Jedii) and select from a basket of 88 workouts (20mins, 30mins or 45mins); or choose the weight-free traveling option and select from a basket of 24 workouts (20mins, 30mins or 45mins).

    For every drill, maXtreme coaches you with detailed instructions (pictures and videos) with an easy intuitive interface to make your experience enjoyable and sweaty.

    MaXtreme playlist allows you the flexibility to select your preferred drills and create your own named workout, just like a music playlist on your iTunes. An intelligent algorithm injects warm-up and rest considerations based on your selected options to create the best custom workout.
