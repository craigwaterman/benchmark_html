Fashion Jewelry Trends – Eternally Changing

How much ever we might want to run away from the word change in your life (be it a change of job, boyfriend, house…), there are some changes that bring beck life in our living. A change in fashion is one of them. I know exactly how it feels when in the middle of your routine life and daily chores you get a call from a friend who tells you about this latest trend in fashion and invites you to go shopping with her to this only store in town that has the stuff. Wow! Suddenly there’s this tickling thing that runs across my body and I so look forward to the trip.

Ok don’t get me wrong. This really is not about philosophy and a woman’s life, it’s actually about the ever changing trends in fashion jewelry and how welcome these are in our lives. So if you are my kinds and you get elevated to be the first one to grab the hot look of the season, here’s a sneak peak in to hot trends for summer/spring 2009 (by the way my fashion prediction never goes wrong).

Big, bold and beautiful. Statement jewelry would be the hot look of spring / summer 2009 (as suggested by the runways). Wild to whacky, choose your style. And the best part – these can be carried on to the fall season as well.

More will score. If you are the kind who either wears a watch or a bracelet – get on both. And wackier the combination cooler the look.

Play with colors. Choose colored gemstone to create combinations that amaze even you.
