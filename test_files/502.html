Amazon Prime Dropping Price To $73 This Weekend :::: #LifeStyle

If you've wanted to join Amazon's PRIME service, but didn't wanna pay the annual $99... this weekend is your chance to get it at a discount.
From 9 p.m. PST on Friday (Jan. 15) to Sunday (Jan. 17) at 11:59 p.m.,Amazon Prime will be on sale for $73.
According to Amazon, the Prime price slash was to celebrate victories for Best Series, Musical or Comedy and Actor in a Series, Musical or Comedy at the73rd Golden Globe Awards last Sunday.

During the promotional period, non-Prime subscribers will be able to stream all episodes of the newly Globe-winning show Mozart in the Jungle.
Amazon is currently battling its streaming competitors Netflix, Hulu and HBO. In addition to streaming video, Prime also offers free two-day shipping when shopping on Amazon.
