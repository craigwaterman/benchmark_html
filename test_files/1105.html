Game Day Munchies!!!

So you have a bunch of people that showed up on a Sunday afternoon to watch the game. No need to panic! Here is a quick munchie dish to get you through your dilemma (had to check to see if that was spelled correctly). This is real quick and easy so without any further gilderlilly...

Sweet and Spicy Fried Shimp

1 lb Raw Shrimp peeled and deveined (31-40 is what I use)
1 1/4 C All Purpose Flour
2 T Garlic Powder
2 T Onion powder
1/2 t Cayenne Powder

Oil for frying

1/4 lb butter (1 stick)
1/4 C Frank's Hot Sauce
1/4 C Sriracha Sauce
2 T Honey

1-2 T Cornstarch
Water as needed

In a 1 Gallon freezer bag combine flour garlic onion and cayenne shake until combined. Add shrimp and shake until well coated. refrigerate for 2 hours.

In a medium sauce pan melt butter and add hot sauce, sriracha and honey. Bring to a simmer.
Add corn starch to thicken. If too thick gradually add water to thin out ( you want a semi-thick consistency)

Heat oil to 350-375 degrees

fry shrimp for 2-3 minutes drain oil and toss in sauce

This is a creeper meal. This dish starts out with a sweet taste and gradually the heat will build up.
