Services

Luxury Interior Design

Knotting Hill Interiors is an upscale interior design firm in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina specializing in creating luxury high-end custom residential interior designs.

Since 1995, Knotting Hill Interiors has offered luxury interior design services coupled with exceptional personalized service and attention to detail. As specialists in high-end residential interior design, Knotting Hill Interiors approaches each client and project individually, with the goal expert execution and very satisfied clients. The results are exceptional, value enhancing, luxury interiors.

From preliminary conceptual planning to the final touches, each Knotting Hill Interiors project is custom-tailored to the client’s need for unique and innovative design, exceptional quality and luxurious results. Knotting Hill Interiors has established an excellent reputation with a special emphasis on luxury properties. The firm’s broad range of expertise includes luxury home interior design, historical renovation design and condo interior design.

Elegantly Green

HGTV.COM SPOTLIGHTS “GREEN HOME” DESIGN BY LOCAL INTERIOR DESIGNER KIMBERLY GRIGG:

Knotting Hill Interiors knows how to create green interior design with every luxurious effect one could ask for. Kimberly Grigg’s Elegantly Green approach makes for a earth friendly and luxurious space. What are some of the green attributes your home can have? Here are a few of our techniques we used on a recent project featured on HGTV:

  • Garage is pre-wired for electric cars
  • Tankless water heaters
  • Whole house water filtration system
  • Propane stove & fireplaces
  • Super, high effeciency HVAC system with Hepa filters
  • Energy star appliances & windows Picture Kitchen appliances
  • Solar panels & solar tubes
  • 50 yr shingles
  • Spray foam attic insulation
  • Rain barrels
  • Bricks, metal trim, gutters, and driveways were re-used along with just about everything else including furniture, fixtures, lights, cabinets etc.

There is a wide variety of materials and techniques that Knotting Hill Interiors uses to make your luxury interior design project a home that sets the example for others, with an elegantly green interior design approach. Let us show you how.

Condo Packages

Just because you’ve downsized to a low-maintenance flat, doesn’t mean
that it should be flat and boring. Use timeless interior decorating
techniques to insure your high rise home exudes elegance. Whether
your condo is designed to house you temporarily, or in fact, you love
a maintenance-free lifestyle and plan to stay for the long haul, beautiful and functional design décor and an elegant lifestyle can indeed be yours!

Some designers find the open floor plan of many new condos a
challenge, wondering where to begin and end the paint, and how to
partition the living spaces. A muted elegant scheme, vivid artwork, bold floral arrangements, and lively printed textiles can serve to portray your current fashion passions. Knotting Hill Interiors has the expertise to
make your condo a warm and inviting nest for as little or as long as
you choose to roost!

Creating a kitchen with style, function, and efficiency is a frequent
request of South Carolina condo owners. Many condos have limited
counter space, but older buildings that have been converted to condos
often have enormous floor space! Consider a stand-alone island! A
four or five-foot long island will store a large assortment of bowls,
utensils, and groceries and will give you another expansive of prep
area.

Children’s Rooms & Nurseries

Children’s room design and nursery design requires easy adaptation. As the child grows, the room should grow as well. Knotting Hill Interiors focuses on the structure and foundation of the space. From this inspiration the interior design can be built upon the growing needs of the child. 

Our all-inclusive approach to designing the perfect room for your little one takes into consideration everything you will need for the ultimate result.

Our design process includes: 

  • Nursery / Floor planning
  • Rendering
  • Weekly Project report
  • Purchasing
  • Installation
  • Safety Assessment
  • Color Scheme
  • Lighting
  • Storage
  • Furniture
  • Bedding
  • Fabrics
  • Art
  • Accessories

Our process of designing children’s rooms incorporates the desires of the parents, the current and future needs of the child and our years of experience in interior design. We make it fun and stress-free to create a functional and loving environment for your child. Our firm is especially sought out for our fine nursery design and children’s room design .   The final results are spectacular and will bring you piece of mind, knowing that your child is in a safe, creative and loving environment.
