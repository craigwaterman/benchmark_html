‘Athleisure’ street fashion

Published on February 12, 2016February 16, 2016 by Katykoo

So many styles and trends have come in and out since the teen years of the 2000s. It’s become much easier to be on trend without breaking the bank. It seems like only yesterday that it was only acceptable to where a pair of stilettos and a bodycon dress for a night out, but oh how times have changed. For the past few years, athletic and leisure wear has been making its way into clubs and bars and sporty chic is officially in full swing. Boutiques have been rolling out the  ‘Athleisure’ wear, and it’s easy for us to pick up the sporty outfits our wardrobes are so desperately craving.

Im loving an edgy look with a matching tracksuit at the moment. I like my clothing to fit my body well and to make a statement. What’s so great about the athletic wear craze is that you can dress up or dress down an outfit with something as simple as changing your shoes or jacket.You can mix and match how you wear clothing like this. Keep it simple by matching, or mix it up with other items, either way you’re embracing athletic chic! Whether I’m heading to the gym or to the shops, I’m street styled and loving this trend.

There are some great online boutiques stocking key pieces that are must haves in your wardrobes and you can mix and match them with anything to get sporty chic in an instant.There are some great items online, and Betabrand has a great collection of women’s pants! This link will take you directly to their pants section that’s full of great options! It’s an awesome place to kick start your ‘Athleisure’ collection at really great prices! Thanks for reading beauties! I hope you’re all sporty chic inspired!
