About Kate

Journalist, media personality, radio talent and columnist for The Sun-Herald.

A daughter of Australia’s racing royalty; Kate Waterhouse has grown up in the media spotlight. She is the daughter of leading thoroughbred trainer Gai Waterhouse and grand-daughter of racing legend Tommy “TJ” Smith. Away from the racetrack, Kate is making every post a winner. With a Bachelor of Arts in Media and Communication, Kate’s achievements in journalism include being the Fashion Editor for The Sun-Herald from 2009 to 2013 where she reported on the latest fashion news and current trends.

Currently a freelance fashion writer Kate also has her own weekly Date with Kate column, featured in The Sun-Herald. Here she interviews the most sought out local and international personalities of the moment, with a list that includes Julia Gillard, Ian Thorpe, Kim Kardashian, Michael Clarke, Miranda Kerr, Elle Macpherson and Dita Von Teese.
