Branding Strategy

Goals and KPIs Your business on line is not just about a website. It’s a whole brand environment, including web site, social media, mobile applications, digital marketing and even print and events. We’ll help you define your immediate and long-term goals, then create a strategy to get you there. We’ll track progress with KPIs and analytics to keep us moving forward.

How Are We Doing? We’ll audit your existing branding and online presence, including web site UX and UI, social media presence, SEO and ORM. We’ll compare you to your competitors to see where you can surpass them. We’ll look at your audience and customers to see where there’s a strong and supportive connection, or where there’s dissatisfaction.

Content and Scope With your input, we’ll develop a detailed plan. We’ll devise efficient ways of creating and maintaining all your content on line.

Web Site

Build It or Fix It Your web site is where your customers will land. If you’ve got a web site, and a team in place, we’ll suggest ways to make your site more attractive, faster loading and more easily usable. If you need a site from scratch, our web guys will make you a great one. Your web site is about your brand and products, not a developer’s idea of flash and frills. We believe in simplicity, beauty and efficiency.

Testing 1 2 3 4 We make sure everything we build is fast, secure and works easily from the start, and we test throughout the development process to make sure your site does exactly what it should.

Social Media

Effective and Growing Social media can generate a real ROI for your business – branding, leads, sales and more. But using Social Media is more than just posting pictures. It takes time, expertise, and strategy. Our Social Media experts know how to target your customers and how to engage and interact with them. We’ll make sure you’re not only noticed, but get responses and generate positive buzz on Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram, Pinterest and more.

Staying On Top of It We’re right there for every update and new trick on all the Social Media platforms, so we can take advantage of them as soon as they’re available. We also seamlessly integrate your Social content and campaigns and your web site, and even your off line marketing. And our ORM team takes care of monitoring the web to respond to comments about your brand anywhere they appear.

Digital Marketing

SEO & PPC Solid analytics and copywriting makes you stay at the forefront of search, even in the face of constantly changing search algorithms. When SEO on its own isn’t enough to achieve immediate results, we can help you develop effective PPC campaigns that ensure you’re only a click away from your customers.

eMarketing Keep your customers happy, and reach new ones. Targeted campaigns and sophisticated monitoring tools help you tailor your offering to the right audiences, and let you know if you’re on to a winner.

Monetization Some brands’ web sites and social media will benefit from monetization – making money off the content on your web site, YouTube channel and elsewhere. This includes automated ad placement, affiliate marketing, and content marketing.

Mobile

Works for Me Nearly three-quarters of web users use their smartphones to access their favorite social media. We’ll ensure that your web site looks great on smartphones and tablets.

Going App! For some brands, the best way to capture mobile customers is with a fast, usable and scalable mobile application. We’ll build you a beauty that will make your customers applaud.
