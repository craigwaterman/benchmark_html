Playlist of the Week

Saturday, 16 August 2014 / Music, summer

                        
     (the quote in this photo was found on pinterest, if you know the name of the author let me know so I can write it here)

I like to share with you some of the songs that I am listening to at the moment. I think, I am going to remember these songs as my spring/summer playlist of 2014. 

 I am a person that is moved by music. So, I love to listen to an up beat music during the day specially in the morning. After I have woken up, I turn to music to help me get energetic and be positive during the day. As you see, I am not a morning person, I don't like to wake up early and I can be a bit grumpy too. 
Without further ado let me show you my playlist. 

Calvin Harris - Summer 

This is my summer song. This song is so vibrant, uplifting and the lyrics is just amazing. Every time I hear this song, I just want to put the volume to the loudest, dance and sing along with the lyrics. The song makes me feel so alive and free. I love having that type of feeling from a song. 


Ed Sheeran - Sing 
I think this song is catchy and it just puts me a good mood whenever I listen to it. 


Avicii - Wake Me Up 
You know those songs that every time you hear it, you can't help but to remember a certain moment. This is like, whenever I hear "Wake Me Up".

Iggy Azalea - Fancy 
I just love this song! 

I hope you liked my playlist. How about you, what songs are listening to at the moment? let me know :)
