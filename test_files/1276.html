THANKFUL THURSDAYS

September 10, 2015


This is only my second week of Thankful Thursdays, but I'm loving the chance to reflect on the little things in life that I take for granted and see how truly blessed I really am. This week, especially, has been one of many blessings and I can't help but smile thinking about them.

If you aren't familiar with my new weekly series, it's a post (mostly for myself) where I note the things in my week that I am thankful for. It can be big or small things that happened, but the point is to see that each day brings a blessing, even if you don't feel like it's been a great week. I encourage anybody reading this to start listing things that they are thankful for each week and soon you'll see how truly amazing life is! Here's my list for the week, what's yours?


I'm so very thankful for being able to buy a plane ticket to visit a long-time friend.

 I'm thankful that my husband laughs off my obsession with Justin Bieber and just takes celebrity crushes for what it is - a celebrity crush.

 I'm thankful that my heart has had a pretty good week.

 Feeling blessed because my husband has been an awesome person at taking care of me this week when I had a health scare.

 I'm thankful that I was able to reconnect with one of my dearest college friends, somebody that has known me through the good, bad and the ugly.

 I'm thankful that my husband has been able to get off work earlier so we can spend a little more time together.

 I'm thankful that my husband always knows how to bring me down to earth when I want to buy everything at Kohl's.

 I'm also thankful that my husband likes to spoil me even when I don't deserve it.

 I'm so thankful for the return of the PSL (pumpkin spice latte, if you're not familiar with the acronym) and will try not to treat myself too much this season.

I know some of these things that I'm thankful for are silly, especially the last one, but I really am thankful that I'm able to buy a five dollar latte every once in awhile! Some people don't even get that - I remember at one of my previous jobs I would come in with Starbucks pretty much every day (it's one of my vices) and my co-workers would only get it on paydays. Obviously I know that everybody spends their money on something different, but I know I'm pretty blessed to afford even one cup of coffee!

What is something that you routinely buy or do that you might be taking for granted? Could it be driving to work each day in your own vehicle or being able to wake up and watch the sun rise each morning? Whatever you are thankful for this week, write it down and you'll start to see all the beautiful things your life has in it!
