Happy Thanksgiving

November 25, 2015

PinThisWishing you a very happy Thanksgiving this year! 

I am a bit gushy this year because I have had the amazing opportunity to work with some incredibly special couples. It is the friendships that develop while getting to know you all over the year (or years for some clients). Watching families grow, and feeling so welcome when I show up at your homes for our annual photo sessions. I have much to be grateful for this year. Being published in so many wedding blogs, being featured as a prominent artist in Litchfield County, and being able to show my work in art galleries. It is all too too much. I couldn’t ask for more. So thank you. My cup runneth over .

As my way of saying thanks, all my clients may shop for their holiday card on my Minted storefront and receive 20% off which includes free pre-printed envelopes! Shop here.

I hope you enjoy time with family & friends, enjoy the pumpkin pie, and turkey!

xo,

Kate
