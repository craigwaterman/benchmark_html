Worth the hype? Nars radiant creamy concealerÂ 

Posted in Uncategorized by onegirlsobsessionwithmakeup

Definatley! This concealer is more than worthy of the hype ! 

It hides a multitude of Sins , eye bags , blotchey redness , pimples . It’s perfect !!!

I was matched to the shade vanilla in store for my light skin tone.   

I use it for eye bags mostly but when I want to use it for spots I don’t use the applicator I use my finger to apply it so therefore my spots won’t be spread. 

 

I have been using this for a few months now and almost daily and I can say it’s my favourite concealer by far , it’s the first nars item I have had as the stores not close to me and it’s enticed me to buy more from the company!  

 

The only problem I have with this concealer is that it runs out fast wich may have something to do with how much I use it! Also it’s slightly pricey for a concealer but it’s way way worth it.

Posted in Uncategorized by onegirlsobsessionwithmakeup

I bought this on a trip to the spa with a voucher that I was given upon arrival , I wasn’t looking for anything but I thought this smelt lovely. When I got home I did some research and it’s really well rated it’s suppose to be a cheaper version of Emma hardie cleansing balm , which I haven’t tried but I would love to!

ï»¿ï»¿

This smells like a spa would be great for people that are into aromathearpy, it smells like lavender which is lovely at night.

I have combination skin so I only use it 2 times a week as it could make my skin oily but twice a week it’s great. 

I use it by massageing it it once I have removed my eye make up i then get a muslin cloth and put it in hot water , then I put the cloth over my face for a few minutes to steam my face and get into my pores then wipe the remainder with the rest of the cloth. It comes with its own Muslin cloth.

I have also used it by leaving it on after  massaging fit in and using it as a mask.
