Makeover Photography

Get the magazine makeover experience! 

Why do women in magazines look so amazing?  Because they’re styled and photographed by professionals.  Trust me – they don’t look like that when they pop to the shop for milk.  Get your gorgeous on with a makeover photo shoot and find out for yourself.  Our Makeover Experience is a fantastic pamper session with something to keep forever.  We’re bringing cool glamour back to the makeover shoot with on trend styling.  It’s your turn.

Because striking images are created, not just captured, we work with you on styling your shoot to suit your personality, choice of outfits and the look you want to project.  The more effort you put into the the more fun you’ll have and the gorgeouser (it’s a word!) your photographs will be.

My talented stylists are trained in make-up for photography and have worked many fashion shoots and Fashion Weeks across Europe.  They know how to style you gorgeous and I know how to shoot you gorgeous.  I will light and direct you into poses to suit your body and choice of outfits to enhance your beauty.

How does a Makeover Photo Shoot work?

A makeover photo shoot at my stunning Sussex makeover photography studio, includes more than just awesome makeup and confidence boosting photography.  It is a couple of hours of pampering, chats and giggles and you end up looking gorgeous and feeling better than you ever did on your very best night out in that outfit (the one still hanging in your cupboard that you swear you will one day wear again).

Here’s what you’ll get with your Makeover Experience:

    • I want to design your perfect photo shoot, so we start with a consultation to chat about you and your personal style.
    • Then I’ll send you hints and tips to help you prepare and get the most from your makeover experience.
    • Your makeover experience starts with an hour of getting gorgeoused by our awesome hair and makeup stylists.
    • Then it’s into the studio for an hour of photography.
    • Your makeover photo shoot will be fun, styled, designed and posed just for you.
    • Before you leave the studio we will book your ordering appointment for a week later.
    • Meanwhile I will process, polish and perfect your stunning images ready for the big reveal.
    • At your ordering appointment I will help you select your favourite images and how you’d like to showcase them.
    • Then my super brilliant suppliers will get busy and a few weeks later you can take home the best photographs ever taken of you.

Makeover shoots are feel good photography at its best, a gift and an experience that will last forever!

Even better – do it with somebody you love!  Makeover photo shoots are perfect for mothers and daughters, sisters or best friends.

Contact my Sussex makeover photography studio to start planning how you dream of being photographed.  01323 723 963
