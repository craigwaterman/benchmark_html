Lisa & Steven / Surprise Proposal in Central Park on Gapstow Bridge

Steven contacted the amazing ladies of A Central Park Wedding to plan his surprise proposal to his then girlfriend Lisa. Christa and her team had flowers and a live guitarist playing Lisa’s favorite song in place as they walked across Gapstow Bridge in Central Park. Stephen then got down on one knee and she did say… YES! Enjoy the photo documentation I took of this very special day.

74 Events Rocks!

As a New York City wedding photographer I have had the opportunity to work with many DJ’s  and all I can say is there is a wide range of styles out there. One company that stands out in the New York City wedding circuit is 74 Events.  These guys are AWESOME! Whenever I have the opportunity to work with them I am beyond excited. They really can get any party going big or small. Gary Hoffman and the 74 Events Team go beyond your expectation to create a memorable playlist for your party and the way they work the crowd is also nothing short of amazing!
