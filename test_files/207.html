Friday, March 20, 2015

Welcome Spring!


I am ready for spring!  Planted my first flowers today. 


I also added a tiny owl and turtle to the garden. Keira has to go outside and pet them multiple times per day. 


The bulbs from the fall are blooming!  Just waiting for the tulips now!



I am obsessed with my yard. Who would have guessed?  Gardening isn't really my thing...or at least it didn't used to be. 


I bought these adadorable ground covers and can't wait to plant them but I have to wait a few more weeks. I also bought some summer bulbs to plant - I am so excited.


And I decided to bring a bit of the outdoors in - tulips in a mason jar?!  Yes Please. This fall I want to get a bunch of bulbs to force so I can have fresh flowers all year.


Chase looks adorable in his new shades. 


And look at my fun new piercings!
