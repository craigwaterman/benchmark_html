Car Makeover

Posted on January 7, 2013 | Leave a comment

My current car was 2 years old when I bought it in 2006.  It was the first car I ever made payments on, and I love this car.  It is reliable and still looks pretty good for its age.

We are a one-income family now that I am a stay at home mom.  I wish that we could afford a new car, but I would miss this one.  I’m thinking about doing a small makeover and putting in some sheepskin seat covers.  They look cozy, and that goes a long way in the winter!  The more I look at them the more I love the look of these luxury car seat covers.  They look so soft and inviting.

While I was looking at seat covers I decided to look at other sheepskin products.  They all look so soft, warm and comfortable. I just want to wrap myself in a sheepskin rug and get a sheepskin crib mattress cover for my daughter.
