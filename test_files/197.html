Paleo hamburger bread

Posted on May 23, 2013 by crossfitmanumit • Posted in Uncategorized • Leave a comment

Recipe

0.85 cups (2 dl) almond flower

2 tablespoon coconut flower

0.2 cups (0.5 dl) sesame seed

2 tablespoon psyllium seed husks

2 teaspoon baking soda

0.5 teaspoon salt

3 eggs

0,2 cups (0.5 dl) coconut fat

0,4 cups (1 dl) water

Put the ovan on 350 F (175 C)

1. Mix the dry ingreedients together

2.Wisp the eggs, coconut fat and water in a different bowl.

3. Mix it all together and let say for 5min

Roll the balls (easy with a little wet hands) and place on a parchment paper covered plate.
Flatten slightly and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
