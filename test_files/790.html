Jared Hail

Jared Hail is a professional cinematographer based in the Pacific Northwest. His creative process and unique content visualization through a lens is truly individual. Jared has worked throughout the west coast on behalf of organizations and companies such as Providence Health Care, Southern Oregon University, Travel Medford, HP, UPS and the PAC-12.
