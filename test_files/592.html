My Fashionable Sister and my New Bangs

Hello all! It's been so long since I've blogged I don't even know how to update anymore!

First of all, thank you everyone for all your comments on my shoes and sharing your favorite shoes as well! I'm so excited to wear them this spring and hope to see your shoes some day too!

I have some pictures of my sister Loan to share today. She is always so fashionable and daring, I love her! She really does need to take pictures of her outfits more often though, she would have an awesome style blog if she did!

At a recent photo shoot in her favorite store Patricia Field

At a fashion show during the recent fashion week in NY

Can be found in gettyimages, heheh I wonder if they are selling this picture? 

My sister Loan is going to be in a documentary soon. They raised enough money to make it and I think shooting starts during Fashion Week in NY this coming fall. I'm so excited for her!

Here's the video pitch, it's very inspiring:




Hehehe some credit goes to me on this video, a lot of the clips were recorded by me while I visited last summer :). I hope to get some more this summer too!!

And back to me! LOL
I got tired of my hair so I cut my bangs this week.

I added more hair so it's thicker than I've ever had for bangs. YouTube tutorials I watched include:
kandeejohnson, kaaatttmarie, and Nee
 I used shears and mostly a razor. 

I realized I haven't worn this hat outside yet! I must remember to wear it soon! Do you have any hats you haven't worn? Unfortunately I have quite a few! I loooove trying on hats!

My boyfriend bought it for me while we were trying on hats in Carmel, CA for our anniversary last year. It's designed by San Marcon and her hats are all so lovely and fun to try on! I want more!

I bought this Sonia Kashuk eyeliner brush to try today, since Keiko Lynn highly recommended this on her blog.

This is how I looked earlier in the day with only bb cream, face powder, and some blush (you probably can't see it though)
And this is after I used the eyeliner brush and Maybelline Eyestudio gel eyeliner in Blackest Black. Also trying on a new top I bought at Nordstrom Rack. Will post some pictures of it in the near future, because it's so pretty and bohemian!

Compared to the brush that comes with the Maybelline Eyeliner, as well as the one that comes with the Lioele All-in-One gel eyeliner (the quality of this one is really nice too), I think the Sonia Kashuk brush is the easiest one to use for a winged eye liner efftect. I love this brush so far, because I've always had trouble making a nice winged eyeliner, and this one totally did the trick! It's really precise and clean! Do you girls have this brush too or something else that does the job nicely as well?

And last but not least, I made bookmarks this week! I drew my character on Corel and mass produced them on bristol printing paper. Now I have even more reason to read my textbooks :D.

Midterms are coming up, so I hopefully I will be disappearing for a while to catch up on my studies and projects :(. I always love looking at your blogs though, you girls are all so inspirational! Can't wait for some more free time!! <3
