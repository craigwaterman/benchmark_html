Autumn Beauty Trends from Fashion Week

Posted on March 17, 2015 by mummabee

0

The fashion industry can soon take a much needed vacation with Paris, Milan and London Fashion Week coming to a close. As the weather starts to cool down here in Australia, there are number of key beauty looks that we can take straight from the Autumn/Winter 2016 runway to the streets.

Our friends at Popsugar.com.au have THE hair and beauty looks from the runway and front row’s of Paris, Milan and London. Ladies, it’s time to start embracing the ‘slept-in’ tousled blow-dry, long lashes, structured eyebrows and dewy hydrated skin, which is uber important in the drier cold months!

Lily Aldridge at Michael Kors AW2015

Getting her beauty chic absolutely on point at the Michael Kors show, Lily makes a sleek up style and kohl lined eyes our ultimate in timeless beauty looks.

Kate Hudson at Michael Kors AW2015

Kate’s softly shimmering silver eyes look really pretty set off with a glossy red lip colour at the Michael Kors show.

 

Gigi Hadid and Kendall Jenner at the Paris Fashion Week Aftershow Dinner

Matching shimmering skin and smokey eyes for these fresh faced models.

 

Clémence Poésy at Burberry AW2015

Tousled, messy texture? No problem. Clémence Poésy makes it her beauty signature at theBurberry show.

Jennifer Connelly at Louis Vuitton AW2015

This fuchsia lip colour looks amazing against a defined brow and long lashes.

Shanina Shaik at David Tlale AW2015

Our Aussie model export lets her natural beauty shine with a barely there base and super-long lashes at the David Tlale show.

Kim Kardashian at Louis Vuitton AW2015

The lady who started it all! Kim keeps her dark brows neat and defined, and keeps her signature cat-eye makeup look.

 

Olivia Palermo at Dennis Basso AW2015

Nailing front row chic at the Dennis Basso show, Olivia pairs her fashionable hair-tuck with smoked out eyes and a nude lip.

 

Leigh Lezark at Chanel AW2015

Not everyone’s going to the light side! Leigh rocks her blue-black coloured lob with a kohl smoked eye and looks flawless.

Doutzen Kroes, Jared Leto and Gigi Hadid at the Paris Fashion Week Tasting Night

Another new member of the platinum blonde family, Jared Leto, makes the other blondes beside him look pretty brunette in comparison!

Lily Donaldson and Doutzen Kroes at the Paris Fashion Week Tasting Night

Matching voluminous swept-to-the-side styles for these Victoria’s Secret regulars.

Chloe Moretz at Louis Vuitton AW2015

Natural and neutral makeup paired with soft flowing locks, Chloe hits chic on the head way before her time.

 

Jess Hart at Tory Burch AW2015

Jess is lapping up those fringe benefits! We love this cute up ‘do and glossy lip combo at the Troy Burch show.

Cara Delevingne at Burberry AW2015

Can we just take a moment to appreciate Cara Delevingne’s amazing hair at the Burberry show? That colour. That texture. It’s just so on point.

 

Jourdan Dunn at Burberry AW2015

Sleek and glowing, Jourdan Dunn shines in all the right places at the Burberry show.

Chrissy Teigen at Badgley Mischka AW2015

Chrissy’s lightened locks, at the Badgley Mischka show, work back beautiful with her skintone. We love the individual lashes just on the outer corners to give her eyes a soft flick, the browny-red lip colour is the perfect neutral shade on the half Thai beauty.

Gigi Hadid Tory Burch AW2015

Who said it was too cold to bare your legs? Gigi shines from head to toe at the Tory Burch show.
