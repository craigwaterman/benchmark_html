Woman Cave

This is how women do Caves. A Woman's Cave should be unique and luxurious, a retreat and a place to entertain. A bar is a must so make it beautiful by adding a pair of gorgeous Plylis Morris faux-bamboo metal barstools from CHARISH.COM. Why not store your favorite libations in a stunning vintage decanter display on top of a vibrant orange tray. Use color and texture surrounding yourself with things you love and you'll create a space you'll love to live in.
