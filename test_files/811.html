Wellness Retreats

“Any time women come together with collective intention, it’s a powerful thing.”

~Phylicia Rashad

Okay, ladies…who is ready to take back their health? Come spend four fun-filled days learning and applying the tools that you can use in your daily life to take better care of yourself and your family. We will sweat, learn, support, encourage and inspire each other.

Accommodations: We will stay at a beach house in Sandbridge Beach, VA. Each bedroom has its own private bathroom and all linens are included. Amenities include pool, hot tub and beach access right outside your back door. Most meals (including the Farm to Table Cooking Class) are included.

Scheduled Activities: All fitness classes are taught by certified instructors.
~2 hour bike tour through a wildlife preserve or 2 hour kayak tour (dependent upon the weather).
~60 minute guided meditation class.
~60 minute yoga class on the beach.
~60 minute beach boot camp class.
~Hiking in False Cape State Park (3 miles from house)
~Plenty of unscheduled beach and pool time too!!

Workshops: Topics covered include: Setting realistic wellness goals, quick & easy whole food recipes, time management tips that work, how to get the whole family on board, healing with food & herbs (natural remedies) and an introduction to EFT/Tapping by a certified practitioner. There will also be a session to put together your “wellness toolbox” that you will take home with you so you can apply everything you’ve learned.

Farm to Table Cooking Class: On Saturday night we will tour a local organic farm and learn about the importance of buying local and organic. We will be immersed in the heart of where real food comes from, preparing our meal right on the farm, in their learning garden, and enjoying it outside under the stars!

Cost: All-Inclusive price includes accommodations, activities and all meals (except Friday night dinner). All deposits and final payments are non-refundable so please be sure you are able to attend before you register. You may transfer your reservation to another person if you are unable to attend but there are no refunds. Space is limited so this will be on a first come first served basis.

~$975 for a single room (single occupancy)
~$900 for a shared room (double occupancy)
~$350 non-refundable deposit is required at registration in order to reserve your spot.

So grab your bestie, sister, daughter, aunt, mom or just come alone and meet some great ladies!!
