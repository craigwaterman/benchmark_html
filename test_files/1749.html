Colorado Teen Summer Adventure - Bold Earth

from Bold Earth Teen Adventures 2 years ago Not Yet Rated

boldearth.com/summer-mountain-bike-whitewater-raft-teen-adventure-camp

Colorado Adventure Summer Camp - Mountain Bike, Hike and Whitewater Raft! Experience the true spirit of the West while exploring Colorado and Utah! From the soaring peaks of Colorado's Rocky Mountains to the desert valleys of Utah's canyon country, this epic summer expedition features unforgettable adventures over two adrenaline-packed weeks!
COLORADO: BACKPACK THE ROCKIES
Leadership, adventure and friendship are the goals on our three-day backpack through Colorado's soaring Gore Mountain Range. Rugged Rocky Mountain peaks and high-country meadows provide a pristine backdrop as we learn backcountry skills, get to know each other and embrace the spirit of the West!

UTAH: WHITEWATER RAFT
We push west from Colorado into the desert of Utah, where Westwater and Horsethief Canyons stun the senses with a mix of whitewater, red rock and endless blue skies. Our rafting trip down the Colorado River combines thrilling rapids with an amazing perspective of the magnificent sandstone formations near Moab, home of world-famous Arches National Park.

COLORADO: MOUNTAIN BIKE
We end our adventure with an epic five-day mountain bike trip in Colorado's majestic Western Slope, where local guides show us the secrets of mountain biking atop the Grand Mesa. This supported mountain bike adventure is exclusive to Bold Earth! For 25 years, we have owned a special use permit in this protected area, allowing us unparalleled access to a myriad of shimmering lakes and world-class trails!
