Dreaming Of Lake Winnie

By Kelly Cordes February 11, 2014 5:08 PM
Share on Facebook
Share on Twitter

 

 

This below zero weather has me thinking about our family trip up to Lake Winnie each year.  This photo might look like a rebel group of young wild men, but actually, this is roughing it at Lake Winnie.  Cousins…enjoying the great outdoors….waiting for their turn to go fishing.  From Left to Right:  Mike, Brody, and Drew.  Cousins that share the same joy as their parents, and their grandparents. As a matter of fact, it’s these kids that keep the tradition going year after year.  Here’s to you, boys!  May you always remember Lake Winnie and all of our great times together.  I’m so looking forward to another year!
