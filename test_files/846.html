{Attire}

For children;

Comfortable clothing, avoiding distracting logos. Nice casuals, kakis, jeans look great in photos with layers, plaids and/or lots of vibrant color/s. Fun summer dresses, or outfits with lots of bright colors/textures photograph nicely ie. ruffles, textures, layers. Hair ribbons/accessories look fantastic too! Color color color! I also have a few pretty tutu's, on hand, for the young ones to wear (fits ages infants up to age 8-9ish).

For adults;

Nice pair of jeans, corduroys or khakis, with nice dress (or casual) shoes avoiding logos, not-so dressy t-shirts and an "all" white shirts. Shirts with nice color/textures with color and style look great even nice hats, suspenders on little kids or even bowties are adorable as well, have fun!

For ladies a cute pair of your favorite jeans layered with a fun stylish top, or layered with a fun jacket or your favorite knit! Fun stylish dresses! Pretty tops with lots of color that are fitted or flowy. Vibrant colors, soft pastel colors and/or bold colors and textures look the best ie. color accents like jewelry/accessory accents ie. earrings, necklaces and/or bracelets, scarfs, hats, ruffles etc. to go along with your outfit! Paired with a pair of high heal shoes, cute shoes, or high or chunky boots. Don't forget the makeup especially blush to give cheeks a bit of color! Wear what you feel great in and have fun!

You are welcome to bring extra clothing for full sessions, I'd be happy to help you chose an outfit.
