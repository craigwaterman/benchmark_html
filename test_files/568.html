    My Drag Race Summer 2014 // GSX-R 1000, S1000RR, Hayabusa

    Thought id share some pics of how my summer went.

    I bought a stock GSX-R 1000 K6 in the beginning of this year. Decided to mod it a bit for a stockwheelbase class that we have here in Iceland. Added a M4 Slip on exhaust, PC5, brocks lowering links, 525 xring chain with 17/45 gearing.

    IÂ´m 210 lbs suited and my best time at stockwheelbase on that bike was 9.6@150mph.
    This season ended great for me. I competed in two classes each with 3x races, so six races total
    I became Icelandic drag race champion in my class which is 1/4 mile and 3x competitions over the summer
    And also Icelandic street drag race champion which is 3x 1/8 mile competitions over the summer

    Same rules for both classes, has to be stock motor, no strap allowed, you can lower your bike but not all the way to the ground, boltons allowed, no power adder.


    Also got to try out some of my friends bikes at the track.

    Tried out a Gen 1 Hayabusa with a stage 1 turbo. Had never driven it before and went 9.3@152mph after a couple of runs

    also tried a 2013 BMW S1000RR with a couple of bolton mods and bolton extensions, went 9.4@150mph on it on my 3rd run. Had never sat on it before

    And then i got to try out a almost completely stock K7 1000 with just a leovince dual slip on + cat delete. No other mods and stock height , no strap. Went 9.92@148mph after a couple of runs

    all in all great season of drag racing
