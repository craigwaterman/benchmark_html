19 Game Terbaru YAng Dirilis Tahun 2015

03:10  Berita Terbaru, Game Terbaru  No comments

Release date: 30 January 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, Windows
This one is about to launch, so zombie-fans are getting pretty excited. It's a post-apocalyptic survival horror game (if you're waiting for H1Z1, which we talk about later in this article, then this game is another one you'll enjoy). Freerunning around this open world game is set to be frighteningly awesome. Find out more in our Dying Light preview.

Best games coming out in 2015: Monster Hunter 4 Ultimate

Release date: 13 February 2015
Platforms: Nintendo 3DS
There's not long to wait for UK-based Monster Hunter fans, as the next instalment, Monster Hunter 3 Ultimate, is coming to Nintendo 3DS on 13 February, to coincide with the launch of the New 3DS, which has several improvements including a second analogue stick which will come in very handy for this game.
Team up with friends to battle monsters and carve items essential for creating better armour and weapons to help you fight even nastier monsters. New weapons include the Insect Glaive and Charge Blade, and new monsters are bigger and better than ever.

Best games coming out in 2015: The Order: 1886


Release date: 20 February 2015
Platforms: PS4
The hype surrounding this PS4 exclusive continues to grow as we approach its release date. Set in London in an alternate history, the game follows an order of knights who aim to rid the world of strange monsters that appear to be half man, half animal.

Best games coming out in 2015: Battlefield Hardline

Release date: 20 March 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, PS3, Xbox 360, Windows
The latest Battlefield title from EA will arrive later this year after becoming available in beta form in June 2014, and it's called Battlefield Hardline. It features a whole new variety of gameplay, which breaks from the traditional style of previous editions. Instead of a military focus, it moves to police and the war on crime. Read about the Battlefield Hardline open beta.
Players will either be criminals or part of the police Special Response Unit. New game modes are Heist, Rescue, Hotwire Mode and Blood Money. Weapons include UH-60 Blackhawk, tasers and even handcuffs.
See also: Best gaming PCs 2015

Best games coming out in 2015: Resident Evil Revelations 2


Release date: 13 March 2015
Platforms: PS4, PS Vita, PS3, Xbox One, Xbox 360, Windows
Another zombie game for you now, this one adding to the popular Resident Evil franchise as the tenth instalment. It's set to be released gradually in episodic format, starting with the first episode on 25 February and being released as a full version shortly after on 20 March. Find out more in our Revelations 2 hands-on review.

Best games coming out in 2015: Mortal Kombat X

Release date: 14 April 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, PS3, Xbox 360, Windows
Here's one for fans of fighting games. Mortal Kombat lovers will be counting down the days until this tenth instalment in the series is released, with the return of some of the franchise's most-loved characters and the introduction of some new fighters.
See also: Funniest video game glitches

Best games coming out in 2015: The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Release date: 19 May 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, Windows
The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt was named Most Anticipated Game during Las Vegas's The Game Awards in 2014, so it certainly deserves a place on this list. It's an action-packed, open world roleplaying game that follows on from its two predecessors. The series was inspired by The Witcher fantasy novels by Andrzej Sapkowski.
Here's everything you need to know about The Witcher 3 including release date, price, editions and DLC.

Best games coming out in 2015: Batman: Arkham Knight


You won't want to miss the conclusion to the epic Arkham series. Arkham Knight follows the release of Arkham Origins but is set one year on from Arhkam City. Batman is at the peak of his ability and must confront the supervillain Scarecrow.
You'll be able to drive the Batmobile on walls, which is pretty much enough for us but there's more. There are new takedowns, the ability to launch higher, glide for longer new gadgets and more. The game looks better than ever so we can't wait.
Find out more in our Batman: Arkham Knight round-up

Best games coming out in 2015: Until Dawn


Release date: TBC 2015 (Summer)
Platforms: PS4
Another PS4 exclusive now, this one a very scary-looking horror/thriller. Created by Supermassive Games, Until Dawn has already been getting rave reviews after it was demoed at Gamescom 2014. It's been described as the "horror-themed Heavy Rain you've been waiting for." Find out more in our Until Dawn release date & rumours article

Best games coming out in 2015: Kingdom Hearts 3

Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One
We've been waiting for Kingdom Hearts 3 for over 10 years, so to know it's coming soon is particularly exciting. The epic conclusion to the Final Fantasy/Disney mash-up is set to be a real fan-pleaser, and we can't wait to find out what's in store for Sora (shown at the top of this article), Roxas and friends. Read: Kingdom Hearts 3 release date, gameplay, screenshots, trailer

Best games coming out in 2015: No Man's Sky


Release date TBC 2015
Platforms: PS4, Windows
No Man's Sky is an incredibly cool-looking sci-fi game that lets players explore and survive in an infinite procedurally generated universe. Planets and solar systems will be built as you play, and each will be different to the next. Watch trailers and find out more in our No Man's Sky release date article.

Best games coming out in 2015: H1Z1


Release date: TBC, available now in beta
Platforms: PS4, Windows
Sony Online Entertainment first announced H1Z1, a zombie apocalypse survival MMO for PS4 and PC in April 2015, and excited game fans have been talking about it ever since. It's available to play now on PC in beta, but is expected to properly launch later this year. You can find out more in our H1Z1 release date, gameplay and screenshots article.

Best games coming out in 2015: Tom Clancy's The Division


Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, Windows
Tom Clancy's The Division is one of the most talked about games right now, but the post-apocalyptic shooter doesn't have an official release date yet despite being expected this year. See screenshots from The Division and find out what to expect from the game in our Tom Clancy's The Division round-up.

Best games coming out in 2015: Halo 5: Guardians

Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: Xbox One
Halo 5: Guardians has been confirmed by 343 Industries, and the release is expected in 2015 for Xbox One. Check out our Halo 5 release date article for more.

Best games coming out in 2015: Zelda for Wii U


Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: Wii U
Ok, you might have have a wait a while for this but it will be worth the wait, we hope. Although Nintendo has confirmed Zelda for Wii U, we still haven't got a solid release date aside from a very vague 2015. We had to wait a long time for Mario Kart 8 and it was worthwhile, so we're sure the same will be true here.
Zelda for Wii U will be set in a completely open world and Nintendo's short teaser is enough to get us excited about the game. Read: Nintendo showcases its future at E3 2014: It looks great but there's a catch.

Best games coming out in 2015: Rise of Tomb Raider


Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: Xbox One, Xbox 360
Despite having no specific release date, there's already controversy surrounding the next Tomb Raider game, as it looks set to be an Xbox exclusive, at least to begin with. It's the second game in the latest Tomb Raider reboot, following on from the events of the last game that was released in 2013 on both Xbox and PlayStation, hence the confusion about the sequel's exclusivity to Microsoft's console. You can find out more about Rise of Tomb Raider here.

Best games coming out in 2015: Star Wars Battlefront

Release date: Late 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, Windows
Star Wars: Battlefront is getting a reboot for the new generation of consoles, and will be released alongside the Star Wars: The Force Awakens movie late this year. It's being developed by EA, and will include playable maps of the planet Hoth and the moon Endor.
Find out more about Star Wars Battlefront in our round-up.

Best games coming out in 2015: Just Cause 3

Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, Windows
Following on from 2010's Just Cause 2, this open world action-adventure game will be set in a map similar to its predecessor's but will include all-new caverns to explore, and more realistically scalable buildings. The grappling hook and parachute that those who've played Just Cause 2 will be familiar with are set to be joined by a new (and likely really awesome) wingsuit. Find out more in our Just Cause 3 release date & rumours article.

Best games coming out in 2015: Uncharted 4: A Thief's End

Release date: TBC 2015
Platforms: PS4
We are super-excited about Uncharted 4: A Thief's End. It's an upcoming third-person shooter that adds to the popular Uncharted series, following on from Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception but set three years after that game ended. Drake is joined by his apparently not-dead-after-all brother Sam as they attempt to solve a mystery involving pirates and, of course, hidden treasure.
