My Favorite Fall/Winter Accessory: The Scarf

 

Scarves date back to the ancient Romans who wore a linen kerchief called the sudarium (Latin for "sweat cloth"). They eventually developed the scarf into a diverse men's fashion accessory (talk about product development!!). The style was immediately adopted by women (naturally!).

 

 
  

With San Diego's legendary 75 degree Falls and 65 degree Winters, we obviously wear scarves for fashion rather than functionality (unless it is a very rare "cold" day hee hee hee). All of the above featured San Diegans are wearing thin scarves wrapped loosely around their necks or just draped.

  

There are dozens and dozens of different types of scarves and a dozen different ways to wear them! Soooooo my fave Fall accessory!

My newest scarf obsession? THE TUBE SCARF!!! Basically just a scarf where the ends are sewn together making a circle.
