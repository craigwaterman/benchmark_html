Style News: Hardrige x colette Boots Collabo

Submitted by Gabriel Williams on January 26, 2010 – 11:04 am85 Comments
Share

Hardrige and Paris-based fashion collective colette have teamed up to create a military aspect of a stylish boot. Boots have become a major trend in today’s society, so be sure to take advantage of the different styles that revolve around you.

The French Army-inspired boot contains specifications of military grade. All of the hand work for this boot was done in France.

This ankle-high boot is the most detailed we’ve seen to date – double-straps, functional zippers on each side and a inspired snake skin patterned tongue and toe. This boot is most definitely at the top of our list.

Switching up from the average sneaker is always a good and mature look. Sneakers should not always be apart of your wardrobe. Learn how to be a little more versatile, when it comes to your choice of clothing or footwear.
