About Me

My awkward nursing school mugshot ;)
First, welcome to my mostly YA book blog! *waves* My name is Liliana, or Lili for short, and I live in Washington state where I JUST graduated from nursing school! I survived!! Here are a couple of random things about myself:

My love for reading inspired my love for writing. I've got so many great idea in my head that I cannot wait to put on paper. Nursing school, though, is taking up most of my time right now *sigh* However, I gathered the courage to join NaNoWriMo 2013 and Camp NaNo July 2014 and I actually started a novel! :D Now to finish said novel...

My favorite color is purple and I might be a little obsessed with it... can you tell? ;)

I love movies, but then again, who doesn't? ;) I especially LOVE horror movies that can scare the Hades out of me! I'm not the type of girl who'll sit down and watch chick flicks like The Notebook...nope, not for me. The scarier the movie the better! I also like movies that can make me laugh until me sides hurt and action-y movies (like The Avengers and Oh My Gods Captain America 2!! Well, I like almost any Marvel movie, really.)

Speaking of Marvel, I am a HUGE Marvel nerd!! I mean, I don't read the comics but I am a huge fan of the movies, as I said. I love me all the Avengers (although I have a soft spot for the Cap ;))

TV shows: I never miss Pretty Little Liars! I love that show! I also love Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. (Yay! More Marvel! :D) I am also a HUGE fan of Charmed!! Witches FTW! And for my inner child: Hey Arnold!, the Rugrats and Mighty Morphin Power Rangers. Ah, nostalgia :) I was a 90s kid, after all ;) And oh my gods, guys I've recently become ridiculously obsessed with the show Supernatural! And I regret nothing!! :D

I am totally in love with mythology! And you can blame Rick Riordan and his AMAZING books for that ;) I love all mythology (Egyptian, Norse...) but Greek has got to be my favorite! My favorite gods/goddesses include: Apollo (god of the sun, music, archery, and healing), Hecate (goddess of crossroads and magic), and I have recently added Hades (god of the dead and the Underworld) to that list :)

If I were to have some really cool supernatural ability, it would be healing. I've always loved that power :)  Now, if I were to be a supernatural creature, I would be a witch! I love magic! Witches FTW!

As for music, my faves are Panic! At The Disco and Fall Out Boy. I LOVE them!

Also currently obsessed with nail art and making cool hairstyles and braids... I LOVE BRAIDS!!

 

I may also be addicted to Pinterest... No, I don't need help, thank you very much ;)
    Uhh...that's all I can think of right now because my life, sadly, is not that interesting, lol.
