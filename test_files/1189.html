Eduardo was born and raised in Mexico City. Having lived for over 11 years in San Antonio, TX, he has a very well understanding of both Mexican and American cultures. Eduardo holds a BBA in International Business from UIW in San Antonio TX. Being passionate for social projects, in 2010, with some friends Eduardo founded AEM Jóvenes, a binational organization that seeks to promote the intellectual and professional development for young Mexican entrepreneurs. As an business leader, Eduardo has participated in many different projects, from organizing sporting events for local charities to developing high-social-impact tech startups such as Cityflag, a mobile app where citizens can report public infrastructure issues and get rewarded and Bag, a mobile app where users have the ability to order groceries on-demand.As far as professional experience Eduardo worked as a business consultant for EY and interned at the NADBank and IPI. Eduardo is a competitive triathlete and enjoys reading, photography and learning new things.
