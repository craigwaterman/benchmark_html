Spring/ Summer 2008 Lips Makeup Trends

Summer is here and we are ready to be sexy, bright and charming! And all we need to know what are the hottest makeup ideas. I’d like to discuss the latest lips makeup trends. Why? What is the sexiest part of your face? Of course it’s your lips. Lips makeup is able to create absolutely different images of you. What are the hottest beauty trends for your lips for this spring? You can choose nude lips, like snow queen, can wear juicy pink lip gloss, or choose sexy deep red lipstick. It’s up to you. I can say that on Spring/Summer fashion week 2008 you can find almost all of these views! So let’s look closer at each of them.

 

 

First are deep red lips! I like this hot look! Wear your favorite red lipstick and make sure that it’s really deep red color. Don’t be afraid to be bright this spring and summer. For such sexy red lips image from Derek Lam you can try this Vincent Longo Satin Matte Lipstick, Dakota Red shade.



Second trend is completely opposite – nude lips. You can complete this make up with smoky eyes like the Ellie Saab models do. I like this makeup idea, because it is tender and sexy at once. For such nude effect apply some toner base at your lips and only after that apply your lipstick. You may choose LORAC Sheer Lipstick for this image.



The next one is bright colorful lips! Orange, fuchsia, bright pink …Sounds really great! This summer you can make some experiments with new colors! To make your look like Valentino models you can try Guerlain KissKiss Lipstick. Enjoy new bright colors!



And this one is my favorite – natural juicy lips. It makes young and fresh image. So if you a big lip gloss fan and if you want to keep natural look like Phillip Lim models – this choice is for you. You can try different colors of NARS Lip Gloss, but I suggest Sweet Dreams shadow.

But what I like the best at these spring/summer trends is that you can change every moment! Enjoy this summer! Try new images and stay beautiful!
