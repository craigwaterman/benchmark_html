32 Week Bumpdate + Weird Things About Pregnancy

April 7, 2015 // 4 Comments

We’re officially under the 2 month mark people! I can now say “We’re having a baby next month!” Eeeek!

That is, assuming that Baby Freckle actually cooperates and shows up in May. I’m going to keep thinking happy thoughts that he’ll actually get here on time!

Here’s to hoping that April continues to fly by, and May gets here soon! I’m so ready to meet the little guy!

How far along? 32 weeks (and 3 days)

Baby’s size? The size of large jicama or a Florida tangelo. Apparently we’re getting quite specific this week! He weighs approximately 3.75lbs and is about 16 ¾ inches long. He’ll gain most of his birth weight in the next 7 weeks!

Total weight gain/loss: Around 23-24lbs, but it fluctuates throughout the day

Maternity clothes? Yup, and I’m starting to run out of things to wear now that the weather is getting warmer!

Stretch marks? Nope, but I’m not holding my breath. I know that this is when most people start to get them because you’re belly starts growing significantly!

Sleep: Sleep is still all over the place. Some days I sleep like a rock and other days I toss and turn all night. I also had a return of the pregnancy insomnia this week and could not fall asleep to save my life. Sigh… I guess this is preparing me for when Baby Freckle gets here!

Best moment this week: Our first Baby Shower! We were so blessed to have so many family and friends attend and spoil Baby Freckle. He is going to be one loved little man!

Miss Anything? Being able to workout like I used to. I do my best to stay active, but now that it’s springtime I’m definitely missing being able to run! Even at the gym, I’m noticing that I can’t lift as much as I could, or push myself as hard. I’m getting antsy to get back into shape!

Movement: Oh yea, all the time. He’s a little kungfu master in there and keeps kicking me in the ribs!

Food cravings: Still loving spicy foods. I’ve also had some weird, VERY specific cravings this week for a tuna melt and a bean tostada. Not really sure where either of those came from, but I just can’t get them out of my head!

Anything making you queasy or sick: Nope. But I am starting to have some… errr… digestive issues again. Pregnancy does weird things to your colon apparently.

Gender: Boy!

Labor Signs: None, although I think I’ve had a few more Braxton Hicks here and there.

Symptoms: I’m starting to feel more and more pressure down there (if you catch my drift) since Baby Freckle has already started to move himself into position. I’m also just generally starting to feel pregnant, in that heavy, tired, blah kind of way. I even feel like I’m starting to get the pregnancy waddle sometimes! This is really the first time all pregnancy that I’ve actually felt “pregnant” in a negative way. He is definitely growing in there!

Belly Button in or out? Still in, and probably staying in.

Wedding rings on or off? On! But definitely getting a touch tighter if I eat too much sodium.

Happy or Moody most of the time: Happy a majority of the time, but a tad more emotional than usual. It has really started to hit me that he’ll be here soon, and I feel like those maternal instincts are kicking in! Every time I see a little boy, my heart swells just a little bit.

Looking forward to: Going through and organizing all of Baby Freckle’s stuff from our showers! I can’t wait to actually start putting things together and actually getting things ready for his arrival!

Weird Things I’ve Experienced During Pregnancy

I’m not going to lie, pregnancy is a strange time. For someone who is a serious control freak, it’s kind of been bizarre to have zero idea or say in what is happening to your body anymore. And it’s definitely done some weird things over the past 8 months!

Just for fun, I thought I’d share a few of the very bizarre things that have happened so far during pregnancy that I was NOT expecting.

- They always tell you that your tastes will change during pregnancy, but I guess I never really fully understood the extent until the first time that I ate a pickle and drank a glass of orange juice at the same time. WHY?

- I’ve literally started to put sriracha on everything. Even things it doesn’t belong on, like bagels and cream cheese.

- My taste in TV shows has changed. All of a sudden I can’t bring myself to watch anything dark or scary, but I am ALL about anything medical.

- I’ve had the most bizarre and vivid dreams of my entire life. And when I say bizarre, I mean just plain WEIRD.

- I knew that pregnant women cry a lot. I didn’t realize that once you start crying, it’s literally impossible to make yourself stop! It’s like my tear ducts aren’t connected to my brain anymore, and 5 minutes later I’m STILL crying.

- One day you can put on your shoes. Literally the next day you can’t.

- I was not prepared the extent to which my boobs would grow. I have had to purchase new bras twice already during this pregnancy, and I’m getting ready to need new size #3.

- Why did no one warn me that sneezing and coughing unexpectedly are not a good idea? Do your kegels, people!

- Never in my life did I actually think I would wake up in the middle of the night and have to get up to eat something. It’s already happened a handful of times and we still have 2 months to go!

 

Mamas/Mamas-to-be: Anyone else experience pregnancy symptoms that they were not expecting?
