Galactic Chic with Scarf from RedBubble's "Art in Motion" Collection

You've likely heard of RedBubble before, it was one of the first, if not the first places for artists to sell their work on apparel, totes, wall art, etc. I hadn't visited their site recently until RedBubble reached out to me and brought their "Art in Motion" to my attention. What a long way they've come! Divided into specific collections like Retro Revival, New Romantic, and my pick Geek Nouveua the artwork from Art in Motion is featured on leggings, pencil skirts, clutch bags, tees, scarves and more. I was grateful to be asked to feature the Space Surfing II scarf from artist, nicebleed.


The material on this scarf is soft and lightweight making it the perfect addition to those Summer outfits when the sun goes down. This scarf is AMAZING in person. The colors are super vibrant!


At first glance this is just a galaxy print scarf but when unrolled it's a piece of art. Just look what a great size this is. I hate short scarves; I want one that is versatile and can be worn multiple ways.


You can get your own Space Surfing II scarf from RedBubble now! You can also check them on Instagram and Facebook. Thanks to RedBubble for sending me this scarf!

Thor inspired look with Mjolnir necklace from Level Up Crafts

Don't get me wrong, I love a good geek logo or symbol on some of my apparel but my favorite geek chic items are those that are subtle. Kudos to designers who create items that require a double take to figure out their origin fandom. My friend April over at Level Up Crafts gifted me this adorable Mjolnir inspired necklace or "Mew Mew" as Thor film fans may know it by.

The necklace is strung on a copper color chain which is appropriate. I think of brushed metals in muted tones for Viking jewelry. The hammer portion has specs of glitter in it and the handle is painted to mimic a real, leather wrapped hilt. Nice detailing April!

I took inspiration from the Icelandic wilderness and forests of Valhalla for this woodsy, earthy outfit.

Necklace: Level Up Crafts
Dress: LIFE Clothing 
Faux Fur Collar: H&M
Tights: Target 
Cuff: Wet Seal
Boots: Steve Madden 









April takes such care into each and every item she makes. She's shown me her work space and she's even made a bunch of custom molds for her resin pieces, the hammer included!. Her layering ability and attention to detail is superb. You know you're getting a thoughtfully, well-made piece when purchasing. Make sure to like Level Up Crafts on Facebook and Etsy. Thanks April!
