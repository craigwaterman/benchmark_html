Advocare breastfeeding 24 day challenge



I did a modified Advocare 24 day challenge for breastfeeding moms last January.  I took Probiotic Restore, Fiber Drink, Meal Replacement Shakes, Spark, and Catalyst.  I lost 10 pounds that month, which for me, was a really strong month, as most of the time I would loose 1-2 pounds a week.  And two inches in my waist (importnant after having a baby!)  I have done a normal Advocare 24 day challenge before and normally loose 6-8 pounds.  
Advocare is expensive, but it has really worked for me.  It helps me stay focused on keeping healthy.  Before Advocare I would diet for a week or two and then give up.  With Advocare, I was able to stick with it for the long haul.  There are many different great diets that our there that work for different people. Advocare is just what worked for me.
