Comparison of breast pump type

  • Exploring the World of Breast Pumps (S Knorr, 2007)
  • Recommending a Breast Pump (Lactation Education Resources, 2009)
  • Most mothers obtain more milk in less time when they use a hospital-grade, double, electric RENTAL breast pump until all multiple-birth infants can latch deeply/comfortably, suckle actively (and swallows are heard) 8 or more times a day, and produce the appropriate number of wet and stool diapers for adequate weight gain.

Parents of multiples may find snug swaddling helps some babies calm or sleep on their backs (supine) for longer periods of time. However, if not done properly, snug swaddling has also been associated with certain physical problems and may contribute to less safe sleep conditions. Snug swaddling should not be used to lengthen time between babies’ feedings to develop a “schedule” or in place of needed cuddling!
