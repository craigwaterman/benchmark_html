YANGA BEAUTY GOODIES!!!


Hi Guys,
I'm BACK!! I haven't posted much this month because i have been so busy and honestly i haven't gotten any new makeup to review!Does that happen to anyone?Sometimes i  go for long periods without buying anything because i just don't want to. I know it should be "MY THING" to want to try out every single makeup product there is out there so i can review it for you guys but sometimes i'm just blehhh!!

A few weeks ago at the BEAUTY AFRICA conference i got to meet the sweetest lady ever. Her name is Jennifer Uloko and she is the brains behind Yanga Beauty!! We got talking and she sent me some makeup to review!! Oh you knowwww i was supper excited! I was sent over five items and i love all of them. Before i go into the products let me gist you a little info on the site just incase someone hasn't heard about it.
