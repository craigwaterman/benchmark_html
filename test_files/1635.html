Welcome!

The people of Blythefield Hills Baptist Church invite you to share in their passion for loving God and loving others. In the context of Spirit-led, authentic relationships you’ll be equipped and encouraged to live as a whole-hearted follower of Jesus Christ. Join us and discover the many ways families and individuals are experiencing true spirituality through faith in the God of the Bible.
