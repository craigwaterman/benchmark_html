Neiman’s Spring’13 Fashion Show Review

February 5, 2013 By msmd Leave a Comment

A few weeks ago I attended  Neiman’s Style Sizzle Show hosted by  accessories style director Marlene Montanez.

As a fashion stylist, there were specific items that stood out to me. Check out my favorite fashion finds below and learn how to incorporate them in your everyday life.

  •  Cobalt Blue handbags. Would be stunning paired with Black and White. Cobalt Blue worn tonally is another fresh look for spring.
  •   Bright bags and shoes in high voltage. This looks great with all black or all white.
  • High top sneakers are hot this spring. The wedge high top is a  great look if you want to keep things casual but still need a little lift like I do.
  • The Gladiator influence is also a statement for spring. Here we can incorporate the lucite trend as well.
  • Sunglasses are plastic and cat eye.
  • Face framing necklaces. Stack on your bangles in threes and fours. A great look to add pizzaz to the minimalist trend.
  • Orange is the new neutral. Orange paired with tan and beige looked clean and fresh.

 

Do you know where to start?
