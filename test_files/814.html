Tuesday, January 14, 2014

Barn Hill animal preserve

Today we received a special treat when the Barn Hill Preserve came out to show us several wild animals.  The kids loved it!  We learned some new facts about animals we were already familiar with, as well as learning about some new animals we had never seen before.  We saw things like porcupines, tortoises, baby kangaroos, burmese pythons, and more!
