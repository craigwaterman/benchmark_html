Cape Wrap

June 7, 2012 by Scarves Dot Net Long Rectangle Scarves, Master Knot List, Square Scarves, Wrap Scarves
STEP 1 Fold a square scarf so the ends meet and form a triangle.
STEP 2 Place a scarf over your shoulders so that the longest straight end is at the top of your back and two triangles corners meet in front.
STEP 3 Make a square knot with these two ends in front.
