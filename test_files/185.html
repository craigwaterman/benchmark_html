WiW: 1.30 - Mustard On My Face?

January 30, 2014

Well, almost. I wouldn't be too surprised because I am a messy eater!

I love mustard to mix with my navy, but I usually keep it far away from my face. But when I saw this dress and loved it, I wasn't too sure about wearing the "yellow bits" so close to my face!

What do you think?
