Lasagna Soup

29 Aug posted by Angelique

This soup will forever be a staple in our home; it’s hearty, full of flavor and so comforting on a cold day!

Ingredients:

1 pound Italian Sausage (Note: I’ve also used light ground turkey sausage)

1 large or 2 medium onions, diced

1 bundle carrots, diced

2 packages button mushrooms, sliced

2 Tbsp. garlic, minced

48 oz. Chicken Broth

1 28 oz. can Diced tomatoes (Note: You can use stewed tomatoes and/or fresh tomatoes as well)

1 lb Mafalda Pasta (Note: I’ve also used Campenelle Pasta)

2-3 cups fresh spinach, chopped

S&P

1 tsp. Italian Seasoning

1 tsp. Dried Basil

1 cup fresh mozzarella, diced/cubed

1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

4 tsp. sliced basil leaves

Directions:

1. Brown Sausage in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.

2. Add onion & carrot, s&p and saute 3 minutes.

3. Stir in mushrooms & garlic & saute another 3-4 minutes

4. Add Broth, Tomatoes, Italian Seasoning and Dried Basil then bring to a boil.

5.  Meanwhile, cook pasta noodles in separate pot and set aside.

6. Reduce soup heat and add the spinach.  Simmer about 10-15 min.

7. Serve over cooked pasta noodles and top with mozzarella (I like Romano) cheese & fresh chopped basil

Additional Tips

Double the recipe for dinner guests.

Triple the recipe and freeze a batch (without noodles).

Add more carrots & spinach for extra veggies.

Omit the sausage and use vegetable broth for a vegetarian soup.

Here’s my suggestion

TRY THIS RECIPE FIRST and next time, tweak it to your liking!

~~~

Enjoy!
