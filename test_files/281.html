I Can't Tell You How Bad This Smells

This is how we spent our "lazy" Sunday... ripping up old carpet.

 

And by "we" I mean Bert.  Bert ripped up the smelly, pet stained, baby blue carpet.  I stood by and held the camera.

Lo and behold, there was nice a Fir floor underneath. But...We have Fir in one part and Oak in another. And they aren't going the same direction.

As some of you know, we've done quite a bit of renovation work in the two years we've had our house.  Ripping out the carpet was our first priority but other projects got started and now we're finally getting to the floors!  This is the before picture of the kitchen.  YIKES!  Yes, that is NEON GREEN Formica! 

This is the after picture of our kitchen. Big changes!  

Our house was built in 1928 and remodeled in the 1980s, hence the baby blue everything. Paint, carpet..

We remodeled our bathroom too but that's another post. :)
