Are You In or Are You Out? Which throwback styles are back—and which need to go

http://www.retailmenot.com/blog/back-to-school-trends.html?SID=d1b9adf5-094d-4f39-adeb-c0eec7d6b2bf&ch=socia&sf40150800=1">

School is starting, summer is ending, and wardrobes are changing. But before you make a mad dash to the mall, study up on which styles are in, and which ones are getting tossed to the clearance racks.

Shake your groove thing, but instead of rocking full-on mega flares (you’re headed for the classroom, not Woodstock), keep the look polished with a figure-flattering medium-size flare in dark-rinse denim.

Classic Levi’s are always a sure bet, and JCPenney carries a variety of sizes. If you feel the need for volume but feel too hippie-dippie pulling off a flare, try a boot-cut style like this textured rinse-wash pair from The Limited. Pair with a low-heel bootie for a styling trick that makes your legs look miles long. Keep the rest of the look structured to keep the jean’s volume in check.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Levi’s 518 Bootcut Jeans ($36.99) from JCPenny | Best Deal: 15% off sitewide

Buy: 312 Bootcut Jeans ($55.96) from The Limited | Best Deal: 15% off for students or teachers

This season, leggings go minimal, so look for a pared-down pair in a neutral palate of black, navy or gray like Madewell’s workhorse basic.

Trust us. This is the piece you’ll be reaching for day in and day out, whether you wear the leggings with a casual T-shirt dress and pair of ballet flats or with a sharp blazer and printed skirt. If you still crave a bit of flair for your gams, opt for leggings with subtle details like this tonal herringbone pattern from Shopbop.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Knit Leggings ($34.50) from Madewell | Best Deal: 15% off for students and teachers

Buy: Dotted Herringbone Leggings ($97) from Shopbop | Best Deal: Free U.S. shipping sitewide

It’s easy to fall victim to the moto-jacket hype, so channel your inner MOD (model off duty) rather than Sandy in her “Greased Lightning” days.

This quilted faux-leather moto jacket from Nordstrom exudes an authentic polished-meets-edgy vibe, while this blush-hue Banana Republic zippered number offers a fresh take on the classic.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Quilted Faux Leather Moto Jacket ($98) from Nordstrom | Best Deal: Free shipping and free returns

Buy: Multi-Zip Moto Jacket ($165) from Banana Republic | Best Deal: 15% off with a Gap, Banana or Old Navy Card

Let’s all agree that this is a toss. Come first period, you don’t want to look like Stevie Nicks or a washed-up ’90s reality-TV star.

Instead, tap into that bohemian spirit with a cozy cardigan. Try throwing on Roxy’s chunky open-front variation over jeans and a drapey tee, or play the texture off a simple knit dress and tights. We’re also digging Aéropostale’s ikat cardigan interpretation, which is laced with metallic thread for a hint of subtle sparkle.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Karid Sweater ($79.50) from Roxy | Best Deal: 20% off $75 and free shipping

Buy: Ikat Shimmer Cascade Cardigan ($35) from Aéropostale | Best Deal: Up to $25 off

Ditch culottes. Once relegated to the sporting arena, the skort is making its star turn as a key seasonal-transition staple. Bare legs work for those Indian summer days, and once the leaves start turning, you can don a pair of opaque tights.

This mini skort from Lord & Taylor has a utilitarian vibe that will mix nicely with more feminine tops, and we’re crushing hard on LOFT’s rose jacquard style. The plum color practically screams fall, but the silky fabric keeps things easy-breezy.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Mini Utility Skort ($88) from Lord & Taylor | Best Deal: 20% off regular or sale items

Buy: Rose Jacquard Fluid Skort ($54.99) from LOFT | Best Deal: Extra 40% off sale items

Toss the high-neck ruffled blouse for a piece that has just a touch of Lady Mary.

This nipped-in-at-the-waist pin tuck blouse from Kohl’s would look smart with a pencil skirt or with skinny jeans and knee-high riding boots. Eye-catching details like the ones on this eyelash-lace-adorned shell make this Nordstrom find an anything-but-basic basic. Throw it on over a tailored miniskirt, or try it with a skort to score double fashion points.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Pintuck Challis Blouse (on sale for $24.99) from Kohl’s | Best Deal: 15% off $100 or more and free shipping on $75

Buy: Sierra Lace Trim Tank ($85) from Nordstrom | Best Deal: Up to 75% off sale items and free shipping

We haven’t seen a proliferation of statement-making tees since “Frankie Says Relax” circa 1983. We want to declare this a toss, but the trend is sticking around for at least another season.

Instead of wearing your feelings for pizza or coffee on your sleeve, try a timeless vintage concert tee promoting a classic band. Hint: Check the guys’ section at Target to find a fit that’s slightly oversize. The feel is more borrowed-from-the-boys than concert groupie. Or give the trend a nod with Forever 21’s on-point statement watch.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Men’s Ramones T-Shirt ($10) from Target | Best Deal: $5 off $50 on select items

Buy: Faux-Leather Statement Watch ($17.90) from Forever 21 | Best Deal: 15% off your order and free shipping over $50

Designers often look to the ’60s for inspiration, but this season is less Twiggy and more Cara Delevingne, so we vote this item a toss in favor of the romper.

To avoid looking like you literally rolled out of bed, choose something with structural details like sleeves, a collar or a button placket. Shy away from bold patterns and go for an overall print in a versatile black-and-white palette like this retro-inspired printed romper ($39.99) from H&M or this short-sleeved viscose number from it-Brit retailer ASOS that’s covered in a sweet hearts-meets-leopard-print pattern.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Strapless Jumpsuit ($39.99) from H&M | Best Deal: 20% off $50 or more

Buy: Brave Soul Heart Print Short Sleeve Romper ($18) from ASOS | Best Deal: RetailMeNot exclusive! Students get 20% off their first order.

Yes, our trusty Dr. Martens saw us through our “Smells Like Teen Spirit” days, but we’ve grown up and fallen for a more sophisticated shoe: the ankle boot.

Try a Chelsea boot. The low heel and sleek silhouette make it both stylish and school-appropriate. This shoe goes with everything from skinny jeans to dresses!

The Zappos-exclusive chestnut leather bootie is destined to become our go-to pair. If you’re still lusting after a little edge, look for motorcycle-inspired finishes like Aldo’s ornamental black leather bootie. A block heel and round toe keep the look more honor roll student than slacker.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Steve Madden Exclusive Neonee ($109.95) from Zappos | Best Deal: Free shipping and free returns

Buy: Arielle ($125) from Aldo | Best Deal: 15% off your next purchase when you sign up for Aldo newsletters

There’s a reason why Chuck Taylors and the Adidas Stan Smith have enduring appeal: They have cool cachet, go with literally everything and just get better with age.

But there’s always room for improvement. Check out Converse’s updated Chuck Taylor All Star II. It retains its iconic look but has been retooled for comfort and durability.

If the Stan Smith tennis look isn’t for you, or you don’t have the patience to keep that white leather clean, Foot Locker has got a killer printed version that will give your fall basics an instant style update.

Editor’s Tips:

Buy: Chuck Taylor All Star II ($75) from Converse | Best Deal: Free ground shipping

Buy: Adidas Originals Stan Smith ($74.99) from Foot Locker | Best Deal: 10% off $50 or more
