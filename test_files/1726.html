Staging – Room by Room

11 May

I dream about staging! I love to think about how I would change spaces to make them more appealing! I tend to nest or stage my own home all the time. I move things around, de-clutter, organize! I know that this does not sound appealing to most, but to me it is heaven!

Staging whether it is to sell your home or just for your own pleasure does not have to be expensive or difficult.

You may find that you have things around your home that can stage/decorate even the smallest of spaces.

The laundry rooms is often an area that is overlooked when it comes to decor, but there is no reason to ignore this room that some of us spend so much time in!

This is a simple stage fresh white towels, neatly folded and two apothecary jars with detergent.
