Poetry / Thursday Thoughts On Love

Favorite Things

July 30, 2015 Rebecca Jackson2 Comments

Hi there,

For this “Thursday Thought on Love”, I’m sharing a lovey dovey poem about my husband. This particular poem plays with the line “these are a few of my favorite things” from that song in Sound of Music, when I was writing it I had the song playing in my head so I decided to go with it.

 Favorite Things

I hope you enjoy my little thought on love this Thursday morning. :)

Rings between pages, Hands against my waist, Lips across my forehead

These are a few of my favorite things

Eyes that follow me kindly, always gently

Love that picks me up, stands me straight, and refuses to let me fall

A warrior heart that vows to protect me

These are a few of my favorite things

Knees that fall to the ground when he prays

Long drives through downtown, you let me choose the songs

Park swings, the sweetest laughter a girl will ever hear, warm arms that always open for me

These are a few of my favorite things

I was afraid I felt too much to write it,

I was afraid no one else would understand it

We’re young but we’re on fire, burn down any doubts, burn down any fears

We’re young, and mapping out all our futures, we’ll take ‘em on together

Cause I’d rather a love that is willing to survive anything, than a love too afraid to handle the big stuff

Cause our love is the big stuff

You are my absolute most favorite thing

Poems in a binder, elevators for kisses, parking lot apologies,

Every moment with you is my new favorite thing
