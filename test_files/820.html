BUMPDATE: 38 Weeks

BUMPDATE: Our baby is the size of a bunch of a Watermelon!

Best moment this week? Seeing Cam get soooo excited (he begs her to come already!) I am getting a little sad all this fun of being pregnant is going to be over. I love feeling her move in my belly! Girls camp was a blast! I am glad I said yes to “camp director” even though I was to be 38 weeks pregnant! ha! And our AMAZING families helped us move in on Monday night, all in 3 hours–we are so grateful! Now onto organizing!
Movement? Yes!
Showing? Yep! Still feeling like she’s really high though
Food Cravings? —
Sickness/Pain? A few stomach aches!
What I’m looking forward to: Getting our place fully organized and spending a fun evening/day this weekend with Cam as our final Hoo-ra, just the two of us!
