Style Defined: Chambray Sashay!

Follow @kpsittingpretty

Oh la la Chambray!  Chambray has been making a major debut this spring and I've been seeing it everywhere.  It's a type of fabric that has actually been around for sometime, it's just gotten an uber chic make over and now everyone wants a piece of Chambray!  It gives off the look of a nice denim, but is often much lighter and softer.  Chambray can be used as an accent in an outfit, or you can go all the way and wear all Chambray!  I personally like it used as an accent or paired with a pop of color or print.  Here are some of my favorite Chambray pieces.
