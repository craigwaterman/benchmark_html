Living Room Tour

For a while now, I have been meaning to take new photos of our living room. This is really the hub of our house. We aren't the family that gathers sweetly around the kitchen table. Most nights, we gather in the living room for our dinner, and some kind of unfinished project is usually covering our kitchen table. This is the room where we watch movies together. Where someone throws a temper tantrum after losing a board game. Where two loads of laundry are folded on the couch daily. This is the room where life happens. And although the toys got picked up (and thrown onto the playroom floor) and the room got vacuumed, I decided to keep the laundry piles on the couch. One day I won't be doing laundry for six people, and although it's not my favorite thing to do, I know I'll miss having itty bitty baby clothes around soon.

A quiet house before Summer vacation starts. Only a toddler and a newborn at home with us for the next week. My favorite Fire Roasted Mallow candle burning from Alki Candle Company. Sunshine pouring in from the playroom.

This is home.
