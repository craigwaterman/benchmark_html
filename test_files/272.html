5 Modern Ways to Style The Midi Skirt

5 Modern Ways to Style The Midi Skirt

By Sarah Kendric of New York Love

To quote the Studio 15 website, “the elegance and femininity of midi skirts will keep them in the spotlight for a long time to come.” Midi skirts have been a part of fashion’s history for many decades. They’re classic and effortless, making them the perfect addition to your wardrobe (in any season, I might add!).

 

The perfect buy for midi skirt lovers and newcomers alike is the gorgeous black net midi skirt available right here at Studio 15!

This midi skirt is so fresh and modern, plus there are a million great ways to wear it! Let’s look at five modern ways to wear this midi.

1. With a cute top and sneakers

Wearing sneakers (especially Adidas and Nike) with high-fashion outfits has suddenly become very, very trendy. The combination of sneakers and a midi has the perfect I’m going on an oh-so-chic picnic vibe, and it’s so fun and youthful.

 

2. With a white tee and a big statement necklace

Since this midi is all black, there’s plenty of room left over to introduce color and play around with statement jewelry. A bright, bold necklace adds just the right touch, and it will stand out against your favorite white tee (which you can loosely tuck into the skirt). Here are a few examples of statement necklaces that would look great!

 

3. All dressed up

Headed out to somewhere fancy? This midi skirt still has your back. Pair it with your nicest blouse, or turn it into a ‘dress’ by wearing it with a gorgeous black bodysuit. I love the ones from Cici Hot and Missguided. Add your favorite pair of heels, a cute clutch, and head out!

 

4. With a graphic tee and denim

A soft t-shirt featuring your favorite band, brand, or otherwise is perfect tucked into this midi. As colder months and days slowly approach, throw on a light wash denim jacket. This look goes well with sneakers and some boots. Emily Geraghty of That Cheap Bitch totally owns this look, which she styles with a leather midi instead.

 

5. With a statement clutch

A great bag makes everything better. Fortunately, not only does a statement clutch look ah-mazing with this particular midi, it also goes rather nicely with every other outfit mentioned above! Studio 15’s black midi skirt goes with just about every design, pattern, and print of clutch ever. This skirt’s beauty is in it’s versatility. Here are some great statement clutches that would make this skirt pop, all from Anthropologie.
