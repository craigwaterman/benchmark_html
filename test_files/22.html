Essential Oils

Esther Ruth | December 11, 2014 | Health | 2 Comments

I started working at a Post Office as janitor with a friend of mine. Just days into it my friend and I started getting horrible headaches. My friend used Essential Oils and claimed they were amazing and worked great for relieving her headaches. Being the skeptic I am, I laughed to myself and didn’t get any oils. But it eventually got very annoying that I kept getting headaches. (This was a 5 days a week for a couple hours in the morning job, so you can understand the annoyance. )
So…I decided to take the crazy dive and get some peppermint oil for my headaches. Guess what. It worked!!!! I didn’t even get another headache for a while month after started to use peppermint essential oil!
So now I am “hooked”! They are amazing!

Recently I have been applying essential oils to my Dad’s back for his severe back pain. He is a skeptic too. (Like father like daughter? heehee!) The oils gave Daddy relief for an hour or two, which for him is awesome….big time! ðŸ˜€

I mainly use Young Living Essential Oils on my skin, orally, and for diffusing. For cleaning I use either Young Living or Plant Therapy.
Plant Therapy is not as “pure”, but still natural and are cheaper. For cleaning I am less worried about them being pure, than if I use them orally, topically, or diffusing.
What company you use it up to you, but I personally absolutely and a Young Living fan!

So, maybe you are like us- Skeptics.
Just give ’em a try and see if they will help you in our life like they have for me and my family!
