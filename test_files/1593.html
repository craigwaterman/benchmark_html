Changing the Soil in Your Containers

April 9, 2014SoilShannon

A couple posts back I admitted I don’t change the soil in my containers every year. Instead I just top them up with compost and carry on. There are, however, exceptions. We have an assortment of containers in front of our store. Over the winter they become a depository for cigarette butts, pop and cold coffee along with all those add-ins of cream, sugar, etc. The one year I didn’t change the soil the plants looked terrible. No doubt! So if your containers have public access or you have friends who see your containers as convenient places to put out cigarettes or as a sink hole for an assortment of unwanted beverages, then you too will need to change the soil every year. Or change friends!
