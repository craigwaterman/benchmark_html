Creative, innovative and instinctively stylish, Rundholz latest collection has fabulous shapes, premium quality fabrics and beautiful, naturalistic colours. Rundholz use specialist dye effects to create gorgeous soft tones and natural shades. The colour palette for the Spring/Summer 2015 collection was inspired by marine life; Tang (German for seaweed) is a dark grey with green undertone, Plankton is a a silver grey with pale green undertone and Alge is a moss green.

This funky tunic has a soft jersey body and a cotton skirt with an empire seam under the bust and inset side pockets. In an oversized shape with a wide scoop neckline and long sleeves.
