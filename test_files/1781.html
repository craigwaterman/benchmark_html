Western Bedding, Southwestern Bedding, Curtains and Home Decor

See Las Cruces Bedding in Southwestern Style

Our Western Bedding and Southwestern Bedding Sets are made with the finest fabrics adorned with decorative conchos, fringe and leather piping. With added touches of lone stars, horses, moons, cactus, genuine leather trims, you can create the perfect rustic western bedroom. The Mustang Canyon bedding is perfect for horse lovers with motifs of running Mustang horses. The washable polyester wool blended fabrics have bold colors of blue, hunter green and brown. The El Dorado Southwestern bedding group has beautiful chestnut brown bed shams and leather piping down the sides of the gorgeous tapestry fabric. The Southwestern Yellowstone bedding has beautiful crimson red and teal colors with rustic deerskin leather bed shams and pillows.
