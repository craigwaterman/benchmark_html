Hey there! It’s another Friday, that means it’s time for another party.

We’re kind of in a holding pattern here at my house, waiting to hear back from Immigration on the status of our visa request. I’m not expecting any trouble, but it’s a bit nail-biting, all the same. My husband and kids are as eager as I am to get our new life started in the US!

Before we get to the linky, I wanted to share this really cool resource with you, called Creative Market.  They are an site that provides all kinds of really cool digital design stuff – like fonts, icons, clip art, stock photos, Photoshop brushes – and so much more. The cool thing is that every week, they give away free stuff to introduce people to what they do and to showcase artists.

It’s a fantastic place to get check out and get some like photos for quotes or new fonts that are different from what everyone else is using on Picmonkey or Canva. You really ought to check it out!

And now – to the party!

 Welcome to the Pretty Pintastic Party where all posts are pinned; features pinned by all!

Important:  Once again this week, we had to have to pass over a few posts that we thought would make great features…. but when we checked to see if the bloggers had pinned the features, they hadn’t, so we had to choose different features (we check not only the repins, but also pins from that blogger’s feed). 

Since repinning is the ONLY rule at our party, we will ONLY choose features from those who repin.

Thanks for your understanding, and please know that we really appreciate those of you who DO repin!
