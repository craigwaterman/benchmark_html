We help relieve your chronic pain with pain treatments and pain management. Are you or someone you know living with chronic pain?

Have they tried all of the usual treatments and found no relief? At Kalamazoo Anesthesiology Pain Consultants, our goal is to provide the highest standard of quality care, using the most technologically advanced treatments available today, to alleviate suffering.

Kalamazoo Anesthesiology Pain Consultants specializes in the diagnosis and management of chronic pain. We use a multidisciplinary approach to help our patients take an active role in managing their pain and regaining control of their life.  Our team of specialists work with each other - and with the patient - to determine the cause of the pain and develop a personalized treatment plan. These programs are focused on the total person, not just the pain.  At KAPC, your treatment plan will be tailored to your specific needs, circumstances, and preferences.

Our physicians have been helping patients for over 20 years.
