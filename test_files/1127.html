Welcome


Welcome to the website of the Greater Boston Chapter of ISCEBS!

 

We are excited about the opportunity to connect with our members and guests about the Chapter, our educational programs and calendar of events, and the International Society of Certified Employee Benefit Specialists (ISCEBS). 

 

If you are a CEBS designee or student and a member of the chapter, we thank you for your support. If you are not yet a member but are eligible, we invite you to join. Please take a look around, and be sure to add the site to your browser bookmarks for easy access. To get started, you may wish to visit the links below.

    • Join our group on
    • Learn about the Scholarship Program for CEBS students.
    • Review the ISCEBS/IFEBP survey results.
    • Find out about the Continuing Education Exams.
    • See our upcoming events on the Calendar of Events.

We would love to hear from you.  If you have any comments, questions, or would like to get involved in the Chapter, please let us know.  Our goal is to make the Chapter as effective as possible for you by delivering timely and robust educational Programs and opportunites to network wtih your fellow Benefits professionals.

 

Thank you,  

Kevin J. Love, CEBS, SPHR
