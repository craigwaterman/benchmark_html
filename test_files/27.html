Making your wedding stationery as unique as your day

Welcome to The Little Touches, where we lovingly produce handcrafted wedding stationery, including invitations, save the dates, table plans and so much more. We have a great love of paper and use only the finest quality materials. Based in Kent we specialise in creating unique stationery and paper goods all with the little touches that your special day deserves. Your wedding is unique to you, so all of our items can be uniquely tailor-made to match your special day.
