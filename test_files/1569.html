Eyes & Brows

Eyelid lift or blepharoplasty surgery can be performed on the upper and/or lower eyelids to create a more youthful and rested appearance. Upper blepharoplasty is performed through an incision in the upper eyelid that is disguised in the natural eyelid crease and once healed is often imperceptible. The approach for lower eyelids blepharoplasty depends on the patent's age and degree of skin laxity - external lower blepharoplasty is performed through a fine incision just below the lash line, while the internal lower blepharoplasty approach is done through an incision inside the lower lid. Brows that sit too low can be repositioned to a more elegant or youthful position. Find out more about eyelid and brow rejuvenation techniques. + More

Ears

Patients consider ear surgery when their ears sit too far away from their head, or their earlobes are large or stretched. Otoplasty surgery - also known as ear pinning (although modern techniques don't actually pin your ears) - is a cosmetic surgery procedure to bring the ears closer to the head so they are less obvious. The results look natural and there is a very high satisfaction rates. When the earlobes are stretched or elongated, they become the focus of attention. Ear lobes can be long naturally, or become stretched by heavy earrings or large piercings. Find out more about restoring a more natural looking size and shape to your ears or earlobes.. + More

Non Surgical Facial Rejuvenation & Reshaping

Many non surgical options are available for rejuvenating or reshaping the face. Neurotoxins, dermal fillers and skin resurfacing can be used on their own or in combination to achieve the patient's goals. These non surgical treatments are often combined with facial surgery to achieve the best results, although patients who prefer not to have surgery have many options as well. + More
