Quick Lunches for Those on the Run

Posted in Food, Travel on the 2nd February, 2016 by justin

Stepping out for lunch on a busy day can be quite a task for those on the run. You are always stuck with a dilemma to skip or choose something greasy served at fast food joints. Coming to your aid are these four great quick serving restaurants that offer the best weekday lunch deals served without keeping you waiting. What’s more the prices listed on the menu are all inclusive erasing the confusion of added taxes.

1. Assembly coffee: The ‘third wave coffee movement’ is placed between Mr. Prata and The Wine Company on Evans Road. The coffee beans are procured from the local roasters to meet the growing coffee demands. While there is a choice of set weekday brunches, the a la carte choices are quite impressive. The service is nifty and the Salted Caramel Waffles served here is mouthwatering.

2. Anthesis Boulangerie, Bistro & Cafe: The in-house master chief baker, serves freshly baked bread to make the perfect sandwiches. The brunch special includes toasts and eggs which is on till late in the afternoon. If you’ve got an early morning and crave for breakfast at mid-day, Anthesis Boulangerie is the place to go. The artisan tea served here is imported from the Art of Tea, Los Angeles. Apart from tea and coffee, the bistro also serves an impressive variety of craft beers. All this set in the scenic location by the river. The place is open throughout the day leaving you the choice to visit at odd hours. If the food is good, the presentation is even better.

3. Department of Caffeine: Located in Duxton Road, the Department Caffeine is true to its name serving the best imported coffee beans from Australian roaster Five Senses. The staff at DOC takes their task of maintaining standards in terms of coffee and food superior throughout the year. The taste is consistent every time you walk in and order your favorite meal. The menu on weekdays offers comfort food at the best prices.

4. Hatter Street Bakehouse & Cafe: A venture from a Former teacher turned chef trained at Bo Innovation, the ambiance and the dishes from the Hatter Street Bakehouse looks like it stepped out of Alice in Wonderland. The pastry menu is constantly reinvented all thanks to the enthusiastic and innovative baker. The signature must try dishes here are the Pandan Ice-cream topped with Gula Melake and the Whoa’ffles. The drive to Kovan seems worth it when you get a taste of the food served here.

Discover The Benefits Of Chin Fillers

Posted in Uncategorized on the 27th January, 2016 by justin

Many people seek the help of a medical professional to help enhance the size of the chin or give it a new shape. The use of injectable fillers to alter this area of the face can provide balance and improve physical appearance. One of the most popular chin fillers on the market today is Radiesse.

Radiesse is made of calcium hydroxylapatite, a mineral that occurs naturally in the teeth and bones. This patented filler is a water-based gel that contains tiny miscrospheres of calcium hydroxylapatite. This mineral is used or various medical applications, including dentistry, urology, maxillofacial surgery and reconstructive surgery. In addition to adding instant volume to soft tissue, the filler is the catalyst for neocolagenesis, which is the natural production of collagen. Over time, the body absorbs the gel and metabolizes it safely.

When compared to a filler made with hyaluronic acid, Radiesse is able to provide better volume enhancing and its thicker consistency allows the medical professional to create better facial contours. The results can last up to 12 months or more.

Implant surgery provides longer lasting results than Radiesse, but surgical patients are always at risk of suffering a variety of negative side effects. Minor side effects include bleeding, swelling, and implant movement. Serious complications can include numbness, damage to teeth, blood clots, and infection. If the patient is put under general anesthesia, there is a risk of an adverse reaction to the medication.

Most people report minimal discomfort during injections. Medical professionals use extremely fine needles and a topical anesthetic, so most patients feel nothing more than a small prick. Some itchiness, tenderness, redness, and swelling are common in and around the treated area immediately following treatment. For most people, the skin will return to normal within a few days.

Results are noticeable to most patients after the first treatment. One of the biggest advantages to using Radiesse is that this filler continues to deliver results long after treatments are discontinued. This is because it stimulates the body’s natural production of collagen. With results lasting a minimum of 12 months in most cases, patients require less frequent follow-up visits for maintenance.

Radiesse is safe for use in most patients. Experts warn against its use by pregnant women or women who are breastfeeding. Other individuals who should avoid this procedure include people who are allergic to gram positive bacteria, those who suffer with bleeding disorders, individuals with a history of developing keloid scars, and anyone with an allergic reaction requiring treatment in a hospital.

A Rainy Day Must

Posted in Food on the 25th January, 2016 by justin

Nothing is as comforting as a steamboat on a rainy day. If you are wondering where to go, here is a list of the top Singapore’s steamboat restaurants for that stay indoor day.

1. Crystal Jade Steamboat Kitchen: An unscathed family favorite, Crystal Jade Steamboat Kitchen stands strong against other traditional Chinese fares. The outlet serves mouthwatering dim sums. Order for a couple rounds as an ideal side for the soupy goodness. You have the freedom to choose from the six varieties of soup bases to order from: spicy mala, tomato, pork’s bone, miso, curry and superior before you move to the ingredients.

2. The Mushroom Pot: Every mushroom lover’s paradise, at Mushroom Pot you are offered a choice of 20 varieties. The simple ingredients like beef slices, handmade fish balls and fish slices offer the warmth of a home cooked meal.

3. New Udon Mookata: The mookata is on a whole new level. While the meat grills slowly over the metal skillet placed in the center, the juices that ooze out are collected in a basin and then added as extra flavoring into the soup. What more could you ask for? Isn’t it the perfect blend of a barbeque and a steamboat?

4. Coca Restaurant: The old timer which has been around the block since 1957 is still kickin’. A normal slicked of cooked meat is transformed into a yummy bite when you dip it in the homemade chilli sauce. Although there are three branches, it is wiser to book a table in advance for the place is always crowded.

5. San Laksa Steamboat: The answer to every laksa steamboat lover, San Laksa Steamboat has mastered the art of laksa. If your companion likes chicken broth, you could get that to for the pot is structured in a way to hold dual types of soup.

6. Whampoa Keng Fishhead Steamboat: Famous for the fishhead steamboat, this place is a favorite dining joint for the locals. Been around for more than two decades, Whampoa Keng Steamboat has only grown from strength to strength. The freshly sliced bits of fish amid bite sized cabbage and seaweed is the main star. The prawn rolls, braised pig trotter and prawn paste chicken wings are worth trying too.

7. The Magic of Chongqing Hot Pot: The three variations of spice offered here is what puts it on the list for must try Singapore’s steamboats on a rainy day. There is also a buffet served for those who want to sample all the dishes.

Getting Around Singapore

Posted in Travel on the 11th December, 2015 by justin

If you are in the country, you are sure to want to see the place, not just stay inside your boutique hotel in Singapore. In spite of being a small country, it is by and large a sizeable city. It is about 50 Km (31mi) wide and 26 Km (16mi) north to south. An entire country is packed within this island state and there is a lot to see. The best way to get around any city by default is on foot, simply because you can take your time to soak in everything you see. Discovering little shops and stopping for food any place you wish. Here are alternates that are bound to work in case you need cover a lot of ground.

MRT (Mass Rapid Transit)

Singapore has a quick and efficient subway system that is the cheapest and fastest way around the city. You can hop on to a train at any one of the colour coded lines; red, purple, green, yellow and blue, five in all. It runs mostly underground, but comes up in some parts. The whole city is well covered and you are unlikely to be in any part of the country where you are not a short walk away from a subway line. The trains come in every seven to five minutes depending on the time of day.

Taxi

While it is possible to pick up a taxi anywhere, including at the doorstep of the boutique hotel in Singapore you are staying at, taxis are technically allowed to pick up and drop passengers only at designated taxi stands. Taxi rates vary through the day and can get quite expensive if you are not familiar with the complex system. They are, however metered and payment by credit card is allowed.

Driving/ rentals

Driving in Singapore is quite an experience. Singapore drives on the left side and their roads are nothing short of immaculate, but the driving experience can be testing. If you are coming in from a country where driving within lanes, using indicators, tailgating, aggressive driving, lane changing and other forms of road etiquette is expected, then Singapore will require you to drive on the defensive side, with your road rage in check.

Apart from these, the bus services in Singapore is also worth mentioning. The services are clean, impeccably maintained and are punctual, with regular services running through the night and covering the island thoroughly.

Discover Team Building Workshop

Posted in Education, Teamwork on the 11th December, 2015 by justin

Everyone thinks that they want to be successful. In order to become successful, you should first take an honest look at your own behavior. To be effective both in your personal life and professional life, you need to learn how to behave in a way that makes other people want to work with you. Your position within a group may go back and forth between leading and following, and real leaders understand the importance of both positions. Some team building in Singapore workshops can help you acquire these skills and more.

The ability to identify strengths in others is a good place to start. This is frequently not obvious, as real strengths can be hidden. Observe the behavior of co workers. Are they patient, are they generally in a good mood, are they considerate of others, these are some personality traits that are easily observed and can indicate strengths and weaknesses. When you are working with people, it is always better to build from a place of strength rather than weakness.

If everyone in life always had the same or non conflicting agendas, there would be no conflict. The challenge to team building is to motivate members to align their agenda with the group agenda. This is the first step towards success. If this key ingredient is missing, counter productive behavior and underlying resentments can become a barriers to successful achievement of the group objectives.

Group synergy can develop alignment of individual agendas, or alignment of agendas can develop group synergy. It does not matter which comes first. In fact, they may even occur simultaneously. The end result is that individuals learn to work as one unit and the performance of the group improves. Everything the individual does is working toward the same agenda. When the agenda is accomplished, each individual in the group has a sense of purpose and success.

Strategic Team Sessions take group members off site where they take the time to know and understand each other. The better individuals understand each other, the better the team will perform. Group dynamics are explored at these sessions as people come to realize that they react differently to different people. Realizing this about yourself can lead to deeper self knowledge. With self knowledge comes the ability to perform more effectively.

Other topics, such as the power of positive conflict and planning for group success by charting new behaviors are also covered in the team building workshop. The program will teach attendees how to communicate clearly both when giving and receiving information. People as individuals are constantly subject to change, and managing a group is an ongoing process. This workshop will improve your management skills.

Reasons for your Child to Attend Preschool

Posted in Education on the 21st November, 2015 by justin

Preschool has become a very important component in a child’s education these days. It is almost essential for a child to be sent to preschool. With the preschools now charging astronomical fees, it might make a parent wonder whether preschool is worth the trouble and money. Well preschool is definitely worth it; moreover it’s very important too.

Growth

Preschool happens to be many children’s first experience of being with new people as well as in an organized environment. It’s an opportunity for them to learn, have fun, share and care. It can also be seen as a preparation for school. This new environment will definitely develop the child; it’ll start the process of growth in a child not just physically but also mentally. Being away from family with other kids of same age will prepare children to independent. Preschools in Singapore give special attention to the welcoming environment to be provided to students.

Kindergarten

Preschool teaches you as well as prepares the child for kindergarten. The kindergarten is the first step to academics; children cannot take a sudden jump without learning the basics of academics like alphabets, and words etc., hence slowly starting them off with preschool is the right and subtle way. Preschools offer different programs for different aspects of the child; it is wise to do a research about pre schools in your locality before sending your child. In Singapore the quality of preschools are very high, hence preschool in Singapore is taken seriously. Since preschools have very highly qualified staff who understands children sending children to preschool is safer.

Social and emotional development

Preschool is important for the child’s development socially and emotionally. To actually benefit and learn from preschool a child needs to be in trustful and happy environment. The quality staff and teachers achieve exactly this; they nurture warm relationship among children. Young children can learn social skills and emotional control in preschool. Young children are very delicate; they can be affected by the tiniest of things. Children can learn to take care of themselves as well as learn academic understanding too. These are all needed as foundations for the over all development of the child over time.

Dynamic environment

Preschool is a dynamic environment where the child is in a constant process of growth and learning. Preschool promotes language and cognitive skills in children. A child’s vocabulary undergoes a sizeable change between the age of 3 to 5 years, learning the language and words at this age is great for the child. They feel independent and free in preschool and they get to make choices like what activity they want to conduct. This offers children a direction and aim; we could say that preschool builds the future student in a meticulous manner even though it’s not apparent generally.

Home Remedies for Hyperhidrosis

Posted in Home Lifestyle on the 5th November, 2015 by justin

Hyperhidrosis is a troublesome disorder that is caused by excessive sweating in different parts of the body like armpits, hands, face, head and feet. Hyperhidrosis can lead to social embarrassment and also cause extreme physical discomfort to an individual. Though there are several hyperhidrosis treatments but for acute hyperhidrosis none of them offer a permanent solution to the problem.

Plus the side effects of the medications make people worried about their usage and advantage. So they use home remedies to manage the problem and maintain personal hygiene to avoid skin disorders that are related to excessive sweating. In addition to staying away from possible triggers, an individual suffering from hyperhidrosis must take extra precautions to avoid secondary infections

Types of hyperhidrosis:

Primary hyperhidrosis: This hyperhidrosis is usually caused due to minor defect in central nervous system or is inherited. This affects only certain parts of body and is generally restricted to face, head, hands, underarms and feet.

Secondary hyperhidrosis: This hyperhidrosis is not localized and affects all areas of the body. This is caused by several factors like medications, pregnancy, alcoholism, certain antibiotics or nutritional supplements and diseases like TB, diabetes, thyroid, hormonal imbalance, Parkinson’s, obesity, hypertension, arthritis etc.

Listed are few home remedies to reduce symptoms of hyperhidrosis

1. Potato slices
By placing some potato slices under arms immediately after shower can effectively prevent sweat glands from producing excess sweat. Even rubbing some fresh potato slices underarms right before stepping out of the shower can keep sweat glands under control and prevent excessive sweating to an extent.

2. Baking soda and cornstarch
Baking soda and cornstarch can effectively reduce hyperhidrosis. Apply some baking soda or cornstarch in the underarm or groin region after shaving, washing and drying these areas, leave it for 30 minutes and after that wash with cold water.

3. Tomato Juice
Tomato juice is often recommended as the best home remedy for hyperhidrosis treatment. Make juice of some tomatoes and have this juice twice every day in morning and in evening for extended relief from hyperhidrosis.

4. Honey
Mixture of honey and vinegar in warm water is also effective against hyperhidrosis. Concoction of 3 teaspoons of honey and 2 teaspoons of vinegar in 1 glass of warm water twice a day reduces hyperhidrosis in the upper body.

Along with these home remedies one should include foods rich in sodium and vitamin B in the diet. These are known to be effective sweat inhibitors. Drink at least 8 glass of water in a day to flush out toxins and impurities that could aggravate the sweat glands into producing more sweat.

Expanding Your Business Space With Awnings

Posted in Home Lifestyle on the 23rd October, 2015 by justin

Most businesses, especially ones that are direct customer service oriented like the food and beverage based ones can always do with a little extra space. Moving to a larger location, however may not be a choice and will end up costing more. Another risk with moving a store is that it may be the best of locations and there is little guarantee of regulars travelling a few more blocks just to get you. In situations like this, awnings can be used to great effect.

Awnings

Awnings from Singapore are the retractable temporary roofs that are often used in outdoor spaces to bring shade. They can be made of a wide range of materials including canvas, fabric, aluminium, and other plastic based sheeting. One of the biggest reasons for the popularity of awnings is that they are easy to set up and dismantle, they can be moved any place without much effort, and are not very expensive to get done. Well made high quality awnings can protect and provide cover from rain, hail, wind, and if needed, even snow. The concept of awnings is by no means a new one. They have been around since renaissance Rome and have seen a lot of transformation, but their utility still remains.

Savings

Awnings are hands down the cheapest way to extend the footprint of a commercial centre. It can be used either on the outside or in an unused terrace to create outdoor seating. If the time and climate is right, outdoor seating will be preferred by customers. There are also energy savings that are sort of a spillover caused by awnings. Using awnings over a large glass area, one that is common in stores, to make sure that there is no direct sunlight on them can save a business a good amount of money on heating and cooling over the year. Well placed awnings can reduce indoor temperatures by 9 to 12 degrees right away, saving as much as 25% in electricity bills yearly.

Marketing

A store with an awning always stands out, and is readily identifiable, it also presents business owners with unique opportunities for advertising, branding, and marketing. Awnings can be custom made to be printed in a certain way, made out materials that are modifiable or even have lighting, making it unique and much more visible that just signage.

If you are a business owner and would like to add immediate appeal to your store front, awnings just might be the thing.
