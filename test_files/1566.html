Exotic home in Bali

Exotic home in Bali, this home belongs to fashion designer Birgitte Raben Olrik.

A rich and tropical Balinese interior filled with all the aspects of Asian decorating, open plan living traditional natural materials and the detail of the craftsmanship.

I visited Bali a few years ago and I just fell in love with the place, the culture and the people are so lovely, the Balinese fabrics, furniture and ornaments, such amazing detail, just lovely.  I warn you go with a half empty suitcase; you will want to buy everything!
