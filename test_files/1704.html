    Where The Locals Go!

    Alaska’s best waterfront restaurant is the Hangar on the Wharf! Located in a historic seaplane aviation landmark, we serve up wild Alaskan seafood of all kinds, hand cut steaks, hearty buffalo burgers, and of course King Crab! Full-service bar with over 125 beers and 25 taps, and the best selection of locally brewed Alaskan beers. Come visit where the locals go! Look for the big blue building at the end of the docks in Downtown Juneau!
