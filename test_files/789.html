Mission


The mission of DRSHEALTH is to first bring healing to the individual; then family, and ultimately the community. We are committed to providing the best care for optimal health and wellness using non-toxic naturopathic modalities. We believe everyone has an innate healing potential and when given the right remedy and the right environment, healing will occur on all three levels; mind-body-spirit.

We believe in the sacred value of human life and that all people are created equal.

DRS2HEALTH core values are Joy in Service, Effective Stewardship, and Respect For All

We seek to Nurture the Spirit, provide Excellence in Service, Practice Empathy, Respect Individuality, Enable Freedom of Choice, Preserve dignity, Maintain Confidentiality.
