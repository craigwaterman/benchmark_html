Friday, March 25, 2005
good friday sermon

i went to wihu's church today for good friday sermon, the fairfield methodist church in tanjong pagar, sermon was in indonesian. edmond asked me to go to his church but at that point of time i had already promised nata, so had to decline. anyway, the rest of the post will be done in black ink because i respect the feelings of christian readers out there who may not share my opinion. if you are liberal-thinker and not afraid to hear a criticism or two about christian, i welcome you to just highlight the whole thing to read it.

stepped into the church like at 5 pm, we were late for almost half an hour i think. went to the balcony and have a seat. started with some prayers and then sing some songs. then the sermon started. i won't give you the details of what he said but just go to the main point that made me angry. this is like what he said, roughly.
what is love? no religion in the world can teach you the real meaning of love. muslim talks about many things but not once in the Koran do they mention love. what is the meaning of eating the bread and wine? there are 4 interpretation on this. catholic teach that the bread that they eat literally becomes the body of jesus christ after being blessed. LITERALLY his body that they are eating. LITERALLY!! the wine literally is his blood. this teaching had mislead many people for centuries. then martin luther said that the the body of jesus christ is together with the bread. calvin said that the bread symbolises the body of jesus christ. but actually, the real meaning is when we eat the bread, the holy spirit is upon us and we can feel the presence of God with us.
then he went on and on about interpretation of love from other religions. religions that he included were: Confucianism, Lao-Zi (taoism), Greek belief. then he went on
catholic teaching that they are eating the body of jesus when they eat the bread is like forcing jesus to die every week! nonsense!! this is a blasphemy (penghujatan)!! the liberals had interpreted God's words in ways that make everything a mess! nonsense!! we, the reformed christian, is trying to get as close as possible to the true teaching of God's words. agustinus were in a time when there was so many teachings and there were so many interpretation that he had to make comparison between many teachings and all.
there were more things that he said. but this is like what made me really angry. the moment he mention muslim, there was a crease between my eyebrows. i don't like preacher who likes to dis other religions. and then he went on to dis catholic and there was definitely a frown on my face, i was actually scowling and glaring at the stupid preacher and i felt blood rushing in my veins. i was definitely feeling very angry. i know i shouldn't feel angry, anger is something that God said we should not do and i know there is no point of staying and hearing the whole sermon if my heart is actually not there. but, like hermione while listening to umbridge's speech at the beginning of book 5 *if you read harry potter, you'd know what i mean*, i want to know what else did he got to say. so i stayed on and continue glaring and listening at the same time. the moment he finished and we were standing up to sing, i said to hany 'i'll wait for you all outisde' and i straight away walked out of the room, down the stairs, ignoring the stares that people gave me, out of the building, and found the nearest rubbish bin to throw away the pamhplet that was given earlier.

the nerve of that preacher! i felt like shouting to him! do you think that catholic people are as stupid as you are to take the bread that they eat as the literal meaning of jesus body?? do you think that we do not know the meaning of God's love? you said that john was the only person who was there when jesus died beside mary so he knows how much God's love is. if so, do you think that YOU will be able to know His love?? what the hell!!!
once outside i walked here and there trying to cool down and tried to calm myself down. getting angry on good friday is not a good thing to do. then i sms-ed yessichan and edmond and tommo, storming >.< geez!!
for all my 6 years in Tarakanita 3, i've never heard my pastor dis and jeer at other religion. after i left, there was times when i went to church *not catholic* and out of these rare times, i distinctly remember that 3 sermons were like that sermon that i had just now. not as much as dissing so many religions, but definitely emphasis that christianism is the best religion in the world. i know that not all christians are like that narrow-minded, but it still kept me away from going to churches coz i don't want to hear rubbish like that >.<

anyway, after that i tried to met tony coz he called me asking whether i want to meet him. and so i did. then later on met up again with the rest of them. the first thing that nata asked was 'daijoubu ga?' *are you okay* and marlin said 'that was a bit harsh, don't get offended yeah? me and nata missed our church too when we listen to that' i couldn't say anything coz i could still remember all the things that he said. later on my sis said 'i should have gone with you just now' and i let off some steam on her...
we went to eat dinner at fish and co at centrepoint. before that met nata's friend, a shing, in cineleisure. one word to describe him is: cool. he knows how to carry himself well. and i thought his smile is so sweet cue to his *gingsul* =D anyway, the pricing at fish and co is really waay beyond my budget, but i didn't want more problems of choosing another place so we just ate there.

ah yeah, yesterday i went to have a nice long chat with lola, sharing opinions and feelings and info about x. he was like in the same league as W*ING, the pro X but don't like X Japan. but the huge difference between W*ING and him is: W*ING hates Yoshiki while Lola adores him, just like i do ^__^ rare people, they, the pro taiji era... i don't have anything bad to say about X, but i do admits that i like X Japan more ^^

is officially released TODAY. a brief description on my day before i plunge full fledge into the my obsession, Yoshiki. how can i resist not posting... anyway, Bio exam just now, spent too much time on 1 essay question and neglected the other... may as well, coz i didn't have much to write about that other question, i can't remember how caffeine stimulates the heart and i can't remember the different features of myoglobin and haemoglobin *sigh* spent loads of time talking about forests and trees and fossil fuels, hope i can pass that section...
