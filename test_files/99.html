Outfit of the Week - Statement Skirt


This season is all about the statement skirt. There are some awesome designs out at the moment especially some like these from, Newlook, Topshop, and H&M. I've had this skirt for a while now and I got it from a friend of mine who was giving it away. I absolutely adore this skirt and I've loved wearing it this season.


Finished with black tights, a black tee and my well loved brown boots! I wore my green wax F&F coat to shield me from the awful rain we've had today! 


I also tried a new hairstyle which I love, a braided crown.

Lots of Love
