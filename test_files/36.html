California Girl Transplanted

January 8, 2015January 17, 2015Leave a comment

Yesterday I completed the 1st goal off my 30 before 30 list (to be published in the near future)–moving out-of-state.  And, boy, what a move this is.  I went from sunny San Diego to Maine.  Yearlong summers to negative 14 degree windchill.  I already miss my flip-flops.

This Cali girl will be moving to Maine in some months #❄️❄️❄️

A photo posted by Simone (@simonedionne) on Oct 24, 2014 at 11:44am PDT

Look at how excited I was when I found out I was moving ^^

I’ve wanted to get away from home since I was 17.  Everyone (read all Californians) thought I was crazy, many still do.  In some ways I agree (now that it’s currently 1 degree farenheit).

-14 windchill. BRUH

— Beyoncé Pad Thai (@simomone) January 8, 2015

Einstein once said that insanity is “doing the same thing over and over again and expecting different results” and I wanted different results.  I was in no way on the path to have poor results, but I was comfortable and that is the LAST thing I wanted for myself in my 20s.

“30 tho” is my search for a life that’s much more than palm trees, beaches, burritos, and sunshine.
