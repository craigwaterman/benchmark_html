Brooke's Mission

“Mom, how can I help sick kids now that I am better?” It was those very words, spoken from Brooke’s heart, which inspired a little girls dream and a passion to give back.

Brooke’s Cause is pain turned to purpose. You are never too young to make a difference. Through her creativity, talent, unbreakable spirit and a smile that lights up any room, it is Brooke’s hope that she can bring as much joy to others as they brought to her in such a fragile time.

She will use her paintings, bracelets and more to help raise money for Children’s Cancer Charities, Foundations and Special Events. A portion of all proceeds will help another little boy or girl who may have a story just like hers.

With each item handmade exclusively by Brooke, it is her mission to bring awareness to others, that despite your age or circumstance all things have a silver lining, you just have to open your heart to the wonderous possibilities.
