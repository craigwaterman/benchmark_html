    Kim Kardashian Baby Bump Update: It's Growing!

    by Hilton Hater at August 17, 2015 8:52 am. Comments

    Kim Kardashian is most definitely pregnant.

    We know this, of course, due to the latest Kim Kardashian nude photo that made its way around the Internet.

    But we also know it because the reality star just shared a new picture on Instagram.

    We're sorry to say the 34-year old is fully clothed this time around, but she's still showing off her growing baby bump, this time by standing alongside Roberto Cavalli Creative Director Peter Dundas.

    "Lunch for 3," Kim captioned the image, making a cute reference to her impending child.

    The occasional model and husband Kanye West are already parents to a two-year old named North West, of course.

    Before the year flips to 2016, they are expected to welcome a son into their famous immediate family.

    This means we'll be hearing plenty from Kim and company over the next several months, as they make plans to add another relative and then as they gush over him once he's here.

    In other words: this news anchor should maybe go live under a rock. It's gonna be a rough year ahead.
