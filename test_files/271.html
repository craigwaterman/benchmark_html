Event Details

Festival Events

During May 7-10, 2015, Red Cat Jazz Preservation Society, Inc. will host its 5th Annual Red Cat Jazz Festival at Moody Gardens Hotel and Spa. The theme of the festival is: “It’s Island Time,” Discovering New Territories. The 2015 festival will feature the following:

Four days of non-stop live jazz concerts featuring a variety of local and international jazz artists. Invited guests include Grammy winner Kirk Whalum, Ronnie Laws, Rick Braun, Norman Brown, Mindi Abair, Peter White, Hiroshima, Jazz in Pink, Mike Phillips and a host of others.

THURSDAY EVENTS

  • Opening night reception, Thursday, May 7, 2015 at 7pm. A “White Tie Gala” sponsored by Galveston County Daily News and Coast Magazine. Attendees will include sponsors, VIP guests and special invited guests such as city officials and members of the Galveston Chamber of Commerce. There will be an art exhibit and special recognition of sponsors. Entertainment will be provided by Yvonne Washington and national recording artist Dean James.

FRIDAY EVENTS

  • 2nd Annual Red Cat Jazz Preservation Society Celebrity Golf Classic hosted by Mike Phillips at the Moody Gardens Golf Course, Friday, May 8, 2015 at 9am. Tee off during the golf tournament at the Moody Gardens Golf Course with some of Houston’s most beloved entertainment and sports figures. The former Municipal Golf Course recently underwent a $16 million renovation creating a beautiful 18-hole seaside course through native wetland habitat.
  • Veteran’s Luncheon, Friday, May 8th 1-3PM at Moody Garden Hotel Spa and Convention Center. Come join your veterans and a special salute to service women. Celebrate the honor of those who have gone before, and celebrate today’s women veterans as they look toward the future. Red Cat Preservation Society is pleased to shine the spotlight on our Women Warriors and proud to support veteran services throughout the Houston area. The luncheon will include a wide range of source materials including photographs, letters, diaries, scrapbooks, military patches and insignias, uniforms, and posters as well as published works. Registration Required. Discount Tickets for women veterans and service women who attend the Luncheon.
  • VIP All White Attire Poolside Party, Friday, May 8, 2015 at 7pm. Attendees are encouraged to wear all white attire. National recording artist Tom Browne, “Mr. Jamaica Funk” himself, will be the featured artist. “The C.I.T.Y,” a well-known band that has toured with national recording group “MAZE” will perform. Guests will experience live entertainment by the poolside under the stars at the Moody Gardens Hotel and Spa. Light hors d’oeuvres will be served. Many local and national jazz artists will be in attendance allowing guests “meet and greet” opportunities. Guests that purchase tickets to the All White Attire Poolside Party will receive complimentary admission to the After Party.
  • After Party, Friday, May 8, 2015 at 10pm. The featured artists will be the recording group “SVK Vintage Band” featuring “Harold G and Mona.” Attendees will dance the night away to live R&B music with entertainment provided by The Singing DJ – Dana Jackson of Majic 102.1FM. Many more surprise national guest artists will be announced.

SATURDAY EVENTS

  • Red Cat for Pink, Women Empowerment Conference, Saturday, May 9, 2015 at 7:30am. Saturday morningRed Cat for Pink will hold a women’s empowerment event hosted by Jan Pink, founder of Wealthy Omnipotent Women, known as “W.O.W.” This Mother’s Day weekend event will be co-chaired by attorney Jolanda Jones and Sherry Stockwell. Women will be inspired, motivated, and empowered. Victoria Lowe, author of 10 Spiritual Principles of Successful Women, will take part in this event along with Dr. Jennie Bennett, founder of Reconstruction of a Survivor and a host of other guests. The event will begin with a morning workout lead by 4-Ever Fit by Yolanda followed by downtime to “Get Glammed” in the W.O.W. Lounge. The W.O.W. Lounge will have a Beauty Bar hosted by Lamik Cosmetics with make-up artists, celebrity hair stylists, massage therapists and much more. The conference will end with an 11am brunch featuring keynote speakers. Gina M. Spagnola, president of the Galveston Chamber of Commerce will welcome the ladies to Galveston Island. A portion of the proceeds from this event will be given to Reconstruction of a Survivor in honor of Red Cat for Pink.
  • Red Cat Jazz Preservation S.T.E.A.M. Scholarship Workshop, Saturday, May 9, 2015 at 10am. We focus on music, science, technology, engineering and math education together not only because the skills and knowledge in each discipline are essential for student success, but also because these fields are deeply intertwined in the real world and in how students learn most effectively. Xavier Samuels of O.B.A.P. will host the workshop.
  • Saturday Live Jazz Concerts, Saturday, May 9, 2015 at 12pm. Feature artists include BWB: Rick Braun, Kirk Whalum, Norman Brown, Mike Phillips, Peter White and Tizer.

SUNDAY EVENTS

  • Red Cat Jazz Legends Awards Breakfast, Sunday, May 10, 2015 at 10am. An intimate up-close and personal breakfast with festival headliners will share their personal stories about their love for jazz and the evolution of jazz in the art form. In special recognition, Ronnie Laws will receive “The Red Cat Jazz Legend Award” and several “Mothers of the Arts” will be honored. We strongly believe it would not be any legends if it were not for their mothers. The legend mothers will be honored during this Mother’s Day event.
  • Sunday Live Jazz Concerts, Sunday, May 10, 2015 at 12pm. Featured artists include Mindi Abair, Ronnie Laws, Hiroshima, and Jazz in Pink.
