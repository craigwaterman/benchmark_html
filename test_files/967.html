MA Special Needs Kids Learn Watersports

By linked source on July 30th, 2014

Photo/Cotting School

A program in Scituate, Massachusetts, has introduced dozens of children with special needs to watersports this summer, teaching them skills in windsurfing, Hawaiian outrigger canoeing, sailing and stand-up paddleboarding, according to an article in the Patriot Ledger.

The program is offered through AccesSport America and the Community of Resources for Special Education, a nonprofit foundation.
