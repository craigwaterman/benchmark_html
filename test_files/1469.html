About Me

Still Learning
My name is Jennifer & I write Blogging God & Me because God placed it in my heart to do so. I was 30 before I could say I truly loved the Lord & Jesus even though I had been saved since the age of 8. Ever since a spectacular morning when God met me in my van in 2003 I have been seeking Him & learning & loving & having a great time with Jesus. I enjoy learning more & more about Him and helping others who are saved but do not have a personal relationship come to have that closeness. I am a busy mom of 4. The oldest is 18 & the youngest is 3. I am funny & I like to fantasize about being a stand up comedian & a talk show host. So many opinions, so little time.
