Friends Place Adult Day Services…

proudly provides Dementia Care in the Greater Dallas Area

Friends Place Adult Day Services is dedicated to providing specialized daytime care and programs for individuals with Alzheimer’s or other dementias; which will enable them to maintain a life with dignity, meaning & purpose, while remaining as independent as possible.

Services and programs support the continued efforts and desires of family caregivers to care for loved ones at home … in their own community. Friends Place provides supportive Dementia Care in the Dallas area.

Meaningful activities, socialization opportunities with a wellness philosophy of care, reflect our mission and make it possible for those individuals with memory problems living in Dallas/North Texas to enjoy life …again.
