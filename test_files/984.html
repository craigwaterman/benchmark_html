Joe Alaskey

13,922pages on
this wiki
View source
  • History
Talk1
Joseph Francis "Joe" Alaskey III (April 17, 1952 – February 3, 2016) was an American stand-up comedian, actor, director, producer, writer, playwright, voice artist, and comedian, best known as one of Mel Blanc's successors as the voices of Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, Sylvester, Tweety, and other characters from Warner Bros. cartoons. He also voiced Plucky Duck and various characters in Tiny Toon Adventures from 1990-95. He was born in Troy, New York.
