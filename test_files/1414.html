[pixel heart.] [Freebie] A Valentine Heart For You :3

February 13, 2014 Leave a comment

[.I Bring You Love :3.]
I started picking up shape demos the other day. I just like to see how other people are messing up the mesh. I’m always thinking about making different shapes for different personalities. It’s a terrible idea because I’ve started to not delete demos. I have thousands of them. Very literally. x:
Anyway, I was watching The Last Unicorn the other day and I thought a Lady Amalthea shape might be cute. Unfortunately the SL mesh is totally not suited for making such a shape so she turned out to be a generic cutie.
She’s very similar to my regular shape just more compact with her eyes being a lot larger. They are huge. I look at her and I kind of don’t notice and then I switch to my regular shape or another shape and I am like “wow, those eyes are huge”. x: You will need well shaded eyes with large irises or she may look kind of noobie. x:
Luckily for you, I don’t really know how I feel about selling shapes and I don’t know if I can bring myself away from using my regular shape but I do really like her, so she’s up on the marketplace for free for the foreseeable future. :3

[Freebie] Something Cute, Something Sassy

December 15, 2013 Leave a comment

We aren’t very festive in my family and I find dressing for Christmas on Second Life to be fairly strange. I like to stand out a little and Christmas wear comes in a fairly limited selection of colours and styles. And I just don’t like red and green together in most cases. x: Holly and roses are pretty; Christmas elf outfits, not so much. x:

[.Something Cute.]

This top is actually a mesh dress from Vivid, I just liked it for this post. :3 Comes with a Lolas applier. You may want to wear skin coloured pasties is you’re not a Lolas wearer because it’s very low cut. x:

The skirt is a wearable demo of one of Candy Mountain‘s newest releases.

The crown is by Schadenfreude and available at this month’s Arcade event. Every piece is retexturable and comes with several options, you just touch it and select what you want from the menus, which I think is awesome for just 50L. I have resized and repositioned it to my personal preference.

And I guess it’s time to explain this hair. In case you have somehow missed it, Alice Project is holding their yearly advent calendar. Every day you can pick up one hair in natural colours with glittery blue streaks. It is available to everyone. After that day it goes onto the wall for 50L.

[.Something Sassy.]

This skirt is a the group gift version of the purple skirt from Candy Mountain. It does cost 75L to join the group but there are regular freebies that are always available including a fatpack of the eyes I am using for this post. :3

The leopard print bandeau is a group gift from Alyce. (It’s seperate from the sheer undertop.) Join the group and click the poster once a day to randomly be given one of seven HUD controlled, retexturable, mesh tops. You can find many cheap mesh fatpacks at this store in a whole host of colours. :3

The other part of Alice Project‘s advent calendar is only available to the group. If you are in the group, you can pick up the day’s hairstyle in gold and silver streaked versions. If you missed one of these, you can still get them from the chart on the wall. Zoom into the style you want and select the part that says “Silver/Gold”. They will be available throughout the month. It costs 750L to join but in recent months, Alice has been good about chaning the group only Midnight Manias regularly. They give out a free hairstyle with texture change HUD. You can even pick up a VIP HUD to boost your number of colour options.

[.With Love and Thanks.]
I don’t really like the textwalls but you’ve got to explain somethings sometimes. x:
I don’t know if I’ll be able to blog again this month so I guess this is it for the year. I hope you have a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year. :3 Thanks for reading and thanks to all the generous creators for their gifts and discounts. :3
