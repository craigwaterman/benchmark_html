Vintage Meets Modern

Vintage Meets Modern is a blog about tips and ideas on living well for less money. There will be posts about books, websites, lifestyle practices, fashion and DIY ideas, interior design, and so much more, all of which I have done and loved along my journey in life to get the most enjoyment for the least amount of money. I hope you enjoy learning along with me!

OOTD: Red & Blue & Blanket Scarf Review

Inspired by this pin.


Mine:

My blanket scarf arrived today. I really like it! The only downsides to this one as opposed to my first one from Ebay is that it is not as soft, nor does it have Zara tags. If I order any more I would go through Ebay rather than Amazon. But hey, this one was free w/gift card, so that's a plus. 

Top: thrifted 
Jeans: Dollar General 
Bag: thrifted 
Flats: Ebay
Scarf: Amazon 
Necklace: Flea market 
Earrings: F21
