My Christmas plans 

Posted on December 1, 2015 by Michelle

8

  
It’s Christmas!!!! In the blogging world everyone has gone crazy and is posting everyday of December, I on the other hand am not. This year I will be putting one post up a week, and maybe a blog once a week. I will be keeping my Tuesday post but my Monday and Wednesday posts will be stopped. 

Next year I will be blogging more but I want to change somethings so I want this month to work everything out. 

Also work and my home life has become hectic, doing this is mainly to keep my sanity. Maybe next year I’ll do blogmas (don’t know yet though)  

But follow me on Twitter and Instagram where I will be posting still! 

I hope you all have a great Christmas and please still read my posts!
