What makes veterans such great entrepreneurs?

Veterans serve years in the pursuit of a greater good. They are a disciplined and talented group that, unlike some stereotypes suggest, regularly engage in independent and strategic thought. They accepted tremendous responsibility at a young age and are accustomed to high stakes courses of action. They naturally embrace training and are very open to instruction.

In sum, veterans are ideal candidates for establishing new industries and creating social change on a grand scale.

The VIBE provides a dynamic, supportive environment to develop practical skills, refine your business concept, pursue capital and cultivate relationships to maximize your chance of a successful startup. Learn from nationally-renowned executives, successful local entrepreneurs and distinguished mentors while sharing the journey with fellow veterans.

  • ​Regular office hours on Wednesdays and Thursdays from 9:00 to 11:30
  • Weekly guests  on Wednesdays or Thursday at VIBE available to take questions related to business or entrepreneurship. Currently, I have Frank Matulis from Kitsap Bank every Thursday. Also have a commitment from Dr. Jared Williamson a former Army Ranger and five time combat veteran that opened a physical therapy clinic in Tacoma to discuss his experiences in leaving active duty and starting a business.  More speakers coming soon.    
  • Spouses will be allowed to participate in VIBE
