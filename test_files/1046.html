Friday, 29 November 2013

Candice Swanepoel

The woman is a goddess and has been my favourite model for the last two years or so. Candice Swanepoel is one of the most prolific faces in the industry, and has received worldwide attention this month as the treasured wearer of this year's Fantasy Bra in the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show. With an archive of editorials, campaigns and endless VS work, I've had to be very selective in choosing some of my favourite photographs so not to make this a monster post. However I do feel a Candice street style post coming on soon! If you needed a new girl crush, you've found one.
