Cheyana Banana

Today is this little girls first day of College!!


Ok..maybe she's not a little girl anymore.  This is her in her dorm room showing the world who her favorite aunt really is!!  I can't believe that she is in college already.  I remember when she was born and had to be life starred to Topeka.  And now she is a beautiful healthy adult.  I'm probably driving her crazy because I keep texting her to see how she is doing. 
 
Aww..not sure what this face was about!  (BTW, I am like, 36 weeks prego here!  Wow!)  So proud of what this girl accomplished, although I think she might be a little bit crazy for how much she runs!
 


Chey and Grandpa.  I just love this picture.  Look how much they are both smiling!


 
Ok..so I started this post Monday, which was the first day of school, but I'm finishing it on Tuesday, which is the second day.  But this is the picture Chey's mom had posted.  She had asked her for a first day of school pic and this is what she got.  Looks like they are hiding under the bed!  Maybe they were too scared to go to school?? 
 
Well, Chey, I hope you have a great time at K-State.  Manhattan is my favorite town ever so I will be down to visit lots!!  Love you girlie!
