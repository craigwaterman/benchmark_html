Paula Driesell
Locksley Lane is a stroll down memory lane. It's where I grew up and called home for many years. I am a wife and mom to three and newly retired.  I love to take vintage pieces that remind us of home... but need to be refinished to fit the life we are in today.  The pieces can be mine or I am happy to work with you to re-create pieces that are already in your life.   I hope I can inspire you as so many have already inspired me.
