5 Food Blogs You Should Read: ALWAYS!

June 13, 2015 By Augustus O. 2 Comments

This post is a sequel to my recently published “unsuccessful” expert roundup on how to start eating healthy. While working on the expert round up, I made a huge list of food bloggers and made it my sacred duty to monitor their blog post for three weeks, some of which I made comments on.

I had a really nice experience monitoring food blog (The pictures are so real, and you would want to have a bite at your screen), even though I was unable to try out most of the recipes shared on these blogs, I however shared some of the nice ones with my friends.

I have made a compilation of five(5) food blogs in my food bloggers list, the list is not ranked in any manner, but my comment on each of the blog would definitely reflect my biased opinion on each of the blog. I would share a little bit of information about the blog owner, with a link back to their recent recipe post and my favorite recipe on their blog.

  • THE ENDLESS MEAL:

Kristen loves food! And she prepares good meal too! Which makes the equation complete. What attracted me to her blog were the pictures. I was checking out food blogs on Pinterest, and one of her picture came up, I landed on the website and I got glued.

Based on the profile on her blog; she run a “small underground restaurant in Vancouver”. I have never been opportune to taste her meal, but the pictures are so amazing, you can’t resist the temptation of not sharing them with your mum or aunt. You just want them to see the amazing thing you’re seeing in those recipes.

My favorite: Mashed Purple Yams with Sesame Brown Butter.

Her Latest Recipe: Grilled Corn Salad with BBQ Farmers Sausage

  • WHAT’S GABY COOKING?

Gabriella is from California and her blog reminds me of the popular TV series “Orange County” The exotic California life is really obvious on her blog. She’s adventurous as well, but that’s another episode of her life we won’t be focusing on in today’s post.

She run the food section of her blog with the help of friends, although the recipes are exclusively hers, but of the pictures and recipe decoration are always credited to a friend.. WGC is the short name for her blog, and it has been featured on popular online platforms like Yahoo!, Wired, Cosmo and People!

My Favorite: California Cobb Salad

Her Latest Recipe: Banana Cream Pie Parfaits

  • A NUTRITIONIST EATS:

When you read a nutritionist food blog, you should know you’re getting two things from each post; a delicious and healthy recipes. Often these combinations are very rare, but that is exactly what Emily shares on her blog. You might want to check out her first blog post too (Spoiler!: It’s awful).

Each recipe had a concise instructional guide on how to create your own replica of the recipes too. Comparing Emily’s blog with Gaby’s one thing is common to both blogs; California Exotic life!

My Favorite: Lentil Pasta with Arugula Pesto

Her Latest Recipe: Simple Orzo Salad

  • COOKIE AND KATE:

Hello Vegetarians! Here is a blog you should never miss an update from. Kate run this blog in collaboration with cookie (if you’re wondering who/what cookie is, please check her about page… *mouth sealed*)

Kate has been running her blog for over five years now and a larger percentage of the recipes on the blog is targeted at vegetarians because she’s a vegetarian herself. If whole grain food is your thing (I find it healthy), you should really check out Kate blog’s and whip out some new recipes for yourself.

By the way, she’s a self taught photographer, and the quality of the pictures on her blog could pass for a pro camera work.

My Favourite: Vegan Chipotle Carrot Queso

Her Latest Recipe: Mango Lassi Frozen Yogurt

  • THE SKINNY CONFIDENTIAL:

There is something about California bloggers! Their blog design would always show you how exotic life can be over there. TSC is no exception. It’s a blog with various sections, but for the purpose of this post, my attention is on the recipe part.

Lauryn is in to photography, wellness, and fashion e.t.c she’s the kind of individual that fits into the “well rounded” category, her blog says it all. Guys! She’s engaged! So wipe that look off your face and focus on the awesome food pictures.

My Favorite: Easy-Ass, Not So Pretty, Vegan Quinoa Salad.

Her Latest Recipe: Argula Pesto Pasta

 

That’s the top 5 food blogs in my compilation, I might make another compilation of Vegetarian food blog in the nearest future, subscribe to my email list so that when I do, you would be the first to know about it.
