Greek's Weekend Plans And Another Classic Throwback Pic

Tweet
 

It's the weekend, people are jacked up.

I'm having dinner tonight with friends and my girl at New York Prime. Saturday- stopping by the Susan G Komen race and then the locker room with Johnny C.

Teddy Covers Will be on with picks- 3 -0 last week. Super Bowl, the line keeps moving up on Carolina. Haven't met anyone yet who is betting Denver- no offense Peyton...will see what happens!
