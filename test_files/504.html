Torch Yoga

Watch this space to keep up with the latest developments within Torch for yoga and yoga related information. Check out our new Torch Yoga logo.

First things first. My name is Leia Lynch. I am a YogaFit certified E-RYT200 registered with Yoga Alliance. I am the primary Yoga instructor at Torch and will be your Go-to-Girl for anything you need to know, find or learn about yoga.  If you have a question shoot it over to me. My training and background is pretty extensive and I love what I do. Sharing my gifts and helping you along your yoga journey is my Dharma. It is what I am called to do and I love doing it!   If I don’t know the answer  I will do my best to find someone who does so that you will ultimately get whatever it is that you need.  After all that is what we are all about here at Torch – making sure you have the best  experience possible – whether that is with Torch Fitness, Torch Nutrition or Torch Yoga.

            Leia Lynch, E-RYT200
