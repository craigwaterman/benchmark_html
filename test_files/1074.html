#Give365CNY #15 Lend a Hand to America's Veterans

9/25/2015

0 Comments

 
#Give365CNY #15 - Lend a hand to America's Veterans at the VA Hospital
Our community is strong with pride for our soldiers and what a great way to give back by volunteering when they are in need.  Transporting patients and supplies from place to place inside the hospital is always a huge help. Visit the VA Hospital website to learn the many ways you can help out.

Comment below and let us know how you gave back today.
#Give365CNY is a campaign to raise awareness about the importance of giving back. Giving back to your family, friends, neighbors and your community. There are so many ways to "give" and no, they don't all involve money. Your time can be even more valuable than your dollars. Our #Give365CNY campaign was designed to make you think and offer a reminder for every day of the year that giving back is required for a thriving community and a healthy spirit.

A simple compliment can be the difference between someone having a good day or a bad day. A simple walk around the block can be the difference between a happy dog and a sad dog; and a donation of ANY amount at ALL can make ALL the difference.

Founder of Hope Chest for Charity, Shelly Straub says "When I was in my 20's and 30's, life consisted of working too much, raising kids and spending off hours complaining about life's responsibilities. I didn't spend any of my time giving back to the community that kept me employed, schooled my children, and entertained me on my off-hours. I hope that these simple daily reminders in the #Give365CNY campaign will offer suggestions to people of any age that giving back is a small task with huge rewards."

Click the RSS Feed to the right and follow this blog, RT the daily shares on twitter, pin on pinterest or comment below with your "giving" suggestion. Either way, please get involved. This is a community effort.
