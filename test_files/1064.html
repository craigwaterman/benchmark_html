A recap of 2014- some of my favourites…

Posted January 9, 2015 to Children & Family Portraiture , Weddings by: admin.

Happy New Year everyone! What better way to kick off my new year with my new blog and a recap of last year’s wonderful clients! Thank you to each and every one of you who made my year great, I really do have the loveliest clients and the most beautiful weddings and families to photograph! I’m bound to have missed some, but I’ve tried to include at least 2 shots from each job this year, I hope you enjoy xx
