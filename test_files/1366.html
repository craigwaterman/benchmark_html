Hormone Helper Nutriblast Recipe

January 10, 2015/0 Comments/in Nutriblast Recipes, Nutribullet Blender /by NutriBullet Recipes

Recipe: Hormone Helper

Summary: This Nutriblast is featured in the now infamous Nutribullet infomercial. It is geared specifically towards women experiencing menopause symptoms and is great to help females balance their hormone levels overall.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/4 beet
  • 1 handful (2-3 florets) of broccoli
  • 10 grapes
  • 10 raspberries
  • 1 tbsp goji berries (Find them
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Water to fill halfway to the line

Instructions

  1. Remove skin and pit avocado.
  2. Add add all ingredients to Nutribullet.
  3. Add water to half way to fill line, blend, then and enjoy.

Deflamation Juice Nutriblast Recipe

October 8, 2013/2 Comments/in Nutriblast Recipes, Nutribullet Blender /by NutriBullet Recipes

Recipe: Deflamation Juice

Summary: This Nutribullet recipe has multiple healing properties! It’s ingredients naturally decrease inflamation within the body, making it a healthy Nutriblast for those dealing with digestive issues..

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber (medium)
  • 1 bulb of fennel
  • 2 handfuls (1/4 cup) of mint leaves
  • 1 inch slice (2 tbsp) ginger
  • 1/2 lemon lemon (pealed)
  • Water to fill line

Instructions

  1. Remove skin from lemon.
  2. Add mint leaves, cucumber, fennel, ginger, and lemon to Nutribullet.
  3. Add water to fill line, then blend and enjoy.

Quick notes

This Nutriblast is tastes great and has powerful healing properties, but it should NOT be used as a meal replacement (due to the low amount of calories, fat, and protein). It’s a great drink in addition to a meal, or you could also add SP Complete to the mix to make a better meal replacement.

Preparation time: 1 minute(s)

Cooking time: 2 minute(s)

Diet type: Vegetarian

Diet tags: Low calorie, Raw

Number of servings (yield): 1

Culinary tradition: USA (General)

Calories: 127

Fat: 1

Apple Vanilla Nutribullet Recipe

October 7, 2013/0 Comments/in Nutribullet Blender, Nutribullet Breakfast Recipes, NutriBullet Deserts /by NutriBullet Recipes

Recipe: Apple Vanilla Nutribullet Recipe

Summary: The combination of these tasty fozen fruits leave a natural, vanilla-like aftertaste, making this a nutricious and incredibly delicious breakfast Nutriblast.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium Granny Smith (sliced and cored)
  • 1/2 cup Broccoli Florets
  • 1/2 cup Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Mango (about 2-3 Mango spears/slices)
  • 1 Tbsp Sunflower Seeds
  • Water to fill-line

Instructions

  1. Slice and core apple and Mango.
  2. Bag each fruit and freeze in freezer overnight.
  3. Add spinach, fruits, broccoli, and sunflower seeds to your Nutribullet.
  4. Add water to fill line, the blend and enjoy!

Variations

If you’re in a hurry, you can try this one without freezing the fruits. In that case, you may need less water.

Preparation time: 5 minute(s)

Cooking time: 2 minute(s)

Diet tags: Raw

Number of servings (yield): 1

Calories: 317

Fat: 17

Protein: 8

ChocoBerry Blast

August 5, 2013/1 Comment/in NutriBullet Deserts /by NutriBullet Recipes

Recipe: ChocoBerry Blast

Summary: This chilled, chocolate-y smoothie not only tastes great, but is guilt free since it’s super healthy and made of all-natural ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Kale
  • 1 and 1/2 medium Banana
  • 1/4 small Apple (remove as many seeds as possible)
  • 1/2 cup frozen Blueberries
  • 1 tbs Chocolate Cacao Powder
  • Water to fill line

Instructions

  1. Add in Kale, Banana, and Apple to your Nutribullet blender.
  2. Add in 1 tablespoon of Chocolate Cacao Powder.
  3. Add in frozen Blueberries (about 1/2 cup, but you can add more/less to ensure berries reach the fill line). Add in water to the fill line as well.
  4. Blend for 10-15 seconds and enjoy!

Quick notes

Adding cacao powder offers to a Nutriblast an all-natural alternative to eating chocolate. We like the Chocolate Cacao power by Navitas Naturals, which you can find here.

Cooking time: 1 minute(s)

Fat: 5.3

Protein: 6.8

Fruity Furnace Fat-Burning Nutriblast

June 18, 2013/0 Comments/in Nutribullet Blender, Nutribullet Recipes for Weight Loss /by NutriBullet Recipes

Recipe: Fruity Furnance Fat-Burning Nutriblast

Summary: In addition to being a low cal, low fat, high protien Nutriblast, we love this recipe because of the combination of broccoli and cauliflower florets. When combined, broccoli and cauliflower have specific phytonutrients, enzymes, vitamins and minerals that aid in the weigh loss process. Feel free to substitute different fruits, however we really love how the natural sugars in pineapple sweeten a vegetable-heavy shake.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of broccoli florets (about 3)
  • 1/2 cup of cauliflower florets (about 2)
  • 1/2 cup pineapple
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 1 cup of green tea (to fill line)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to Nutribullet blender. Add green tea to fill line.
  2. Mix for 15-20 seconds and enjoy.

Quick notes

We often use the Jarrow brand protein powder, which you can find here.

Variations

Other fruit options you may want to try: 1/2 banana, 1/4 mango, blueberries, raspberries, or blackberries.

Cooking time: 1 minute(s)

Diet tags: Low calorie, Reduced fat, High protein

Number of servings (yield): 1

Culinary tradition: USA (General)

Calories: 192

Fat: 2.8
