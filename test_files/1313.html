The Creative Blogger award

Posted on July 24, 2015July 24, 2015 by Vikar

Yay! My first award on WordPress! I feel ecstatic. This is so amazing. I am so very grateful to Aplis, the poet for nominating me for this award. You can go and check out her blog: Scribbles and Rambles She is really an admirable poet. Most of the people I love and my closest friends also write poems. So, you know I am the kind of person who is in persistent awe of poets-they are just so lovable and great people. Thank you Aplis!

Here are the rules:

1.Nominate some blogs and notify the nominees.

2.Post the link of the blog that nominated you.

3.Share five random facts about yourself.

Random facts:

1. I love the Big Bang Theory. The characters are just amazing….and maybe I love them for I can connect to all of them at certain points…like the “oblivious to human emotions sometimes” character of Sheldon :p

2. I tend to generalize routines in my brain. I have found that my mostly stays in passive mode and I screw up most of the chores that are not a routine to me. Example: I end up turning to the ‘now-blocked’ street over and over in a single day simply because I have been going through the same route since two years.

3. I just hate it when people say words without meaning it or without realizing they’ve said it. Example: I stop suddenly and stare dead eye into my sister continually whenever she says a phrase starting with the word “shit!”. It’s annoying but she doesn’t stop doing it.

4. I have very week eyesight. My contact lens have ‘-6 power’. So I can see practically nothing without my specs. Hell! I don’t even see the outlines of objects let alone the details-should you place it just 3 meters away from me.

5. I can black out of all the conversation the next person thought we were having. What that means is I frequently trip out of the physical situation and get lost in thoughts and daydreams. Worst condition is that I ask someone something then immediately stop listening and after they are finished I have to admit I heard none of it and I then ask them again! (sometimes they get mad at me for this.)

Happy blogging! And felicitations to me! ;)
