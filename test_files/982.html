Student Discount Information

We’re proud to offer student pricing on our Attendee pass! With a valid .edu email address and student ID, we welcome you to register for Techweek under our Attendee pass for $20. Simply click purchase above, and select Attendee Pass – Student. Fill out our questions and you’re good to go! Note that you will not be admitted with a student pass without a valid student ID.

Non-Profit Discount Information

We’re proud to offer non-profit pricing on our Attendee pass! With a valid non-profit email address, business card, and a government issued ID onsite, we welcome you to register for Techweek under our Attendee pass for $30. Simply click purchase above, and select Attendee Pass – Non Profit. Fill out our questions, and you’re good to go! Note that you will not be admitted with a non-profit pass without a valid ID and non-profit business card.

Ticket Upgrade Information
