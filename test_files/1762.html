Event Details

The District Fifteen Educational Foundation
and the Rolling Meadows Park District Foundation are proud to be working together to bring the
Kelly Miller Circus to town under the
Big Top at Community Center Park,
located at 3705 Pheasant Drive, Rolling Meadows.

 

RAIN OR SHINE!!

 

Limited seats for each performance—purchase tickets early!

We are expecting to entertain more than 5,000 people
with two performances each day, and anticipate all shows
being sold out—don't wait!

Come out and enjoy a day of fun filled excitement at the circus with a show that will truly amaze you! Ride on an elephant or pony!
Enjoy the Circus Midway and all it has to offer!

***ALL SALES ARE FINAL—NO REFUNDS***

**Infants and toddlers under the age of 2 are free,
but must remain seated on an adult’s lap throughout the show
for the safety and comfort of all guests.
