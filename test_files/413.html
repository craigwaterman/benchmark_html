    A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

    Since 1974 when our firm was founded in Austin, our mission has been to serve as a champion of the people. Representing “the little guy,” our firm has taken on the United States Government, large hospitals, auto manufacturers, and large corporations. We have the resources and experience to fight a Goliath-sized battle for victims of serious and catastrophic injuries. We can help with every stage of the case in a complex legal system, from the initial evaluation to pre-suit claims and negotiations, through litigation, trial, and appeal.
