Good till the last drop – Tea Cup Find

Had to share with my photographer friends out there.  These have been some of my latest finds that have really been a great additions to my prop collection.

It’s on the smaller scale but good for newborns who are under 7lbs.  I purchased this one at Michaels using on of their 40% off coupons.  The regular price was $16.  Since I bought the blue one I also have purchased a purple one.

To position the baby use a thin or small cloth to cover over where their head will rest.  In the case of the image below I used a white cloth diaper.  I keep a pile of these on hand during my newborn sessions.  I had the Dad hold the baby’s head up with his finger and clone stamped him out.

pin this

Another teacup find happened the other week at of all places, Walmart.  I’m ashamed to admit I now stop there on occasion.  When we lived in DC I would have never considered even checking this place out.  Now that I’m in the burbs and have few options I will look where I can.

I love this cup/ planter but wish they had more color and pattern options.  This cup will hold any size baby that comes your way.  It’s great.  In fact, I think I could get a small 3 month old inside for a shot.  I rolled up a fleece throw from Ikea.  The teacup was found in the garden dept of Walmart and cost $19.  The fleece throw was only a few dollars and found in the bedding dept from Ikea.  I have it in several colors.
