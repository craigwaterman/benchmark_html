Fighting the battle against… DARK CIRCLES!

November 19, 2015November 25, 2015

One of the main aims I have when it comes to applying my make up is to cover up the dark circles that linger uninvitedly underneath my eyes.

Before applying MAC Pro Longwear Concealer

I have tried (and failed) many times when it comes to covering up my dark circles. I would lash on the concealer in the hope that they would just disappear and that my under eyes would look refreshed and of those  that belong to a celebrity. Flawless. But instead, caking on the concealer and foundation just left me looking, well, awful.

Now I know that sleep can help reduce them but sometimes it just feels like they never want to leave no matter how much of a good nights sleep I have…

HOWEVER, it all changed when I came across the MAC Pro Longwear Concealer. This concealer worked wonders when it came to covering up my very unwanted dark circles.

After applying MAC Pro Longwear Concealer

This concealer is quite thick so you only need a small amount of product to get the job done, which is also a bonus as it means that it lasts longer and is 100% worth the price of £17.50!

To apply this product I use my trusty friend… the Beauty Blender. I cannot live without my Beauty Blender and it is a MUST HAVE in my make up bag if you are after that flawless finish.

However, I am not saying that after I apply this product I have absolutely no darkness under my eyes at all because after all I’m human and sometimes life just wants you to look like you’ve had a few rounds in the boxing ring and forces the dark circles upon you, no matter what you do to try and get rid of them.

BUT… I would 100% recommend this product if you are after maximum coverage whilst wanting to maintain a natural and ‘non-caked’ finish.

Feel free to comment and I hope this helps you win the fight against dark circles!

Jess x
