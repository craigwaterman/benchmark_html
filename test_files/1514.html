About Me

Hello, visitors, I'm Chelsea. I'm a NYC transplant from Central Ohio. Currently I'm a bookseller at Books of Wonder with the goals and ambitions of breaking into the children's and YA publishing industry. I'm passionate about book marketing and publicity--the outreach and process of creating content to encourage readers to pick up a book. Hence the blog. My own small contribution to talk about books I love, hoping others will take the time to see what I have to say.

Nerdfighter. Whovian. Crumpet. Quite frankly, I'm just unapologetically nerdy.

I write reviews and other things, and I hope you'll join me in my adventures.

Books I Want To Reread

November 4, 2014 Chelsea Maya Top Ten Tuesday 2

Top 10 Tuesday is a weekly meme hosted by Broke and the Bookish. Each week there’s a theme for bloggers to create a top ten list around.

Please comment and post links to your top ten list below! I visit each and every person who takes the time to stop by and leave a comment.

This week’s theme is “Top Ten Books I Want To Reread.” This was a ridiculously easy list for me to come up with. I don’t reread books very often because my list of new books that I want/need to read is so long that going back and revisiting ones that I’ve already read is hard. These are some books that I’ve been considering rereading for awhile (besides Trial by Fire and Atlantia, I just finished those recently and as soon as I closed the books I wanted to open them and start them all over again, I really loved them that much).

The only book on this list that will probably get the reread treatment before the end of the year is Fahrenheit 451 by Ray Bradbury. This is because I’ve tried to reread that book every year since 8th grade. So far I’ve succeeded. I love it to pieces (so much so that I have a 451 tattooed behind my right ear, true story).
