OOTD: IGIGI Jean Maxi Dress via Gwynnie Bee





My hubby asked me out on a little lunch date, and I thought this dress would be perfect. It's beautiful and comfortable, totally my style! I recently picked this one out on Gwynnie Bee, and I was soooooo soooo excited when it arrived. It's so colorful and fun. I am so obsessed with IGIGI by Yuliya Raquel dresses right now, so I am totally thrilled that Gwynnie Bee has such an awesome selection to choose from.

If you wanna give it a try or try some other new brands and style, you can try Gwynnie Bee for only $10 for your first month! You can click here to get started.
