About Us

Clay S. Nelson, Founder/President

Clay is a leadership development specialist, executive mentor, and the founder of Clay Nelson Life Balance, specializing in empowering leaders to reach their greatest potential in both their professional and personal lives. As an experienced entrepreneur and executive mentor, Clay’s 35+ years of on-the-job experience enables him to relate to the work-life issues that business leaders and executives deal with every day.

The core of Clay’s executive development philosophy revolves around work-life balance and accountability for learning, growing, and then empowering others. Leading from this core philosophy for more than three decades, Clay provides leaders, and their teams, with business and professional coaching, leadership development, team building, mediation, facilitation, planning, system analyses, and seminar leadership.

Those who work with Clay find his sense of energy contagious. Not only will you laugh during his workshops and coaching sessions, but you’ll also learn to incorporate that joy into your life every day. Clay Nelson Life Balance proves that it’s easier and more profitable to incorporate fun into work.

Clay "created Clay Nelson Life Balance to focus on what [he] saw was a deeper need; helping people and companies fulfill their potential; maximize profits; make themselves, their clients, and their families happier; and have a lot of fun in the process." He knows the elements that make a business successful and uses his innate intuition and people skills to help his clients and audiences realize who they are, what’s stopping them from achieving the success they’re looking for, and create the plan of action necessary to get what they say they want in any aspect of their work and personal life.

In addition to being a leadership development specialist and executive mentor, Clay is also:

  • Co-Author of The Change 7: Insights into Self Empowerment with personal development powerhouses, Jim Britt and Jim Lutes
  • An author of several workbook guides on the topics of Business Planning, Marketing, and Personal and Business Accountability, a monthly online newsletter, and an educational CD series, as well as a brand new book titled, Moving You Forward: One Thought at a Time
  • A featured speaker at many national education events and conferences, as well as private corporate retreats for companies such as Donley's Inc and conferences including: World of Concrete, International Builders Show, Remodeling Show, and International Pool Spa Patio Expo

Clay resides in Bozeman, Montana.

Beth Geier, Managing Director

As Managing Director, Beth is responsible for the management of our team’s daily efforts to make a difference for all those we serve. Among her responsibilities is to assist Clay in client relations, schedule and manage speaking engagements and workshops, manage the creation and distribution of company communications, and cultivate relationships with like-minded organizations and individuals, allowing Clay Nelson Life Balance to make an even greater difference in the world! Beth has a Bachelor of Science degree in marketing from Bradley University. She began her professional career as an intern for a marketing communications firm, and prior to her graduation from Bradley University, Beth accepted a position in that firm as their Local and Regional Media Buyer. With time and experience, Beth’s responsibilities were increased to include account coordinating, and in 1998 she was promoted to Media Director. Beth joined our team in 2000 in order to put into action her desire to have more time with her family and make a difference in the lives of others. Beth’s outside interests and activities include supporting her daughters in their All-Star and school cheerleading activities and being the Administrative Mom for an event started by her daughters in 2008, The Backyard Talent Show for St. Jude.

Diego Berber, Webmaster

Diego has served as the Webmaster for Clay Nelson Life Balance since 1996, taking the company's web site through several major and minor revisions over the years and keeping CNLB up to date as new technologies become available. The evolution of CNLB's products and services has required him to take on many other roles on the CNLB Team including print production artist, recording engineer and videographer. Diego holds several degrees and certificates in Graphic Arts and Business, and is a past Gutenberg Award recipient in the print design category. He operates his own web site and graphic design business, and is currently focused on helping clients venture into social media as a new component of their online marketing efforts. His other interests include league bowling, do-it-yourself projects around the house, and music. Diego currently resides in Santa Barbara with his wife and son.
