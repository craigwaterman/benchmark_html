Tuesday, April 1, 2014

Hello Hello,
I officially only have 1 week left of work at my current job. This is definitely a bittersweet feeling. I am so excited to move and be closer to family and some friends, but I wasn't expecting to feel this sad about leaving our current city and my job. We have always said that we would someday move back closer to home, and I thought I was really ready for it and I am, but I am truly going to miss these wonderful people and this amazing place. I know this is the second time I have written about this, but I guess the 1 week countdown is just hitting me a little harder than I thought it would be. I have lived in this city since I was 19 and I am quite a bit older now, and that is a long time to live somewhere I feel like I will be leaving a piece of me behind. Maybe its my young wild self since I will be leaving my college town and a part of me grieves for that because it truly was a wonderful time in my life, I feel like I have learned who I am in this moment in this city and that's a beautiful thing. I am already looking forward to coming back to visit, truth be told we already have our first visit back planned out we know what places we want to hit up what restaurants are on the musts list and it will be so fun to come back and visit this place that I love.

So last Friday was our last night out on the town, so I thought I for sure have to document this, so I have a little outfit of the day video for you guys, be warned I was in quite a mood and lets just say that weird doesn't do it justice, some Fridays are like that for me by the time I get to the end of the week my brain is fried and the only thing I can do is just be silly, enjoy.
