My Fitness Adventure

Posted by annajanewray on November 21, 2015

I can’t confirm or deny if I have recently put on weight. I can tell you that I have received subtle hints from my “man-child” that I should join him in a week of just white meat and green veggies, which for some reason I agreed to after my third glass of red wine (dammit red wine why do you make me so easy to convince?). Since I have started on this clean-eating phase, I decided to add in some stair sprints because I don’t half do things and Halloween costumes lie…. I bought a skirt which in the photo on the packaging went to the model’s knees, but my arse clearly was showing once I put it on. Sorry little bro, but your friends are going to think I am a grade-A-skank at your Halloween party.

There is no way I will convince people running stairs is a good idea. It is one fucking emotional, concrete filled journey. Every step felt like my arse muscles were going to explode. On top of that, loud panting is not something which is attractive. People in our building seem to always be renovating which leads to tradies being everywhere. I have had a few open the staircase door to find me panting really loudly, redfaced with a sweat moustache – I know that is some pants dropping imagery right there.

Females who work out and still remain sexy… just how? I have one friend who can vouch for how disgusting I look while exercising. If you want to form a lifelong friendship go to Bikram yoga with someone of the opposite sex and if they still want to hang out after, that my friends is true love. As Bradley explained, childbirth is the only thing potentially more disgusting he could see me go through (sorry for what I put you through Bradley, it got real).

The amount of pressure we put on ourselves to look good is just stupid. I know most of you come here to read my words because I am a specimen of perfection. You are welcome. No, but seriously, back off magazines! I earned these dimples on my ass. I put in years of burgers and beer. I endured many nights of “meat sweats” because I couldn’t settle for a half rack of pork ribs and went a full rack. I am not a quitter.

So I refuse to read magazines for two reasons. One being I can’t actually read and two is….oh there is only one reason. Turns out I can’t count either. Recently though I have purchased my first magazine in years because if some of you know me personally you would know that Australia has gone completely nuts and my younger brother was featured in there as a nominee for Australian Cleo Bachelor Of The Year… pffft and Greece thought they were having issues.

So after I went to the checkout and purchased it, I walked home with the magazine rolled up under my arm as I could feel my self-esteem drop tenfold as it was the swimsuit issue and I was not bikini ready(whatever that is supposed to mean). Once I opened it in the privacy of my own home (I am totally making this sound like I was looking at porn)anyway I will cut to the chase, I opened it, I fell on the floor with a fit of giggles, I got up and saw it again, I fell over again. Then after 40 minutes and at least 10 pictures texted to my family group message I composed myself. Coming from a country town with an obese brother it wasn’t something I ever envisioned seeing.

There are many things I didn’t envision like running up a 12 level flight of stairs 5 times a day because I was jiggly. Jiggly, what a hilarious word. Not that I have a problem being jiggly but since I am between jobs with not much on my plate but spreading my wisdom I thought I better actually do something. Plus when I work out I get that “proud parent look” from Dan, almost like he is saying “there you go, I knew you could do it”. Well obviously I can do it, it just isn’t in the top 10 of my bucket list. On a side note I will list my bucket list below.

  1. Buy a horse
  2. Purchase a jousting stick
  3. Take jousting lessons
  4. Purchase a life supply of antihistamines because I am allergic to horses
  5. Purchase the house that looks like a castle in Geurie (there is actually a house that looks like a castle just outside Dubbo)
  6. Purchase body armour
  7. Get blonde streaks in my hair so I look like Heath Ledger’s hot sister
  8. Re-enact A Knight’s Tale
