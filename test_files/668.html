Welcome to the Transforming Your Care website

Transforming Your Care (TYC) is the name given to the programme of work aimed at changing Northern Ireland’s health and social care system so that it meets our needs now and well into the future.

Transforming Your Care is focussed on improving the care provided for individuals and families across Northern Ireland. This means more care at home and less care in hospitals. It means supporting people to live as independently and healthily as possible, for as long as possible. In essence, it is about getting better at preventing ill health in the first place but making it simpler for you to access health and social care should you need it.

We have now started to see real, meaningful and positive change through the implementation of the reform plans. You can find out how Transforming Your Care is already improving lives by watching some of the real life stories on this website.
