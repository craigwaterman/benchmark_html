Summer Jean Shorts

Posted by Administrator on 8/5/2013 to Style News

Summer jean shorts are so in this season but with a cool twist - Color! That's right - bright colored denim is the fashion trend this season.  From designer brands like True Religion having colorful jean shorts in their collection to celebrities such as Rihanna wearing them - jean shorts are just about everywhere!  Adding to this trend fashion designers and fashion labels are mixing it up not just adding bight colors but mixing prints, adding various denim treatments, and cut-off frayed edges.  How do you feel about this trend?  Also the summer jean short sketches were created with Digital Fashion Pro Software.

Article and design sketch by Michael Harper. Story posted by John Thomas.
