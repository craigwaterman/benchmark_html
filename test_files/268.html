Fact of the week – 21 August – Autoimmune Disease

It is estimated that over 50 million Americans suffer from at least one autoimmune disease, with women more than three times likely to develop it than men. In comparison, 12 million Americans suffer from cancer and 25 million suffer from heart disease. Although not widely recognised, autoimmune disease is directly linked to diet as are diabetes, cardiovascular disease and obesity. Changes in diet and lifestyle can have powerfully beneficial effects on autoimmune disease, including complete reversal!

 

Source: The Paleo Approach, Sarah Ballantyne, 2014 (Affiliate link)
