Come on in...

08/31/2014

0 Comments

 
Welcome to the Kinsey Art Room. This blog is for parents, teachers, and anyone who is interested on an "inside view" of what's going on in our room. My goal is to feature at least one grade each month and describe what they are doing and most importantly take some pictures so you can see them in action!! 

Before we really dive into projects on the blog, I wanted to give you a tour of our Art room.
One of the new decor items in my room are my table paint cans hanging from the ceiling! Students seem to be really excited about these. We do a lot with our "table groups"--2nd-4th Grade each table even has their own Art Job (see Jobs listed below). The kids really have taken on these rolls with great passion. I love to see all of the responsibility in the Art Room!
My rules, are listed front an center in my room. On the first day I explained to all students that when they walk into the door of the art room, they all become ARTISTS. I explain that in order to be successful artists they need to follow the rules.
Here is a gallery of close ups of my rules, helpful hints posters, our table storage and art centers space (COMING SOON!)
Another thing kids are excited about, is the ability for them to earn a class "paint" (point). Each day that their individual class can have a great day in art, following all of our artist rules...they can earn a paint color to put on their class palette. Once all 7 colors are earned, we get to have an artsy party! This would be something that isn't normally done in art, something extra fun, and the class will vote on which reward they would like to have!
And finally is my teacher desk. Next to my desk, I have a poster that I refer to often. Its a reminder to how important Art is for young people. Yes, we make some awesome things, but we are learning so much more in this room. Join me in being an advocate for the arts!!
