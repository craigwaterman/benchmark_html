Hey, man, what's your sign?

Have you heard about this business with astrological signs changing?  It seems this ideology has been around for 3000 years, but we are only really hearing about it this week.  It's all well and good for most people, like my friend who has "leo" tattooed on her in runes-her sign is still the leo.  But what about me?!  I'm a Libra through and through...but now i'm a Virgo?  What the flippin' F?!  What about my obsession with this?!


not mine, tho i'd like it to be!

Libra-Idealistic. Affectionate. Expressive. Easy-going. Sincere. Fair. Stylish. Good mediator. Well-balanced. Sociable. Artistic. Romantic. Creative. Charming. Diplomatic. Communicative. Absolutely fabulous.  oh yes, for sure.

Let's look at what a Virgo makes-Observant. Sympathetic. Dedicated. Creative. Reliable. Kind. Organized. Analytical. Sensual. Witty. Efficient. Helpful. Charming. Thoughtful. Industrious. Compassionate. Sexy as hell.  Well.  Guess it's not all bad, but the Libra scale symbol suits me best.
Here's what the new signs are, and notice the addition of one between Scorpio and Sagittarius:

Capricorn: Jan. 20 - Feb. 16
Aquarius: Feb. 16 - March 11
Pisces: March 11- April 18
Aries: April 18- May 13
Taurus: May 13- June 21
Gemini: June 21- July 20
Cancer: July 20- Aug. 10
Leo: Aug. 10- Sept. 16
Virgo: Sept. 16- Oct. 30
Libra: Oct. 30- Nov. 23
Scorpio: Nov. 23- Nov. 29
Ophiuchus: Nov. 29- Dec. 17
Sagittarius: Dec. 17- Jan. 20

Now, before you go and get laser removal for your body art, the addendum is that this chart is used by astronomers, not astrologers.  But still-why you gotta go and mess with a sure thing?  A thing that helps people define themselves?  A thing that people have felt connected to? And how many "sure things" are there out there, anyway.  Guess now there's one less.

So, does this change things for you?  Do you plan to stick with the sign you've always been?  Or are you off to see what your "new" sign means?  It's also going around that this is only relevant for people born after 2009, but i'm seeing conflicting stories.
