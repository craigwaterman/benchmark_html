Holiday Gift Guide 2014



Holiday shopping is always a little tough, but sometimes finding things to ask for can be just as hard.  So whether you are doing the shopping or making your list, here is a perfect little gift guide for 2014.





 Polaroid Camera

Polaroid cameras made a major come back this year!  They are great for capturing those crazy nights and give your pictures a cool filtered look straight from the camera.  A fun thing we have started doing is using the pictures as a unique way to decorate your room.



Naked On The Run

This new palette by Urban Decay is a must have for any make up lover.  With six all new shades of eyeshadow, bronzer, blush, a full-size Naked Ultra Nourishing Lipgloss, a travel-size 24/7 Glide-On Eye Pencil, plus a travel-size Perversion Mascara, this is one of the best palettes of the season. 


Michael Kors Slim Runway Watch

A nice watch is always a great gift. This sleek design is totally modern and will give the perfect touch of class to your favorite looks. Stack it with an arm full of silver jewelry or wear it alone as a bold statement piece.


Pull Over by PINK

This cozy pull over with a slouchy neckline is a must-have by Victoria’s Secret PINK.  You might have seen it in this year's Victoria's Secret Fashion Show.  With the fun detail down the arms and the unique cowl neckline, this is a different piece that every girl needs in her closet for those rainy days.



Black by Rue 21

Smelling nice is always a good thing! Girls, cologne is a great gift because you get to pick out a scent you'd love for your guy.  The perfect scent just makes everything better.  And after you give it to him, make sure to spray his favorite hoodie that you stole with some of it too!  And a little Street 23 secret, this is one of T.C.'s favorites!



Pull Up Bar 

This gift is great for the guys that like to work out.  It has a variety of possibilities when it comes to workouts and it's super lightweight so you can travel with it too!  And after all that holiday food, you'll be thankful you have this!


Beats Pill

This portable music speaker is an awesome idea for any music lovers. You can pair and play with your Bluetooth device at a 30 foot range!  So whether you're by the pool, at a party, or just need some music while you're in the shower, this will liven up the mood.





His and Hers Beanies


These beanies are great when it gets cold but are still super fashionable.  His and Her gifts can sometimes be a little cheesy, but these are far from that!  Letting your significant other know that they are your King or Queen and wearing it for the world to see is one of the best gifts anyone can ever receive. 


NFL Ugly Christmas Sweaters


Ugly Christmas sweaters have been a crazy tradition that has gotten super popular lately.  This year the NFL has come out with ugly sweaters for their teams.  This is a perfect gift for those sports lovers that love to have fun with their gift giving.  So whether you are a Seahawks fan or a proud Saints supporter, they have the ugly sweater for you!
