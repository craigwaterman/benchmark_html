How to Optimize Your Blog Articles to Rank Higher on Google, Bing, Yahoo! and Other Search Engines

Publishing blog articles is an excellent way to keep your business website ranking high on search engines. Sites with new content always rank higher than websites that aren't updated. However, you can make your blog articles more effective by using a few tricks.

Quality should be the main focus for anyone writing a blog article. You can use all the SEO tricks in this article, but your blog isn't going to do well if you don't have content that's informative, entertaining and consistent. You should only think about employing SEO tricks in this article after your content quality is as high as possible.

Long Tail Keywords Add Valuable, But Pogo Sticking Detracts

Targeting a long tail keyword in each blog post will attract more organic traffic. Long tail keywords are phrases that are searched frequently. These keywords are less competitive than shorter keywords that only consist of one or two words. This lower level of competition makes it easier for you to rank higher for these phrases.

The searcher's intent is starting to become just as important as keywords in the search engine algorithms that determine what results pop up in response to queries on Google, Bing and the rest. The newer, more complex search algorithms even take into account the time users spend on pages and whether or not they pogo stick on the page. Pogo sticking refers to the act of clicking on a search result and hitting the back button immediately. The negative impact on the page is immediate.

You Need Remarkable Content for High Search Engine Rankings

Your content must be remarkable to satisfy search engines. This is where many people fail. They don't place value on quality content and think that link building and lip service is enough. It's not.

Your content needs to satisfy humans, not just web-crawling computer programs. You can't churn out technical writing that reads like sales copy or an owner's manual and expect your site to achieve success. Your content needs to capture the imagination of the readers. This requires real writers to realize.

Internal Linking is Important

You want a site set up that encourages visitors to click on successive pages within it. The percentage of visitors who enter on one page and don't click around to go deeper into the site is known as the bounce rate. You want to keep your site's bounce rate low. This means you need to entice readers to visit other areas of your site by using compelling titles and making internal links easy to see.

Quick Tips

 - Use Your Targeted Keyword Phrase in the Title

 - Hyphenate the Keyword Phrase in Your URL

 - Write the Meta Description, Complete with Keyword Phrase, for Human Consumption

 - Use the Keyword Phrase Organically Throughout the Post Three to Five Times

 - Use the Keyword Phrase in Your H1 Header Tag

 - Use the Keyword Phrase in One or More of the H2-H6 Tags

 - Name Image Files with the Hyphenated Keywords

 - Include the Keyword Phrase with Detailed Alt Tags for Images

 - Assign Categories to the Article

 - Link to At Least Three High Quality Articles on Other Websites in Each Post

 - Link to Other Pages on Your Own Site

 - Use Google Authorship to Build Authenticity with the Search Engine

 - Share Links to the Article on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and Google+

 - Request Links from Authoritative Sites.


Make the Most of Your Posts

Blog posting involves much more than just writing great content. Although, great content is the most important thing to include in your blog posts. Your website needs content that draws more organic web traffic. Hire an experienced blog writer to help you improve your company website's search engine rankings and drive more customers through your doors!

How Can Using Periscope Benefit Your Business?

The world has been turned upside down by a revolutionary new social media platform called Periscope. What could be more revolutionary than Facebook and Twitter? Periscope allows users to stream video in real time from anywhere on the planet. We've officially entered the future we all longed to see all those years ago.

The Periscope iPhone application may be an exciting new form of entertainment, but what does it have to do with you? You can use it to get more business. Periscope can help you reach and engage more potential customers. Many businesses have already started using the Periscope app to promote their own businesses.

Online video watching has increased over the last few years. According to a study conducted by the Global Video Index, 75% of mobile users prefer to watch videos that are longer than 10 minutes. Most users said they prefer to watch live streaming video over video on demand.

ABI Research predicted that viewership of online video on mobile devices will exceed 21 hours a month per user by the year 2019. The average was already 12.7 hours in 2013. This projection should spur every company to include video as a part of its marketing strategy.

Periscope Does Video Better

Periscope does not just allow you to stream live video in real time. It also offers a variety of incredible features. People watching your video can interact with you and comment in real time.

All your Twitter followers are notified when you go live on Periscope. As you can imagine, this spikes follower engagement drastically. Anything that increases engagement effectively is worth doing.

Periscope edges out its closest competitor Meerkat by offering better functionality. Periscope users can view your videos even after they've streamed. However, they still need to watch them within 24 hours.

The large network of established content creators and curators on Periscope makes it stand out from other apps. Periscope's Twitter backing helps it out in this area. These users are attracting many companies to the platform to perform live product demonstrations and host customer feedback sessions and other events.

How You Can Use Periscope to Grow Your Business

Periscope is a great tool that you can use to engage your audience and add excitement and momentum to your content marketing campaign. However, Periscope is still just a tool and, like any other tool, you need to use it properly in order for it to be effective.

There are three ways you can use Periscope that make the most sense to reach hour audience:

  • Get live customer feedback and address any concerns they have.
  • Announce new products and offers and make viewers feel like they are getting in on the ground floor of something exciting.
  • Get an influencers to market the product. If you make guitars, get Trey Anastasia from Phish to play one live on Periscope.

Add Periscope to Your Arsenal

A successful content marketing campaign consists of a balance of engaging content delivered through many different platforms. Using just one platform won't give you the powerful sales growth you'll see from a full-scale campaign across multiple fronts. Periscope can be an effective part of that mix, but you should use it consistently in concert with blogging, promoting on Facebook groups and all your other efforts.

Bad SEO is for the Birds


You may think you can save money by handling the search engine optimization (SEO) for your company yourself. However, you may be very wrong. Bad SEO can cost your company a lot of money.

 Bad SEO can destroy your company's Internet marketing campaign and general web presence for years to come. Most people may not understand just how detrimental bad SEO can be for a website. This is dangerous because it can take a lot of work to undo bad SEO efforts and get the website ranking well again.

 You can make many different costly mistakes when you try to optimize your website for search engines and you don't really know what you're doing. Everything you do to optimize your website for search engines can have positive or negative effects. The execution is important in everything you do. 

 Bad Links

 Link building is one of the most important factors in a successful content marketing campaign. However, there are many things you need to consider when building links or you could do more harm than good.

 You can fix these bad links, but it takes a bunch of manual work. You will have to put together a list of links to your website from undesirable websites. There are many programs out there that can help you do this. Then, you'll have to look up who registered each URL. This can be done by doing a Who Is look up online. You can find email addresses for most web masters by doing his. However, some are private. You'll have try to find their email addresses by using the contact form on their websites. Even that doesn't work all the time. You might have to get creative and try something else like Googling the URL with the word webmaster or something else. It's up to you to find the email addresses of these web masters.

 Once you have the email addresses of the web masters for the websites you want to take down your links, you'll need to email them to ask them to take down your link. Each email should include the URL where the link is posted. You need to be very nice because you are asking them for a favor. Most people will take down the links, but some won't respond.

 You have to keep track of when you contacted each email address. You need to contact them in good faith three times before you can submit the links for removal. Even submitting the links for removal is not guaranteed to be successful. It's all you can do. You do not want to post bad links. 

 Bad Content

 Content is king, but not all content is created equal. Content containing grammatical and typographical errors is not going to do you any good. You need content that informs and entertains.

 Taking it down and posting valuable content in it's place is the first step in fixing the damage caused by posting bad content. Good content needs to be posted consistently to reverse the poor reputation you've cultivated by posting poor quality content. 

 Duplicate Content

 Publishing duplicate content can be very bad for your website's search engine ranking. Never post duplicate content. This will get flagged by Google as spam and cause your rankings to go down. 

 You can fix your problems created by posting duplicate content by taking down any duplicate content you have posted immediately. You need to make sure everything you publish is completely original and not similar to anything published anywhere else on the Web. You should use Google or Copyscape before posting anything written by freelancers. 


 Too Many Keywords

 Keywords are great. You need keywords. But, there is such a thing as using too many keywords. Google will penalize you for using too many keywords. It will be seen as spam, and your ranking will drop. 

 The only way to reverse the damage done by stuffing your website with keywords is to go through every piece of content and remove the keywords. Then, you'll need to talk to Google and get the penalty removed and act better in the future. 

 Targeted Anchor Text

 Targeted anchor text used to be a great SEO technique that was used by many OKC SEO experts to get their websites ranking higher for the keywords they were targeting. Unfortunately, this practice became so prevalent that it came to Google's attention. Google started seeing the practice of using targeted anchor text as a form of spamming. You can still use targeted anchor text when posting links to your website. However, it won't have as strong an effect and it could even hurt your ranking if you do it too much. 

 It's very difficult to go around the Internet changing all of the anchor text linking to your company's website. You'll need to do something similar to what you would do about outright bad links and manually contact every webmaster to get the links taken down. That can take a long time to do.

 Article Submission

 Writing guest posts on unrelated websites can affect your search engine rankings negatively. There are two factors that cause article submission to be bad for achieving desirable OKC SEO results.

 Firstly, it's easy for people to make the mistake of submitting articles to the directories that you have published on your own website or other article submission directories. Most articles posted to article submission directories are syndicated on multiple websites. This results in getting into trouble with the search engines for duplicate content. Posting duplicate content like this will cause your rankings to decrease instead of increasing like you're trying to make happen.

 The second and most important reason why most of the article submission websites you'll find on the Internet don't have the high rankings they once did is that Google lowered their rankings in one of their recent algorithm updates. Google did this to discourage web masters from submitting articles to these websites solely for the purpose of improving rankings from what is seen as an artificial proliferation of high PR backlinks to their websites across the Internet as opposed. It sees this as a trick to get higher rankings instead of organic growth that comes from delivering excellent content. This means that you don't even get the link juice you want and might even get some juice drained. 

 Be Smart and Hire Professionals

 It isn't worth destroying your chances of ranking well on search engines in an attempt to rank higher on them. The damage you can do from acting out of ignorance can be hard to reverse. 

 Hiring an Oklahoma City SEO expert means that you'll raise your ranking the right way. The SEO expert will reverse the damage done by prior misguided SEO efforts. However, that will cut into the time the skilled OKC SEO expert has to perform new positive SEO efforts.

Prepare Your Oklahoma City Business For the Mobile Internet Revolution

You need to optimize your website to be viewed on a mobile phone or tablet if you want your Oklahoma City business to rank high in Internet searches and attract more customers. Mobile Internet use has been steadily increasing and is becoming the norm. Google's latest algorithm was nicknamed "Mobilegeddon" by many SEO experts due to the fact that it focused on rewarding mobile-optimized websites with higher search engine rankings and slapped down websites without mobile optimization.

Research shows that half the Internet searches made on mobile devices have "local intent". Local intent means these searches are trying to find places close in physical proximity to the searchers. These people are looking for places where they can get the products and services they need.

Morgan Stanley predicted that more people will be browsing the Internet on mobile devices than on desktop and laptop computers for the first time this year. This will be a major paradigm shift for the Internet. Your Oklahoma City business needs to adapt to this new reality.

Do Better by Your Mobile Users

You must do various things to help mobile users find your business on the Internet. The most important thing is to make sure your website resized to fit the screen of the device on which it's being viewed. You should make sure the site loads on any device and users can read the information on there.

The second most important thing you can do to help mobile Internet users in the OKC area find your business is make sure that your name, address and phone number are correctly displayed on your website and in any directories where your business is listed. Mobile users may search for your business using part of your address in order to find you. They won't get any results if you haven't correctly listed your name, address and phone number wherever you can.

Mobile Users Check Business Reviews More

Mobile Internet users are more likely to check online reviews before deciding to visit your Oklahoma City business. Anyone can leave a review of your business online. These reviews influence potential customers.

The scary thing is that you really don't have any control over these bad reviews posted online. All you can do is make sure you're delivering excellent service to your customers. Internet users expect a few bad reviews. Some people just can't be pleased.

You do want to encourage people to publish good reviews. Good reviews published on Google and other important citation sites can actually influence your search engine rankings. A steady stream of reviews can get you ranking higher than your competition.

Citations Help Mobile Internet Users

You may think people only search for things on Google and Bing. You're wrong. There are many people who use online directory sites to find local businesses. These are sites like Yelp, Urban Spoon and Citysearch. Many of these directories have apps that make it easy for people to look through the ratings and pertinent information about local businesses on their mobile devices.

A listing in one of these directories is called a citation. Citations help the search engines to gain more information about your business. The search engines use this information to make it easier for people to find your business when they are searching for the goods or services you provide.

Many GPS devices get their data from these directories. This means that listing your business information in some of the larger online directories can make it easier for people to find your Oklahoma City business using their GPS devices. Many people use GPS to find where they're going when they're driving around OKC. It's worth building some citations to make sure you shoe up when they look for you.

Make It Easy for Mobile Internet Users

You need to cater to mobile Internet users to be successful in your search engine optimization strategy. Websites that aren't mobile-friendly will continue to drop down lower and lower in the search engine rankings. This is your opportunity to get a jump on them. Optimize your website, encourage online reviews and list your name, address and phone number in as many popular online directories as you can!

Use Twitter to Increase Your Holiday Sales

With all of the hype around social media, it can be hard to figure out whether or not a strong Twitter account will actually pay off with cold, hard sales. Nobody can blame anyone who thinks Twitter is just a big waste of time. Fortunately, many companies have been able to increase their sales by engaging people on Twitter.

Twitter Engagement Leads to Sales

Many people use Twitter to find Christmas and birthday gift ideas and deals. This Twitter engagement drives real sales. A study conducted by the DB5 website during August 2014 found that 54% of the users surveyed said that they were motivated to purchase certain products after learning about promotions on Twitter. DB5 surveyed 2,100 American shoppers ages 13 and older. Over 50% of these shoppers admitted to using Twitter regularly.

Twitter users start their holiday shopping before non-users do. They also spend more money and are more prone to impulse buys. They even enjoy shopping more than non-users. They are perfect customers for any company that wants to boost its sales.

Twitter Users Purchase a Wide Range of Products

The Twitter users who were surveyed told the researchers that they were planning on spending money across a wide range of categories. Clothing and shoes were the most popular with three fourths of those surveyed planning on buying them. Gift certificates, movies, music, video games and electronics were close behind clothing items as top gift ideas.

Almost 90% of the Twitter users surveyed said that they end up buying extra stuff when they are shopping for Christmas presents. Only a little over 70% of the non-users said they buy extra items. This means that you can easily influence people to buy extra products on Twitter.

Tailor Your Online Sales Campaign to Convert Twitter Users

You need to start your sales campaign early on Twitter. Twitter users said that two things have the most influence on what they purchase. These things are great prices and positive reviews. Incorporate these two elements into your advertisements and make your landing page mobile-friendly. Mobile devices are used by 70% of Twitter users who purchase products online.

Tweets Correlate to Sales

Twitter conversation volume correlates to sales, according to data obtained by Crimson Hexagon and the NPD Group in November and December of 2013. The data showed almost a perfect correlation between Twitter conversation and the sales of televisions and laptops. You can use this to your advantage by encouraging conversation about your brand during peak purchasing periods like Cyber Monday and Black Friday. You can supercharged these efforts by offering limited time offers just for Twitter users. Research shows that most Twitter users try to use the limited time offers they see tweeted.

Holiday Purchasing Decisions Get Influenced by Twitter

Over half of the users surveyed said they learned about products on Twitter that they later purchased. Many people use Twitter on their phones. More than half the users surveyed said they look at tweets about products they see at the store while they are shopping.

Monitor the results of your efforts on Twitter each day. Optimize what works and encourage users to tweet about the products. These tweets will encourage others to buy.

Twitter Leads to Sales

Twitter has become a valuable platform for guerilla advertising. You cannot afford to ignore it if you want to compete in today's market. Effective and engaging management of your brand on Twitter is vital to keeping your company's sales figures healthy.

How to Get More Traffic from Facebook


Let's face it. Facebook is where the people are. Many people would rather not mess with Facebook anymore. I understand that. There are many reasons why staying away from Facebook makes sense. However, you need to promote your business on Facebook if you want to reach a wide swath of potential customers with your content marketing strategy.

A social media marketing agency called Vitrue conducted research that found that only about 6.5% of the people who see one of your Facebook postings actually clicks on the link. Seems like there is room for improvement for people who want more click-thrombus from their Facebook posts.

Shares and likes are not the only ways to get more traffic from Facebook. There are many other simple ways to generate more clicks. You just need to know what to do.

Get More Traffic from Facebook by Creating an Effective Call to Action

The pictures in your wall posts are clickable. Take advantage of that fact by including a call-to-action button in the image. This should only take you a few minutes to do in Paint or Photoshop.

The SEO experts on the influential Internet marketing blog Quick Sprout say that they see a 14% increase in click-through rates when they do this on their Facebook posts. That's a huge increase from just adding a call-to-action button graphic to your images. It doesn't make sense not to try it.

Get More Traffic from Facebook by Sharing Your Thoughts

Post some personal thoughts along with the image and link. It's easy not to do this on Facebook, but making an effort to share your thoughts can pay off in more traffic and higher sales.

The experts at the Quick Sprout website tested this technique and found that including personal thoughts with your link posts on Facebook increases click-through rates by 8%. That's a nice little increase from taking a few moments to compose your thoughts.

Get More Traffic from Facebook by Making Image Colors Pop

The colors of the images in your Facebook posts can have an effect on how many clicks your content receives. You would think that images and call-to-action buttons that have high contrast and stand out tend to convert the best. This may well be true on other websites, but Facebook is different.

Images that blend in get more clicks on Facebook. This means your images should be rich in blues, greasy and whites. Hexadecimal color #4c66a4 is particularly bland and highly-clickable.

Using non-abrasive colors in your images on Facebook can make a big difference in the clicks your website receives. The experts at Quick Sprout tested this one and got 16 to 28% more clicks. That's a big click gain for just using bland colors!

Get More Traffic from Facebook by Using Open Graph

Most people haven't heard of Open Graph. Open Graph provides meta tags that you can use on your website. These will help you make sure that your URLs are shared the right way on Facebook. It just makes your posts look much better.

Using Open Graph can make the biggest difference in how many clicks your Facebook posts get. The Quick Sprout experts tested it and found that their click-through rate increased by a whopping 39% when they employed this tactic in their Facebook content marketing campaign.

Get More Traffic from Facebook by Using Timing and Frequency

The frequency and timing of your postings on Facebook has a significant effect on how many people click on them. The most engagement happens with posts published on Thursday and Friday, according to Quick Sprout research. That same research found that posts published at 1:00 p.m. Got the most shares, but 3:00 p.m. Posts got the most clicks.

The researchers went on to study what effect frequency of posting had on Facebook clicks. The findings indicated that it's best to post every other day. Some people start to ignore your posts if you publish multiple times over the course of a day.

Get More Traffic from Facebook with Engagement

Posts with more engagement get more clicks on Facebook. Engagement in this case means comments by you responding to other people's comments left on your post. Liking comments doesn't have much effect. Quick Sprout found that responding to every comment can increase your click-through rate by 9%.

Get More Traffic from Facebook by Shortening Your Headlines

Headline lengths on Facebook work the same way as headline lengths on your blog. Shorter headlines get more clicks. Facebook posts with less than 80 characters were found to receive 15.4% higher clicks.

Try These Tips and See If You Get More Facebook Traffic

Give these tips a try. You have nothing to lose and everything to gain. Good luck with the Facebook component of your foment marketing campaign!
