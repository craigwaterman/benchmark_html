Faux Leather & Sweater Weather

February 11, 2016

This week was a bummer! My plans and desires to attend New York Fashion Week had to be put aside very last minute due to some road blocks in my Lyme disease path to recovery. I am trying not to put too much weight on this downward dip in my health, because anyone who has had chronic tick-bourne illness will tell you that there will be times when the progress gets put on hold. Of all weeks to feel terrible, this is the least ideal! However, we must work with the hand we’ve been dealt and find a way to move forward, because there are always worse hands to be given.

There is a great quote by Regina Brett that says, “If we all threw our problems in a pile and saw everyone else’s, we’d each grab ours back.” This is something that I will do my best to remember this Friday while I am not at Fashion Week!

Anyway, currently I am very excited about layering with lengths, and mixing up hemlines. I love this deep cardigan because of its pointed hemline, I think it adds a nice visual interest. This color is sold out, but they have some others. I linked some other similar cardigans below!
