Five Things Friday

March 5, 2015March 6, 2015

So this is what it feels like to work out of the office all week – after last week’s winter weather I thought I was working from home full time ;) The return to normal makes this weekend all the sweeter in coming. I have a few fun plans lined up, am aiming for some massive sushi eating, and get to pack for my trip to Florida next week! But before I get ahead of myself, let’s jump into Five Things Friday! Actually, word press decided to delete number three so how does four things Friday sound?

First, Cake Bake!

It’s no secret that I love to bake. I am the first to jump at the chance to bake a friend’s birthday cake, honestly, it’s just as selfish as it is an act of friendship, I get so much joy out of it! For a dear friend’s birthday this past weekend, I made Joy the Baker’s White Chocolate Rose Cake with Strawberries.

Pretty impressive huh? Nope! Ya’ll I have baked, and broken down over (just ask my mother) a whoooollleeee lot of cake in my 20-something years. For whatever reason, baking brings out my perfectionist side and as I say to my mom, with baking, there is no grey it’s black and white, perfect or awful. I have problems I know . But seriously yall, despite it’s impressive look this was the easies cake I have ever baked! The cake itself stayed together perfectly (I froze it before frosting) and the whipped icing, which I had never used before spread like a dream! As for the rose petals, it is nothing more than circling them around each other just as you did drawing roses when you were little. If you are looking for a Pinterest worthy super easy cake, this is for you!

Second, I’m Going Home!

I don’t know how this happened, but Gate River Run is 8 days away! While I might not be race ready, Gate is an absolute blast and I am going to focus on enjoying every mile! Running Gate also means I am going home to St. Augustine next week! I will visit three times in the first half of this year alone while I only visited twice in three years before that!

Gate also means exactly one year ago I was in Moab Utah with my Mom and Brother enjoying the best weekend of my life running my perfect race, the Canyonlands Half. Let’s just relive that for one moment please….

Moab I love you, will you marry me?
