Gothic Wedding Invitations



You and your fiancé have decided to celebrate your wedding event in the night of Halloween, when it is actually the birth date of you love. It was in the Halloween night when you have first met at the gothic night that your friends have organized two years ago. Since then you celebrate each year your first date anniversary and now that the wedding date had to be fixed both of you came with the same idea: the Halloween day. You are both aware of the fact that celebrating such an important event has to reach the dimensions of a different wedding party, other than the white traditional one and as such every detail included has to be related to the spirit of this strange holiday.
 
But both of you are really enchanted by the idea, both of you attending the last year of drama classes and with the possibility of hiring the stage where you have your exam performances, not to mention that plenty of costumes can be put at your disposal, on a rental regime, that is! Incorporating the sense of mystery and drama in the atmosphere of your wedding will turn this special event in an unforgettable memory both for you and your wedding guests as well.


Announcing your parents on the upcoming event and letting them know about the Gothic spirit of your wedding, you understand instantly that you haven’t taken into account your parents’ wish to wear the traditional white dress for the celebration of your wedding. You see how disappointed they are in this matter and talking this matter over with your fiancé, you decide to have two distinct celebrations for your wedding day. The afternoon of the day to be held in the church where you could wear the white wedding gown and the joining into the holy matrimony to be performed in the presence of God’s blessing.

The evening moment of the daytime should follow the ceremony with a reception held in the theatre right on the stage that you have previously thought of decorating with specific Gothic props. At this point of the virtual wedding planning your parents show themselves satisfied enough and going further with the preparations you announce them that the time has come to create your Gothic wedding invitations. Now it is up to you to decide: should you go for different wedding cards? Apparently you have to. One can not combine a traditional religious wedding service with the dark mysteries meant to be displayed in the atmosphere of a Halloween themed wedding reception.


At least this is what your parents advise you; and since they assure you that they would be the ones to pay for the cards you let yourself go with their opinion. Their condition is to let them choose and word the ceremony cards while the other ones, the Gothic wedding invitations for the reception part of your wedding celebration will be left under your charge. This condition suits you the best, and one evening, all being gathered in the living room, you start searching for the two distinct sets of wedding cards that are meant to be dispatched for your assembly of wedding guests.11
