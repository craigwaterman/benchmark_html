Spring Has Sprung IN My Home!

It could still snow...it's still a bit early for spring, but none the less, I have taken down the winter decor and decorated with birds and eggs and nests, oh my!




I glued tea cups and saucers on white painted iron candle stands and added tealights.



I painted an old table leg and made it into a stand.






Framed vintage post card.

Drew a bird on my kitchen pantry door chalkboard.

NOTE: You should be able to click on most of the pictures to enlarge to see further details.
