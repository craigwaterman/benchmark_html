Top 5 Tuesdays: Fashion Blogs to Follow

1 Reply

Hey yall,

In case you didn’t already know, (spoiler alert) I’m a fashion blogger! So reading other fashion blogs kind of comes with the territory. For today’s edition of Top 5 Tuesdays I’m featuring 5 fashion bloggers you should really be following.

Note – I’m placing a photo for each blog so you can get an idea what each is about. These are not my photos and they’ve come from each blog. 

1. Wendy’s Lookbook  

Her style is always super elegant and classic but has a unique twist. 

2. Southern Curls & Pearls 

I have serious outfit envy every time I’m on this blog! The fall style being featured right now is so stylish and actually makes me look forward to cooler weather.
3. Mariannan   

In addition to just fashion, here you’ll find a range of posts from photo round ups to home decor. 

4. Kayture   

She has gorgeous, quality photos, trending style, and seriously seems like a super busy person.. In a good way! You can tell she puts a ton of effort into every post.

5. Queen of Jet Lags

 
You also need to follow her on Instagram.. Pro tip. She has a unique style (which I love) and travels to a ton of destinations!
Of course I follow a ton of blogs and love them all, so I’d love to do more of these! Be sure to leave your links in the comments.

While we’re on the topic of following blogs, don’t forget to follow mine so you never miss a post!
