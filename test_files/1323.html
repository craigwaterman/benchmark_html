The healthcare industry is constantly exploring new frontiers as the need for medical solutions is driven by a better understanding of all aspects of disease. The growth of the healthcare industry does not face the same constraints as many other sectors of industry - medical needs are ongoing and technological advancement cannot remove the vulnerability of the human body. Mirroring this growth potential is the wellness industry which is driven by the desire to live healthier and longer.

Media is essentially the interface between the medical industry and the patient beyond the doctor's room. The patient of today is able to access accurate medical information and make informed choices mainly due to the convenience and versatility of the internet.Understanding the interplay of healthcare, media and the patient requires the input of professionals from several fields or a single multi-skilled and dynamic individual with a cutting edge grasp of healthcare today and in the future.

Dr. Maulik Vyas is one such professional. With a 10 year history in healthcare management, marketing and consultancy, Dr Maulik helps clients in the healthcare industry by offering a unique perspective through his diverse training and experience. He plays a key role in clients achieving new heights by offering consulting services in product development, media strategy and tailor made business solutions for the health care industry as a whole. Dr. Maulik's multi-faceted perspective of healthcare, media and the patient allows him to delve into the project with a practical background knowledge of what works and how practical it is for all participants and through all chains of distribution. He has experience of teaching/Faculty & Training too.
