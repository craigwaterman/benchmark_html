From Fat to Fit

October 8, 2015 by coachcindynewman@gmail.com in Nutrition, In Home Fitness, Beachbody, Fitness 2 Comments

The journey from fat to fit has been a long and adventurous one. It took a year for the fat to come up and I am still a work in progress.  Working on toning, staying on track, maintaining, having my moments, and refining.

My why has change a lot over the last few weeks, not necessarily changed, but I have gotten more clarity on my why. My mom recently passed away from liver and kidney failure due to alcoholism. I tried for years to have her quit and change her lifestyle to no avail. I finally had her start drinking Shakeology, but it was too late….

Rewind about 17 years, I was married at the naive age of 18 to a man
I thought I loved. As I grew up I knew I was wrong. I was in a verbally abusive relationship with a man who cheated on me. I made up for my unhappiness with food and alcohol. As time passed I went from a size zero to a size 16. I was unhealthy, unhappy, and a drunk (and not a fun one either). I was on the same path as my mom. After about eight years I made a decision to change my life. I left my husband and went straight to another verbally abusive relationship. It’s what happens when you have no self-esteem. One day, 4 years later, while trying on some clothes I realized the size 16 did not fit anymore. I broke down and cried in the dressing room. I had been in denial for years. I was one of those girls that wore clothes that I shouldn’t be wearing because they were WAY to tight for me. I m
ade the decision to loose some weight. I saw an infomercial of P90X and started to get REALLY excited. I ordered Power 90 because I felt I wasn’t quite ready for P90X. Long story short, I started eating healthy, lost TONS of weight, and in the process found my own self worth. Enter P90X and wow! What a difference…I lost 17″ in 90 days and 14 lbs! I did Insanity AND Asylum and felt ready to conquer the world. I stopped drinking and realized I was still unhappy. I moved out after 3 years with a new body to give me the courage to go. I lived with my mom for a year and essentially started over. When I finally had enough money saved up I packed up my stuff and moved to California with my doggie. At this point I was down to a size 2 and feeling great about myself. I met Jeremy, my fiancé, at the local fit club. Now I eat only organic foods, rarely drink, use no over the counter drugs, I’m happy and healthy. The only thing missing in my life is my mom.
My why is that I want to help as many people as possible get healthy and happy. I want to be a successful stay home mom to our future children and show them how to live happy and healthy lives. I want to see our team succeed. Nothing has been more rewarding to me than being at home with the love of my life and our four legged babies.
