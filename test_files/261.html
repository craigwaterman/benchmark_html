Top Reasons to Support Local Businesses and Examples of How We Practice What We Preach

February 12, 2015|Infomation

     1   When you make purchases from local businesses, the chances are that your money is used to strengthen the local economy. When you choose Carney & Son 72 Degrees, you are not only supporting us, but other local companies as our parts and equipment are purchased locally. Also, since we believe in supporting local companies, you will always see our trucks parked at local restaurants during lunch time! Overall, we share our revenue with the community in every way possible to keep our dollars in the local economy.

  1. Did you know that small businesses are more likely to support local non-profit organizations than big chain businesses or franchises? One of our favorite places to pay it forward is the Lowcountry Food Bank!
  2. When you make purchases from local businesses, you are also making an ecofriendly choice. You reduce the amount of gas and pollution on the streets. To add to that, we recently traded in one of our Ford Econoline Vans for a Ford Transit which gets much better miles per gallon.
  3. When you choose a local company, you are supporting the local employment rate! We are a small company with only 6 employees but with our recent growth we hope to add at least two more positions in 2015!
  4. Local companies tend to be more inclined to build relationships with customers and provide better service. Our customers are like family to us and we pride ourselves on our integrity and honesty. We believe in the products that we offer and take the time to be professionally trained in our field.
  5. Local businesses are more likely to participate in the community. We like to give back to the community by participating in local festivals (Green Fair, Bridge Run, Jazz Festival Sponsor to name a few) and sponsoring local teams and events (Peewee football team sponsor, Folly Beach Anglers Tournament Sponsor, etc.)
  6. Local businesses make your community unique! You won’t see our trucks anywhere else in the world! It is wise to support local businesses because they add to the uniqueness of your community.
