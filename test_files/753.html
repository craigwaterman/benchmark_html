It's Autumn!

Hey everyone! It's that time of year again! Time to get out your sweaters and those old pumpkin pie recipes. It's autumn!

So around Webkinz World, everyone is getting ready. I'm talking MyPage statuses, new trends, and don't even get me started on the baking thing going around. Everyone is all over baking autumn-themed recipes. Pumpkin pie, apple cider, you know, that kind of stuff. And I am under the autumn spell, too. I have been baking with Silly a lot. It has been a great school year so far. The first quarter just finished last week. I got an A on my English quiz! Yay! :) I have been decorating my house to fit the fall theme. Almost every room has a vase of red and yellow flowers. Happiness!
