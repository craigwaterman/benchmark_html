#OOTD Voodoo Vixen ‘Zara’ Dress

April 3, 2015April 3, 2015

Hey there space cadets!

Today I fiiiinally got around to taking some photos in my Voodoo Vixen ‘Zara’ Dress.

You may remember from my post ‘Feel Good Swaps’ that I had decided to clear out my wardrobe of unwanted clothes – for wish list items! Zara by Voodoo Vixen was one of my top 5!

I would never of thought I’d have the confidence to wear – nevermind post a picture of myself – in such a figure hugging dress. I just love this dress so much – it’s made me ditch my fears and go for it!

The print was designed in-house and features space pinups, starburst, rockets, boomerangs and tons of atomic goodness!

I fell in love with the print the minute I saw it and I absolutely adore the piping around the pockets, shoulders and collar. Also, the pockets are REAL.

The fabric has a nice amount of stretch in – my hips rejoiced at this – and it hugs in all the right places.

I’m not a small gal, so I was a bit self-conscious about my stomach sticking out a bit – but the print is pretty busy and by the time I’d had another look at the laser guns, I couldn’t care less about my tum!
Overall, thank you Voodoo Vixen for creating dresses that flatter all shapes and sizes – you are clothing wizards – I love the Zara so much.

On another note, I’m going away to Ireland with Paul until Tuesday so I will be very quiet for a few days – lucky devils.

I hope you all have a wonderful Easter weekend!

Georgie
