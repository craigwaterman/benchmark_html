Do you accept PayPal in your small business?

Share

Do you accept PayPal in your small business? If you don’t, you should definitely think about it.

Did you know there are 5.5 million active PayPal accounts in Australia? With more than 7.5 million payments processed each day. And it’s not just for online payments anymore. With PayPal Here, you can avoid the fixed costs and hassle of a merchant bank account to accept credit and debit cards. Or your customers can use the PayPal App and leave their wallet at home.

would you put your money into the bank account of a startup you’ve only just met? No? Me either. That’s our main reason for partnering with PayPal

If you accept PayPal as a payment method, it not only gives your customers more choice, but it gives you more chances for opportunistic sales. Got a local walking by without their wallet? They can use their PayPal app to check-in and pay for that great lunch special or that dress in the window, or that cake they have just earned on their walk.

Got a website? PayPal Express Checkout can help increase sales an average of 14 percent. Even David Jones and Myer accept it. It seamlessly blends the online and offline payment experience for your customers, they get a uniform experience either way.

Why do over 110,000 Australian businesses accept PayPal?

It’s secure. It’s simple. It’s trusted by business and customers globally.

These are the reasons we chose to use PayPal as our gateway. As a startup, like any small business we wanted to keep upfront costs down, using PayPal there is no upfront cost. We have a great idea which we are passionate about – making sure Australian small businesses and sole traders get paid on time. We feel that this important part of our economy has been overlooked by the ‘tech revolution’ and it’s time something was done about it. But would you put your money into the bank account of a startup you’ve only just met? No? Me either. That’s our main reason for partnering with PayPal, they are trusted and they are well known and widely used. Paypal have a great reputation which will enable us to deliver a much needed product to Australian small businesses.

So I’ll ask again, do you accept PayPal in your small business? If not, head over and find out more then have a look at your options to suit your business.
