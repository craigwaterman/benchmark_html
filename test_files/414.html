Blogmas Day 6 | Christmas pamper evening essentials

Courtney Sunday, 6 December 2015 1 comment
Who doesn't love a good pamper evening? I enjoy a good pamper all year round but I find it a lot more exciting around Christmas where I can get cosy in some Christmas pyjamas and indulge in a nice warm bath. Here are some of my essentials for a cosy winter pamper evening!

The attire


I love it when I have a free evening and can get into some comfy clothes as soon as I get in from work, I normally opt for a pair of Christmas pyjamas like these and some fluffy socks to keep my tootsies nice and warm. 

The products


I'm going to be a beauty blogger stereotype right now but the first thing I like to do on a pamper evening is light a festive Yankee candle by the bath, this is the Cranberry Ice candle and it smells SO GOOD. When I'm having some 'me' time around the festive season I enjoy indulging in a Lush bath bomb from the Christmas range, today I opted for the Star Dush bath bomb, you can see my review of it here with more pictures! To be in-keeping with the festive touch I also used the Philosophy peppermint shower gel which smells just like candy canes, I'm really into peppermint scents lately. 

The extras


Once I've had a bath I like to get cosy with a blanket and watch Christmas films or catch up on any tv programmes I've missed (loving The Apprentice at the mo!), a favourite film of mine is A Muppets Christmas Carol as I used to watch it every single year as a child. For me a pamper evening isn't festive without a yummy hot chocolate, keep an eye on my other Blogmas posts to find out how to make this delicious Nutella hot chocolate!

What do you like doing on a wintery pamper evening?
