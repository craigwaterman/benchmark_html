Cardio vs. Weight Training

March 10, 2015March 10, 2015FicLifts

For all my friends out there starting to get fit, let me debunk a common misconception for you. Cardio is fantastic. There is no doubt in my mind that cardio does wonders for you. However, eating healthy and running hours at a time won’tÂ let you achieve that super fit, tone body that you are looking for. Weight training is just as necessary and can burn just as many calories as cardio. Many women believe that lifting weights will make them very bulky as well as someÂ men. Weight lifting is not going to turn you into a freak-a-zoid, muscle bound, meat head of an animal. When we see those men and women perform at Mr. and Mrs. Olympia, we see just how extreme lifting can change the human body. Don’t forget, that is their job. They lift, eat and lift again daily. Â So for all the men and women who are afraid of getting “too big”, have no fear. Enjoy a healthy mix of cardio and weight training and I guarantee some of the best results.

Lifting Tip of the Day: Dead lifts and squats are just as good as cardio when it comes to burning calories! Don’t neglect these two for an extra hour of cardio.
