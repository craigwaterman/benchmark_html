Pearls of Wisdom- PPB’s Top 5 Handmade Wedding Favors on Etsy

written by pinkpearlsblog

Hey you guys!  We’re packing you full of wisdom this morning!  Any Etsy lovers out there?? Well as an Etsy seller myself I am always purchasing from there.  And I guess it’s not really only to support handmade because I know how tough it can be doing it as a business, but also just because I really like handmade things!  Anyhoo, I thought today I would share some of our favorite Handmade Wedding Favors via Etsy.

First, I found these lovely coffee favors from Apropos Roasters.  We thought the “Love is Brewing” was such a cute touch to the package, but the back is what really stole our hearts…a sweet quote from the one and only Johnny Cash.

Isn’t that so sweet?!  You can learn more about the Apropos crew by following them on Instagram or Facebook.

Number 2 on our list is from Mint Favors and More, and has just the right amount of cuteness.  They also have the most beautiful little tags and decoration to wow your guests… And who doesn’t love an edible favor??

On top of that, Melanie, the shops owner, was such a delight to work with in helping us with this post… Which gave me a taste of what her customer service is like.  :)

You can follow her Instagram or Pinterest for more updates and inspiration, and if you aren’t an “Etsy person” visit her direct at www.mintfavorsandmore.com

The third little wonder on our line-up is from Porcelain Favours.  These gorgeous handmade hearts are such a sweet little keepsake!  I know mine would come out once a year and go right up on my Christmas tree to remember my friend’s big day for years to come…

Number 4 on our list are these absolutely stunning bird figurines from This and Thatish.  The colors are just beautiful.  They would be lovely on a shelf in any home, and can you picture the tables at the wedding??  WOW!

And last but not least are these sweet little soaps from Roots Soap Co.  Not only are they super practical, but we just adore the little engraved initial details.  I would gladly take one of these home from any affair and use it proudly during the next week.

Well that’s all for now pearlies!  Hope you love these favors as much as we do, and consider something unique, lovely, and maybe even handmade for your big day!

xoxo,

Chrissy and MiMi
