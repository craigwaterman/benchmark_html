Aztec/Native Tribal Print & Vintage Floral Print week.

What I wore this whole week unedited Raw Street Fashion

My apologizes, I haven’t updated in a while. I’ve been feeling under the weather lately. I am back on track and ready to serve & TICKLEDIFANCY. Hope you guys enjoyed my San Francisco Street Fashion post last week my friends are hot aren’t they =P Manila Street Fashion coming soon.

These are all unedited or filtered by the way and are all taken by a regular Ipod touch.

The quality of the picture really depended on the weather and lighting that day. I did take an actual photoshoot with a real camera though..I’ll post those up later  ;)

  • Friday- Aztec/ Native Tribal print loose dress shirt, leggings, snake skin platform oxfords =) & a peacock earning one one side.

  • Thursday – Vintage Floral loose sheer top on top of a mini blue and white stripe dress. & my favorite pair of beige oxfords with open slits on the side =)  Polka Dot head wrap under a regular hat; simple as that.

  • Wednesday- It was really hot & sunny that day so sorry that the picture is a little bright I wore an awesome floppy hat, with a vintage floral print mini dress undress a royal blue pleaded asymmetrical skirt. It was so bright but you can’t see that I’m also wear beige platform oxfords =)

  • Tuesday- I wore a Aztec/Native Tribal Print Jumper. Very comfy I may add, with a self made read crochet beanie & a dream catcher necklace with Hello Kitty Glasses

  • Monday- I wore another vintage floral mini dress. Very girly, with turquoise tights with platform oxfords  Head Accessories was self made as I instructed in a previous post. Just puka shells wrapped around  my curled hair clipped on with a flower  Look was inspired by Zooey Deschanel with a Dian twist to it.

I know that my style might be a little odd & weird to some..and flavorful & unique to others..but It’s me and this is who I am..It’s my skin and clothes are like wearable art. Like what I always say..

It’s not what you wear..it’s how you wear it. You can buy fashion but you can’t buy style

Manila Street Fashion coming up next & Some of my photoshoots..Thank you to all my readers from all over the world. I know I’m an odd one but we weren’t born to fit in..we were born to stand out.

Fashion Diaries- What I wore every single day this week at work .Daily Dian Street Fashion.

I decided to be diligent this week and take a picture of what I would wear every single day at work. =)
I’m currently in Manila, Philippines as an English Teacher, and as you can see..you can take the woman outside San Francisco but you can’t take San Francisco out of the woman. ;)

No, I don’t normally plan my outfits. I wake up literally 40 minutes before work and rush my way there..I’m not that brand conscious.I always wanted to be apart of lookbook.nu but every time I tried to post something I would have to state what brand it is or where I got it from. I would make most of my beanies and earrings..and most of the things I own are vintage from my moms closet from the 70’s, and the other stuff from I just get from all over the place. I believe it’s not what you wear it’s how you wear it. You can buy fashion, but you can’t buy style.

  • Friday 

Close up of my face, My Hello Kitty cheetah glasses & my heart printed slacks. Cream vintage blazer with a black knitted vest under because it’s so dam cold in my office.No filter because it’s was nicely lighted at my office. Here is my whole outfit, I had to filter this a tad bit because it was taken on a gloomy morning.

  • Thursday

Sometimes I like to feel extra feminine. Self made red crochet beanie. Black wife beater and a pretty long pretty skirt with a design pattern which I Fancy, don’t know what the pattern is called exactly.

  • Wednesday 

I was literally in a rush this time, but this outfit is still all Dian. It’s a purple/pink/gray one shoulder Tie Dye/Paint Splatter mini dress which I wore with black leggings and my comfy Aubrey Hepburn flip flops. I call this my New Age Hippy Look,

  • Tuesday

I wore zebra leggings which most would think it’s hard to pull off, just scroll down to my previous post and there are simple instructions how to pull it off ;) If it fit’s your personal style rock the fancy out of it.

  • Monday

At the beginning of the week I wanted to be super casual, but you can still be casual with a twist right? I just added extra accessories to add some flavor. A vintage gold bag and a gold clock watch that’s always on 4:00. Four is my favorite number.

So there you go..that was me for this week ;). My apologies for the poor camera quality again.
It’s a early Saturday morning, and I am ready to hit the sheets.

Sanrio Surprises,Hello Kitty Street Fashion and how to pull off Animal Print Leggings.

Sanrio Surprises always has always been a fancy must have ever since I was in elementary school. I can still remember the 5 storey store they had at downtown San Francisco. To have items like pencils, to erasers from Sanrio Surprises was pretty much “cool” back in my elementary to my high school days. Some how I remember the erasers being scented and it smelled nice ;)

 
Back then Pekkle and Keroppi were my favourite characters =) & if you can remember them, you’re probably a 80’s kid and grew up in the 90’s like me.

Hello Kitty‘s character has always been one of main dominating characters. Until this day Hello Kitty is  still number one, and for a while now..has been a fashion craze which I’ve been tickled with myself.

Yup I got the glasses, and I do plan on putting my actual grade in them, but not these..I have the brown cheetah version that I will rock the fancy out of. ;)

You can also rock a hello shirt over a nice floral mini dress ;)

and yes..I actually did get my nails painted in them once =) but my ring finger was a tuxedo

and not to mention the case. I did indeed got the case.

I don’t know what it is about Hello Kitty but it sure does TICKLEFIFANCY !!!

=) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =) =)

Since we are on the topic of animals in some way let’s talk about animal print leggings shall we?

Here are some well known celebrities rocking them out.

I love Whitney’s look by the way. I am also a huge fan of Blazers & for her o match with some cheetah leggings were TICKLEDIFANCY  times two.

If you were to buy a pair of Animal Print Leggings here are some advice.

  • Wear a plain loose or hip vintage inspired loose top.key word “loose”
  • If you were to wear it with a tight tank top, make sure sure you match it with a nice Blazer
  • Or if were to wear it with a tight tank top again,add layers =) add a loose mini hip vintage inspired loose tank. Like me in my pictures for example ;)

This is yours truly rocking out Animal Print Leggings, (ZEBRA) with a black wife beater and I layered it with a Mini Loose Vintage Inspired printed tank top with black and cheetah oxfords platforms =) with one of my favourite words and logos. Love & Peace sign.

I call this pose..
PEACE LOVE & HAPPINESS..HIGH ON LIFE.

Here I am just posing normal <3

Thank you to all my readers. I’ve only been blogging for about hmm…a little over a week now and I am overwhelmed with the views and love I get from all over the world, I did not expect it at all. I truly appreciate you all and thank you for taking out time from your busy day to read my site ;) there will be more to come.
