~Welcome to French Bohemian Farmhouse~

   
Hi , I am Karen..Welcome to my new blog French Bohemian Farmhouse..Combining my love of flowers, aged wood, metal, stone, industrial elements, and painted pieces from the past..My home has a time worn but timeless ambience..I am not new to blogging, I have had several.My most recent blog Romantic Reflections is also here on blogspot ..That blog is more colorful, romantic and Shabby Chic like..But as I grow older, I am in my 40's..I am going back to my roots..When I was a girl my mother had a large collection of Victoria Magazine's..I would browse the pages and would be drawn to all the white décor ..It seemed so dreamy to me & enchanting.White, it evokes a feeling of being clean, fresh, and yet whimsical..I feel serene and peaceful surrounded by white furniture, linens, lace, and vintage finds from flea markets, estate sales, and thrift shops..When my two daughters were 1 & 3 years old, I went through what I like to call affectionately the "crazy color faze"..Children are into bright colors, happy colors at that age, and my girls were always drawing, so as to inspire them I restyled my home for them..It did not last long I must tell you..And now that they are 17 & 14.."I" have grown out of the color stage completely I feel..I am re-creating a home that is inspiring to me, that I feel peaceful in, and that I want to continue to make lovely memories in..Our home is our haven, our sanctuary, a respite from the world where we feel safe, happy and content ..Welcome to my small corner of the world..This is just a few whispers from my rustic & romantic enclave...
 
 
 
 
Worn and weathered pieces of furniture grace almost every room in my home..The table above was purchased at the flea market..The Hoosier cabinet from an estate sale..My mother had a Hoosier when I was a young girl, complete with glass knobs..I always tell mom she was shabby chic before it was defined and given its name..I grew up helping my mom tend her garden, baking & cooking with her in the kitchen, hanging freshly washed laundry on the clothes line in the backyard, vintage chenille bedspreads covered every bed, fresh flowers scented every room, tea, music, art & poetry is what this nature girl grew up on..My Twitter tag line explains me best..~A girl raised in the 70's on Jesus & vinyl.I am a stylist/photographer who adores vintage..
 
 
A worn weathered white palette lends an ethereal ambience to a home..I use architectural elements mixed with vintage pieces for a look that is elegant yet relaxed..Vintage finds lend a sense of history to your décor..There was once a story there..I often wonder, who gazed into this mirror?, who arranged flowers in this vase?, who sat in this chair?..I love knowing that their story will continue through me..by me....I am the consummate nostalgic romantic..
 
Fresh flowers grace my home every day..Having something from nature in my home makes me feel connected to the earth..They add such beauty to my life..
 
 
 
In the coming months I will be sharing my home, garden, as well as recipes,repurposed pieces, and inspiring places I visit..Thank you for spending some of your time here at French Bohemian Farmhouse..I hope my captures, memories, ideas, and home will inspire you to dream,to create, to smile, to sigh, to perhaps live a more free spirited,thoughtful, spiritual & ethereal lifestyle..  
 
 
Xo..Karen~.......
