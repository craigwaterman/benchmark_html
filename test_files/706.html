Decorating with Transparent Furniture, Not Just for the Modern Home

Posted by Linnore Gonzales on Thu, Jun 05, 2014 @ 06:00 AM
Tweet
 

Transparent furniture is considered a modern décor element and therefore largely presumed to only be featured in modern designs, but this assumption is simply not true. Anyone can incorporate transparent pieces into their home’s current décor for some added visual interest and to create a unique look through the blending of styles.

Whether you are looking to provide a minimalist element or simply add a little sparkle to your traditional décor, let’s take a look at what transparent furniture can do to enhance your home’s style.

Highlight an Accent Color

If your space already contains a bold accent color, or if you are considering redecorating to include an accent color, transparent furniture can help show off your carefully chosen pigment. Transparency provides simple, noncompetitive functional elements that draw eyes to the accents, not to the furnishings.

This kitchen is the perfect example of how a design of this nature can capture a color.

Photo: Houzz

Additionally, you do not have to stick to just one color. You may have an entire accent wall or special architectural element worth showing off. The transparent furnishings allow particular design components to remain as focal points while adding functionality to your space.


Photo: Pinterest

Complement any Neutral Color Scheme

Your neutral color scheme can be greatly enhanced with the addition of transparent furniture that naturally adds a little sparkle to the mix. Enrich the look further by utilizing as much natural light as possible to maximize the reflections off the furnishings.

This dining room embraces grey tones while the following sitting room radiates an earthier feel exemplifying the versatility and beauty encompassed with transparent elements.

Photo: Houzz

Photo: Houzz

Create a Feeling of Openness

Transparent furniture is great for small spaces because it will make the room feel larger. Couple the furniture with light colors or a great view for a serene and calming effect such as exemplified with this impeccable bedroom.


Photo: Pinterest

You can even create a perfectly comfortable workspace out of a small area with a transparent desk and chair.

Photo: Pinterest

Tinting is an Option

“Transparent” does not have to mean “clear,” but rather “see-through” providing the option of tinted furniture like in this dining area. The chairs add visual interest through varying colors that still enhance the room’s natural light and beauty.

Photo: Houzz

The Perfect Addition to Traditional Décor

If you currently inhabit a traditional home, but are looking for a facelift through a slight modern touch, transparent furniture could be your perfect solution. You can start with a chair or two and upgrade as your taste for the style grows stronger.

Photo: Houzz

And if you are not quite ready to make the commitment to large pieces of furniture, try a chandelier for some added sparkle to your traditional space.

Photo: Houzz
