Dirty Martini Smokey green gold Eye makeup look

As the Monsoon gradually makes way for the Autumn season here's a simple eye makeup look inspired by Fall colors and Dirty Martini. 


Products Used
  • MAC Pigment Old Gold (Part of Ahoy Sailor Limited Edition Collection)
  • Sleek i Divine storm eyeshadow palette- Dark shimmery green shadow used on the outer corner and blended in to Old Gold pigmented and beige gold used as highlighter
  • Hint of  Glamour Doll eyeshadow -Dirty Martini eyeshadow used in the crease
  • Colorbar I define eye pencil Flirty Turq used on waterliner
  • Maybelline Colossal Kajal
  • Bourjois ultra curl mascara
  • New U lash glue and KKcenterHK false eyelashes 
 

Hope you like this look 'coz some like it *Shaken ,Not stirred*
