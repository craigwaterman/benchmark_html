Re: Retro Arcade List

by rugdoctor » Mon Apr 07, 2014 12:00 pm

http://www.laser-force.co.nz/arcade/ Laser Force on Taranaki St, Wellington has a couple there.

From memory, the outstanding examples are:

1. Multiplayer Scud Race, Daytona and maybe a Rush driver cabs.
2. Beatmania with a generic sony widescreen..
3. Dirtbike racer game (name escapes me for the time...)
4. T-Mek (but broken)
5. House of the Dead 2 in a sitdown cabinet
6. Time Crisis 3 (I think...)
7. 3 x Japanese candy cabs (Exeleena 1) with Prehistoric Is 2 & Puzzle Bobble, Metal Slug 5 & SNK vs Capcom and Street Fighter 2 Hyperfighting/Turbo.
8. Death Crimson (HOD2 clone)
9. Original Virtua On cabinet, play this one as its pretty well preserved. Highly recommended.
10. Original Simpsons 4 Players cabinet

Probably one of the last dedicated arcade in the CBD and with free parking! So drop in and experience 1/8th of what an arcade use to be like.

Some seedy ninja-shots of the cabs..















Last pic is not from Laser Force, but from eVent cinema in Westfield Lower Hutt...more on that place next time.
