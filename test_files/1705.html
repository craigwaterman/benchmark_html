About Louise Campbell-Anthony

I am Louise Campbell-Anthony, the publisher of OfftheKlock.com. I have a keen interest in working toward economic freedom through cooperative economics. I have developed an innovative approach to people being able to pursue their passions with unlimited financial resources at their disposal through what I have termed "income cooperatives." I am a stay-at-home mom of 10 children, married of 24 years to my husband, entrepreneur, student of personal development, and naive enough to believe in the possibility of ending the cycle of "brokeness" for people who can believe with me.
