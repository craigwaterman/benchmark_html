Event Details

Dear Grandmasters, Masters, Instructors, Competitors, Referees and Parents,

It is with Great honor that Grandmaster and I invite you to participate in the 13th Annual Sacramento Invitational Taekwondo Championship on Saturday, February 28th, 2015. This Championship will be held at the Sacramento Memorial Auditorium. It is one of the most beloved buildings in our region and is listed on the National Historic Register.

The Sacramento Invitational is committed to providing a level of competition for all ages and ranks from beginners to the national-level. Our Taekwondo tournament is for competitors who are seeking quality competition. Our tournament staff is committed to making this event as fair and enjoyable for all participants as possible.  We are continually refining various aspects of our tournament production to better the overall tournament experience.

This prestigious event is known for Electronic Scoring by True Score, Certified Black Belt Judges, and fully padded competition rings. Our competition is used by many athletes to prepare for national-level tournaments.

We look forward to seeing you February 28th, 2015 at the Sacramento Invitational Taekwondo Championship.

Sincerely,

Master Clinton S. Robinson

Tournament Director
