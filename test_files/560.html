    Alcohol Detoxification

    Ready to get clean and sober? Alcohol withdrawal can be dangerous and difficult if you have a serious dependency. Medically-assisted detoxification using Acamprostate (Campral®), Naltrexone (ReVia® and Vivitrol®), and Disulfram (Antabuse®) can make it safer and easier for you to detoxify, and increase your chance of success.

    Opiate Detox and Withdrawal

    Opiates and Opioids are among the most difficult substances to withdraw from, which is why Methadone, Buprenorphine (Suboxone® and Subutex®) and Naltrexone (Vivitrol®) are becoming increasingly popular detoxification options.
