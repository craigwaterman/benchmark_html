Keep Me Going Cereal Review

Twitter  \  Instagram  \  Bloglovin
Keep me going is a healthy breakfast cereal which is perfect for diabetics. Those wanting to reduce their sugar intake and those who don't eat wheat.
Keep me going is
wheat free. Low GI, wholegrain and high in fibre.

I wasn't sure whether I'd enjoy the taste, as I have a sweet tooth and it's so low in sugar - Lower than a lot of other cereals on the market. However I was pleasantly surprised as it tastes great!

I prefer it with cold milk, which is surprising also, as I normally need to soften high fibre cereals with hot milk. I find that these tasty little parcels however, have just the right amount of crunch as they are. This cereal has become a firm favourite with my son.

Keep Me Going contains a lot of our essential daily vitamins and even contain the minerals magnesium and chromium - Two important minerals, which are not easily sourced from foods. Chromium helps to stabilise blood sugar levels and reduce sugar cravings.

A 30g serving of Keep Me Going contains just 1% fat and only 3% of our daily sugar intake!

Visit www.keepmegoing.net for more info.
