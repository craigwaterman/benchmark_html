Website Design Trends 2015

People have started predicting about the website design trends of 2015 like what kind of new and useful features will be available in future for website designing part. It is a commonly known fact that updating website with latest and impressive web designs is a good idea to attract more number of people and binding them throughout the various pages. Internet users more often expect for the responsiveness and user friendly features of websites. If you have not updated your website since last few years then you are missing a golden opportunity of attracting people with designs and graphics. Just read out the blog and get 8 cutting edge ideas for creating a unique and appealing look for your website.

9 Modern Website Design Trends for 2015

1. Responsive Website Design
The number of mobile and tablet users is successively increasing day by day and resulting in lesser number of desktop users. It only means that people are accessing the web via multiple number of devices of different screen resolutions. For this, every device should be able to get the resolution compatible view of websites to make it easy for viewers access the site. In this situation, the concept of responsiveness comes to mind. Responsive website design is the trick that sets its layouts according to device width and height. So provides the user-friendly website design to its viewers. Responsive designs are the first requirement to be considered while designing any website
2. Sliders
Sliders are the great and ideal way to show big content, photos, videos or some other stories. Mostly auto scroll feature is used for sliders that allow users to pause while hovering mouse over them and catching visitors’ attention with sliding features.
3. Parallax Scrolling
Parallax scrolling is the technique in which foreground images move faster than background images. You can get the “wow” word quite easily from people by using this neat technique. This is perfect for telling long stories on single page. These all are creative web design techniques following worldwide.
4. Slide-Out Menus
Sometimes, navigation bars seem like cluttered and so web design is growing upto advanced level and in 2015, web design will gravitate towards simplicity and friendliness. Slide-out menus are the extension of such type of designs and these are specially designed for mobile web platforms to avoid the space issues.
5. Fonts with Personality
Gone are the days when sites were using boring font styles like Arial, Times New Roman etc. Today, quirky fonts are quite in demand that adds a decent personality and style into a website design. You will be happy to know that these attractive fonts don’t affect the site loading time and mostly browsers support such type of stylish fonts.
6. Flat Design
Now, you can say goodbye to beveled edges, drop shadow like designs as Apple iOS7 brought us the glimpse of “Flat Design”. Bright colors are used more often for flat designs to make the layout simple. These types of designs will be very popular in 2015.
7. Videos in replacement of Text
Using short and descriptive animated videos can drive more traffic on a website that throws the whole information in the form of video slides. According to recent surveys, approx 90% of people get more interested in video information instead of textual one. Using videos with some creative ideas can really bind the visitors to spend more time on website.
8. Hand Drawn Illustrations
Hand illustrated images and icons add creativity and uniqueness to your website. As like common photos, illustrations are becoming demanding in 2014 and 2015. You will not believe but a number of vintage styles are coming back in vogue.
9. Cards
You might have noticed that information is often arranged in small business cards. Popular websites like Google, Twitter, Pinterest and Spotify are also using the web design trend of cards to share the information in a digestible way.
