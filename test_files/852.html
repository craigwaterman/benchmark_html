Wish I didn’t…

Posted on July 3, 2015 by mbabij

0

Woke up on Sunday and couldn’t shake you. Tried to get my footing and couldn’t find the ground. 

Seeking comfort in strange arms, Felt further from myself.

I made it worse. Exposed the wound more. I shouldn’t have closed that open door behind us. It was a mistake, it was awful and cheap. Too swift to count, never want to repeat. 

What was I thinking, who had I become? This wasn’t me, it was furthest from myself I’ve ever felt. 

Get up on Monday and try to shake it all. Focus on work don’t drop the ball. I really wish it wasn’t a choice that I made but take it for what it was, one big mistake.

Follow Me…

Posted on June 15, 2015 by mbabij

0

Follow me into my heart. Follow the beautiful music, the vibrant colors…into the whirlwind of my love. 

Let’s go together, on this life adventure.
Where the wind blows hard and the sky is ever-changing. 

Let’s fall into it together.  Open your heart to me. 

Let me follow you into your heart.

Where you have a place just for me and you carry me wherever you go. 

Let our souls hold hands and our bodies intertwine. Let us get lost in each other and find ourselves better each day. Not just better for eachother but better for ourselves.

Come Find Me

Posted on June 15, 2015 by mbabij

0

Love, please come blind side me.On the right date at the right time

Come throw me off my path 
Make all of these poor decisions 

And incredible nights with friends

And mixing and mingling be what makes you jump out and get me. 
Please find me, find him, push us together with the force that neither of us can fight. 
Make it love that is forever and adoration that never leaves. Give us graceful words and kind hearts. Adventurous souls and meaningful connections. 
Give us what forever feels like. Give us time. Give us light. Give us each other. 
Love please come grab me and throw me into us, we, our. Make it mutual and make it awestriking and most of all…make it true, unconditional love.

I’m looking out for you, but who is looking out for me?

Posted on October 21, 2014 by mbabij

0

Take care of those in your life. Look out for the people that come into it, be their presence permanent or fleeting.

I wish someone would look out for me, because it seems in every matter of the heart, new relationship, long term love, I find myself wondering, “if I’m looking out for them and they’re looking out for them, who is looking out for me?”

This could be a dissertation on looking out for #1 and caring for yourself because no one will if you don’t…and that very well may be…but maybe, just maybe, we could start looking out for each other a little more and everyone will feel a little less alone and a little more invested in each other.

Here’s to hoping.

“I don’t want to get to the end of my life and find that I lived just the length of it. I want to have lived the width of it as well.” – Diane Ackerman

—  *  —  —  *  —

Go out and live, make memories, share food, take pictures, hike along trails, swim in lakes, dive through waves, ski down mountains.  Live with intention and don’t be afraid, in fact, channel that fear and go after doing things that scare you.  Break down walls and keep growing, expanding, and creating.  

Be intentional.  Be kind.  Be adventurous.
