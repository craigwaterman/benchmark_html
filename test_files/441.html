The Samurai Way : Don’t Dress To Impress

Posted in Lifestyle

The Samurai Way Part 1: Don’t Dress to Impress

 

Today I wanted to start a new series of articles, that will be called “The Samurai Way”.

The purpose is to learn how to live simple, cheap, without all the unnecessary things polluting our lives.

I choosed to get inspired by Samurai, as they were noble warriors, with honor and values and they always followed a strict moral code.

I think today as entrepreneurs, we are the Modern days Samurai, so we can all benefit from their lifestyle and codes.

 

Samurai at their time use to be fashion icons, the way they dressed was influencing the fashion.

Their clothes were not chosen because of the price tag, or designer names, but simply because it will fit their needs.

Remember, they were great warriors, so they needed quality clothes that will give them the ability to move easily in case they need to fight, but also easy to are travel with, light, so they can keep their movements speed etc.

Even though it was a long time ago, we can still learn from those great warriors today.

A Fashion victim, will dress to impress, or at least try to, by purchasing, the most famous designer brands of the moment, or the most expensive outfits, and that way think he/she is special.

It doesn’t matter if it is useful or comfortable, as long as it’s FASHION.

The Samurai way is different.

A samurai also want to look good, but not at any cost or by sacrificing important aspects.

Next time before you buy any clothes, ask yourself those 2 questions:

1. Does it fit my needs?

2. Will it be useful?

By doing so you will realise that you can find quality, simple clothes that will serve you better and at the right price.

The money you will save following The Samurai Way simple life hacks will help you to reach your goals faster.
