How did we pick the winner for PR Giveaway 2014?

January 15th, 2015

We’ll be announcing the winner for 2014’s PR Giveaway very soon. It was picked around New Year, but we had to settle some details first. We’ve contacted the winner now and we’re ready to finally give the prize very soon.

But before that, a lot of you are probably wondering how we picked the winner for this contest. Well, as announced in the contest page, Chris picked the winner based on the quality of the entry.

First, I compiled all entries and numbered it. I then emailed them to Chris with their corresponding numbers/labels (entry #1, #2, etc.) and omitted the names of the entrants. This is done so that Chris can simply judge on the entries. After picking the winning entry, we then finalized all details and processes for the winner to enjoy their one-month PR subscription very soon.
