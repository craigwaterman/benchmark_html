Dress the Holidays with fine Holiday Decor

by Kim Kaczor-Wardlow, Director of Visual Merchandising & Bristol Kitchens

Last week, we moved into a new home in an adorable little neighborhood. Ever since, we’ve been enjoying evening bike rides (you can thank the warm weather for that), and checking out all the fun holiday house decorations for Halloween and Fall. It’s great to see the effort people put into decorating their homes! Though we just moved and most of our stuff still sits in boxes, I made it a point to locate my existing decorations, and pick up a few new fun items too. Needless to say, our house is decorated and we are looking forward to all the trick-or-treaters and holiday entertaining this year.

So, where was my go-to place for Halloween decorations? Well, you may be surprised that I found many of my festive items at Bristol Farms! You can find wonderful Halloween & holiday décor (and unique treats) all at Bristol Farms. It’s just too difficult to pick a favorite, so I’ll tell you about a few I picked up:

If you’re looking for items to decorate a great tablescape for the holidays, check out the holiday décor by K&K and Mudpie. Beautifully crafted white pumpkins trimmed in gold, and burlap pumpkins add so much warmth to a festive fall table.

Kurt Adler brings us incredibly detailed fall ornaments! The sugar skulls and Day of the Dead bride and groom are such fun décor to decorate the house with.

I love the LED candles from Candle Impressions! Light up your windows or decorative shelf with these great candles and never worry about blowing out the candle or the cat knocking it over! These are both safe and fun. They come in fun designs for Halloween, such as skulls and Jack-o-Lanterns.

The pumpkin figs, owls, and witches are absolutely stunning from Gallerie II. The witch owl is sitting on my hutch!

Two’s Company adds great fun to our Halloween assortment with the wonderful melting pumpkins and skeletons. These are so much fun to give as a gift and keep the kids occupied! you build them up, and they melt before your very eyes. You can use it over and over again.

Of course, Design Design rounds it all out with everything you would need for a party from bright colorful paper tableware, holiday gift bags, and the very best greeting cards.

We encourage you to visit our Kitchens department to browse a great assortment of festive holiday decorations. Enjoy!
