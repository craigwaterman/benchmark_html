Business World Magazine

#MediaMoves

May 5, 2015 GoSkribe Movers & Shakers Business World Magazine, Femina

Business Journalist Ashish Sinha joins Business World Magazine, New Delhi as Senior Editor covering Corporate Affairs, Economy, and Media & Entertainment and has previously contributed to The Financial Express and Business Standard. He can be reached at @sinha_ashish130. Nairita Mukherjee  joins lifestyle publication Femina as Features Writer. Prior to this, she was with Society Mandate Magazine – Men’s Lifestyle Magazine. Mumbai based Nairita can be reached at @Noir_Memoir.
