The Ultimate Feel Good Song

In Music by Kaela LuckApril 13, 2015Leave a Comment

Love Me Like You Do by Ellie Goulding is my absolute favorite song right now! If it is on the radio when I get in the car in the morning, I know it is going to be a good day. There is something about this song that just makes me want to smile, fall in love, go on a run, and of course dance. I am a huge fan of Ms. Goulding and when I went to go check out the 50 Shades of Grey soundtrack and heard this song it was love at first listen.

There are few songs that I love the first time I hear it, sometimes I have to listen to a song 2 or 3 times before I realize it is my jam; so when I love a song 30 seconds in, I know it is special. When this song comes on I will stop conversations to turn up the volume. I feel like I even want this song to play for my husband and mine’s first dance. It is crazy how just a song can make you feel so happy. I assume everybody has heard this song by now because it is all over the radio, but if you haven’t heard it yet, trust me you need to go on iTunes right now and buy it. Watch the video below.
