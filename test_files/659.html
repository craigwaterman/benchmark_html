Event Details

Home Buying Class

Thinking about buying a home but don't know where to start? Redfin Agents Lindsay Weingart and Holland Cohen will walk you through the home-buying process so you know what to expect.

What we'll cover:

  • Is buying for you?
  • Tacoma home prices, inventory & market conditions
  • Special property types (short sales, foreclosures, new construction)
  • Home financing & the purchase process

 

Free bites & beverages will be provided!

 

Hosted by Redfin Agents:
Lindsay Weingart and Holland Cohen

Lindsay Weingart and Holland Cohen are expert agents on the Tacoma team. Want to meet them before the class or just have a quick question? Feel free to give them a call at (253) 204-3130 or email them at tacoma@redfin.com. They're happy to answer all of your home buying and selling questions.

Can't make it? Click here for a full list of free Redfin classes and events!

Free and Never any Obligation

Attending this class doesn't obligate you to work with a Redfin Agent but it does mean you agree to Redfin Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and End User License Agreement. We will also create a Redfin account for you.
