From Pinterest To Your Wardrobe

I



I am absolutely loving Pinterest. I will totally admit that I have only recently discovered it's brilliance and created an account a few months ago. It's safe to say that I love it, (why did it take me so long?)It is the perfect platform for gathering and sharing images. My favorite pin searches are for fashion and wedding inspiration. 

For this post I have selected three fab looks and recreated a version of them. You can purchase online or from the highstreet. The looks are bang on trend for AW. Featuring ponchos, fringing, pleather, over the knee boots and duster coats. You can take all of the pieces together or just one or two depending on your own personal style and taste. 

These looks can be casual or dressy. Using some tweaks, the more casual can take you through to the night. My top tips - Look :1 Switch the ankle boots for heels and the poncho for a blazer. Look 2: As shown in the image with the grey dress. Change the boots for strappy heels (not crazy on the ones in the photo) just a plain pair would be fab. Switch the bag for a clutch  Look 3: change the casual t-shirt to a dressy top like the one featured. For a casual look wear boots instead of heels.
