Tropicana: New Tieks Flats and Escada Bag OOTD

Casual, Fashion

I thought it would be fun to post an outfit featuring my new bright handbag from Escada, as hauled on my channel here. Serendipitously, it matches an old sweater from Anthropologie, so I decided to go for a breezy matchy matchy look.

I’m so happy with the quality and colour of this handbag. I would highly recommend Escada bags for any of you who care about quality above brand recognition for bags. My sunglasses Dior Summerville tortoiseshell, and my shorts are from Dynamite.

As for my rose gold Tieks flats, worn above, those are also a recent purchase. They are foldable, and buttery comfortable from the first wear. I love that they come with a cute pouch I can toss in a suitcase or handbag. I think the rose gold pairs nicely with their trademark teal soles. The rose gold is a little more pink than I anticipated in person, so perhaps in hindsight I would have gotten the copper. However, I think the quality is just as nice as the Tory Burch Reva flats I’ve always otherwise been loyal too, and they come in a rainbow of fun colours which I like.

Sunset colours like orange are definitively my thing right now, and I love wearing brights for spring and summer, both in and out of the office!
