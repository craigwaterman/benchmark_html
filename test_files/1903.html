10 Things You Might Not Know About Me

Saturday, 1 August 2015

This photo is not mine


I thought it would be fun and interesting to share 10 things you might not know
about me, with you to either get to know me better if you've been reading my blog for a long
time or if this is the first post you've read of mine and don't know anything about me.


1. It has been a dream of mine for as long as I can remember to write a book, and
I am thinking of writing one but I don't know what to write about!

2. When I grow there are so many different jobs I want to do, such as; social work,
fashion journalism, anything in beauty or work in a foster home.

3. When I go to college in September I will be studying health and social care

4. I am such an organised person. I write everything I do in my planner and write down all
of my blog post ideas and when I'm going to post them etc.

5. If I had a superpower it would either be to time travel because I could go all over the 
world in different eras of time, or to be able to fill things because I could have 
constant full drinks and products and everything and would save so much money!

6. I have had about 3 youtube channels in the past and I used to upload videos,
but stopped as I didn't want people to find out, but I am thinking of starting posting
videos again soon!

7. Anything makeup, beauty and fashion related I love to do, talk or write about. It 
has helped me to escape from everything else going on in my personal life 
and gives me a chance to do what I love.

8. Everything I know about beauty and makeup is all self taught, and I've learnt
from youtube or from reading books.

9. I love everything and anything to do with baking. My favourite things to bake are;
brownies, cookies and a classic sponge cake. 

10. I am so thankful for everything anybody does for me, whether it be somebody 
buying me an ice cream or somebody doing something really 
special and sentimental. 

xoxo
Lea-Mai
