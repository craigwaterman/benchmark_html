Healthy Split Pea Soup Recipe

By wrapwithberdel on

Split peas is easy soup to prepare because you don’t have to soak them before cooking. Whether you choose the yellow or green split peas they both are filled with a lot nutrition.

Split peas is low in calories and easy for your stomach to digest. Research has shown that one-half cup of split peas has 20 grams of carbohydrates and about 10 percent of your recommended daily allowance of iron, magnesium, potassium, zinc ad folate.

Most people are looking for a better ways to get more wholesome food into their diet. Here is recipe that will help you lose weight and have more energy. This is a delicious soup recipe just add a green salad and brown rice for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts water
  • 2 cups green split peas
  • 1 stalk celery, coarsely chopped
  • 1 large carrot, coarsely chopped
  • 1 small onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Himalayan Pink Salt
  • 3 cloves of fresh garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Preparation

  1. Boil for 20 minutes; add vegetables and bay leaf, then simmer until peas are tender; remove bay leaf.
  2. This will make about 8-10 servings.
