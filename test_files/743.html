Event Details

REGISTER NOW

The Jailbreak Brevard 2014 Adventure Race is HERE! Located on the grounds of the Brevard County Jail Complex in Cocoa, Fl., this off the grid adventure race is like no other.  Covering approx. 3 miles of muddy, wet, fun filled obstacles all while in hot pursuit by Brevard's finest.

Watch out for the bloodhounds and SWAT team.  You don't want to get detained in the holding cell.

 

Important Information

Teams: If you would like to create a team and are the first to create that team, click the "Create Team" button first and register you and your team members. If someone wants to add to an existing team and the team name has been created, please click "Join A Team" and search for the team name that you want to join to add to that specific team. 

Discounts available for All Government Employees and Students with discount code.  Please contact jailbreakbrevard@bcso.us via your agency email or see your school administrator. Only one discount code can be applied per order.

Runners must be at least 12 yrs of age.  Anyone ages 12-17 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian throughout the race. Minors must have waivers printed and signed by parents.  See attached Waiver

While the course may be extreme; all fitness levels are welcome.

For questions, contact Cheryl Sink at 321-264-5201 ext 1048 or email: jailbreakbrevard@bcso.us 

This event will take place rain or shine and no refunds will be issued.  Event details are subject to change without notice.

Items to Bring

Loose fitting clothing that you don't mind getting torn or stained.  Bug spray, a towel and sunscreen are also a good idea.

 

By participating in this challenge, you'll unlock a true sense of accomplishment and have a great time while supporting United Way of Brevard, the Children's Advocacy Center and local Animal Shelters.
Have questions about JailBreak Brevard 2014? Contact Brevard County Sheriff's Office
