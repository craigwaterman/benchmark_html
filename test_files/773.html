What are essential oils?

Essential oils are the extracts taken from natural sources like stems, leaves, flowers, barks seeds or even whole plants as fragrant oils equipped with all the positive energies a plant has in itself. Now these essential oils or you can say magical fragrances can be used for the well being and treatment or even as a supplement to other therapeutic processes as catalysts to improve general health and cure.

It has been proven that essential oils can work as a miracle alternative medicine to treat skin diseases, nerve problems and many other diseases that have resulted as a reaction to stress or constant strain on muscles. In order to use these essential oils it better to take them in the form of fumes spread into the surroundings. For this purpose there are certain kits available in the market that are used to create fumes by diffusing essential oils into the air. These kits are known as essential oil diffusing machines and are available in different types depending upon the way they act on the oils to produce fumes.

Type of essential oil diffusers

There are a number of different types of brands and styles available for these diffusers, but mainly they are of following types:

  • Ultrasonic diffusers for essential oils
  • Evaporative
  • Nebulizer kits
  • Diffusing through heat
  • Reed oil diffusing kits
  • Candle based diffusers

Though all these types are meant to produce fumes of essential oils that needs to be used still the way fumes are produced effects the nature of the fumes, thus produced as well it differs in the way the oil will have an effect on you. Now we shall discuss the types mentioned here one by one.

The ultrasonic diffusers for essential oils

These machines use ultrasonic energies to diffuse the essential oil that is being put into it. These waves cause the oils to evaporate as mist into the air. Though it has been proven that these kits provide a good source of aromatic effects for people using aroma therapy still these kits may lower the health benefits of essential oils by breaking them down while diffusing.

Evaporative

Such types of kits consist of pads on which oil rests and then get evaporated into the air due to heating. These are best for diffusing oils when you only need to have a good source of light fragrance in your room. The fumes, thus produced may only be useful to make your room fragrant and it may not have the exact health benefits of any essential oil in it as the heat may reduce the effectiveness. Another problem with such diffusers is that

Nebulizer

Nebulizer diffusing machines are considered to be the most effective diffusing kits for these oils, as they create micro droplets of oils in the air that are then sprayed without getting any chemical change. These droplets lasts longer than other fumes produced via ultrasonic or heat based diffusing machines and contains oils in its natural form and has maximum health benefits you can get from any miracle oil.

Heat diffusers

These are simple in their function. They simply heat the oil that is being placed in it and the vapors thus produced are spread around the place. Such machines are good to produce sufficient fragrance in the room, but still it affects the chemical composition through heating of oil and may not be able to give you the exact health benefits you may need. But it would be best if you only need to have a calm environment with soft aroma around you.

Reed oil diffusers

Reed diffusing kits as its name indicate have reeds to produce fumes in the air. The reeds are placed into the oil that is in the kit and the oil then flow to the top of the reed and then released into the air. This process is a bit slow and may not be able to produce lots of aroma quickly around the room.

Candle oil diffusers

Candle oil diffusing kits are stylish and often used as a decorative, aroma kit in rooms and spas to create a soothing environment. The oil is placed under the candle and the candle slowly burns the to release the oil into the air. But the aroma thus produced is limited to a small portion where the kits is being placed and may not be able to diffuse the aroma in a large area.

If you are pondering upon a question that, why should you buy or have an essential oil diffuser at home? Then you should be feeling very confused. If you have been suggested to go through an aromatherapy as a part of your medical treatment then you should be taking it seriously. Or if you are going through an aromatherapy session and need regular aromatic fumes around you then you should also consider having a kit that will cater to your needs effectively. You can buy and these aroma aromatherapy diffuser for the following reasons:

  • Need a soothing and fresh environment at home
  • You need to relax by using natural aromatic oils
  • You are suffering from stress and need to undergo aromatherapy regularly
  • You want to cleanse your surroundings from infectious bacteria and viruses

How to choose the right essential oil diffusing machine for you?

Now the most important thing that you should be considering while buying a diffusing machine is to know which one is right for your use. You can decide it by considering following important things that should be kept in mind while making a decision:

  • For what purpose you need to buy an essential oil diffusing machine
  • How much area you need to cover
  • Which oil you want to use or are prescribed to use
  • What results you need

It’s important to decide about these points before you choose any product because it would help you to choose the exact machine that would bring the best results for you. You may get an idea here: Ultrasonic diffusing machines- are best for to get health benefits and a consistent and equal distribution of aroma around a large area. Nebulizer diffusing machines- are best for long lasting fragrance and maximum health benefits when used for your desired essential oil Vaporizing diffusing machines- useful for small places and light fragrances with slow effects on mood Reeds and candles- are best to be used around a table or a very small area where you relax or meditate.

How to use the different types of the essential oil diffusers correctly?

Having a diffuser for your aroma oil is not just all what you need. Rather using it correctly is the most important factor to help you get your desired effects. In order to use it correctly you should know about the functions and features of the kit you have got as well as the required results you need. As, for example, if you need to make a large room fragrant and filled with your favorite smell then you should have a nebulizer diffusing machine in the middle of the room. While in contrast to it if you need a small area to be fragrant and filled with the essential oil effects then you can use a vaporizing kit or a candle oil diffusion kit. Some basic points to follow:

  • Always follow the guidelines given along the product to use it correctly
  • Always place an appropriate amount of oil in the diffusing machine
  • Place it in a correct position to help you get the desired spread of the aroma
  • Never use it for prolonged periods
  • Never leave it turned on when its empty or don’t have sufficient oil in it

It also depends on the effects you need. If you need to get all the health benefits in any oil you have been suggested or chosen, then you should be going to use a nebulizer or an ultrasonic oil diffusion kit.

Things you should avoid while using an aroma oil diffusing machine

Though, the health benefits of using these oils are great and proven to be effective in alleviating side effects of medications, symptoms of mood disorder, stress and pain still using them excessively or in a wrong quantity may be harmful in many ways. Never let the diffusing machine remain on for long periods of time or for hours or the whole day. It would cause you to experience “olfactory fatigue” due to the persistent exposure to the smell. You may feel dizzy or insensitive to certain smell. Never start using a machine regularly right from the start. Try to start in intervals like for one day use it for 15 minutes only then for the next day use it twice in a day for 15 minutes each session. And then set your time and frequency as prescribed by your aromatherapy practitioner.
