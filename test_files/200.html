Our fall weekends are jam packed with activities. From baseball to running to a photo shoot to going to a fun family activity, we seem to spend most of our weekend in the car. When you spend so much time in the car, that means you are going to grab something quick to eat.

Eating in the car can be a bit messy but I think I found something that fits the bill! Wendy’s has brought back their Grilled Chicken Flatbread sandwiches back for a limited time.

This was the first time that I tried the flatbreads and went with the Smoky Honey Mustard. At only 370 calories and with my favorite Honey Mustard dressing, I knew it would be perfect!

And it was!

The flatbread makes it portable and easy to eat when you are on the go! I will be definitely going back to try the Asiago Ranch. It has bacon, so you know it will be delicious! But I have to hurry. The flatbread sandwiches are only available for a limited time!

Would you like to win $6,000?

Who wouldn’t? 6 seconds could win you $6,000 in the Wendy’s #6SecondsFlat Flatbread Sweepstakes.

Tell Wendy’s what you think about their flatbreads in a Vine or Instagram video then send it @Wendys (through twitter, Instagram or Vine) with the #6SecondsFlat tag. Each week of September, one lucky winner will be randomly selected to receive a $6000 prize.

Here’s my #6SecondsFlat video!

Wendy’s is handpicking the best video entries to re-post from their official Wendy’s social media accounts to be featured on the contest website. Be sure to check out the sample videos on the 6 Seconds Flat landing page by clicking on any of the video squares.

Enter to win here: #6SecondsFlat.
